# A policeman near Itirapina-SP spotted  ‘6 groups of UFOs’ over Broá dam [1978]

*2020-07-15T19:11:19-03:00*

October 18, 1978

UFO sighting report according to a letter sent by the General Commander of the Military Police of São Paulo to the Commander of the Pirassununga Air Force Academy, about “foreign objects”. “[…] appearance of strange objects that flew near “Praia do Broá”, near  Km 214 / 300 of SP-310 highway (Washington Luiz).

A sergeant from the M.P. in the city of Piracicaba-SP, on October 18, 1978, at 8:00 pm, reported having seen, “in the vicinity of the dam named “Broa”, 4 (four) groups of illuminated and strange objects, followed by another 2 (two), in landing operations “. According to the reporting witness, other people observed the ‘spaceships’. The sighting location was Km 214 of the SP-310 highway.
