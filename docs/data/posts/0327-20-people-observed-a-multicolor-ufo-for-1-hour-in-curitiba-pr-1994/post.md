# 20 people observed a multicolor UFO for 1 hour in  Curitiba-PR [1994]

*2020-07-04T16:44:14-03:00*

JAN 15th, 1994. Time: 20:05. Duration: 1 hour. In Curitiba, Paraná State, about 20 people spotted an unidentified flying object moving south. According to the witness that reported the sighting, the UFO was red and sometimes changed to yellow. The same witness was unable to estimate the size and shape of the object that emitted the lights. It was informed that there was filming of the sighting, but the video file was not made available.
