MAR 3rd, 2012. In downtown Alegrete, a city in the state of Rio Grande do Sul, on a clear night, a witness observed twelve (12) unidentified flying objects with a round shape and orange color. The UFOs resembled small stars when viewed with the naked eye and  were approximately 200m apart from each other. For about two minutes, the witness observed the deslocation of the twelve UFOs to the Northeast direction, at a presumed altitude of 7km. The witness also reported that “there were 12 objects separated into 2 groups. The first with 6 or 7, and the second with the other objects”, and also that “the objects resembled small stars with luminous halos, with 2 or 3 of them pulsing quickly and becoming white. “
