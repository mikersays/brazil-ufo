# A big rectangular UFO flying slowly at low altitude in Parobé [2002]

*2020-06-11T17:41:05-03:00*

SEPT 1st, 2002. 21h00am. Duration: 10 min. In the city of Parobé, State of Rio Grande do Sul, two witnesses spotted an unidentified rectangular flying object about 40 meters in size, flying at low speed and with little altitude without leaving any trails or displacement trails. The sighting of the two UFOs was carried out under conditions of visibility described as “good” from the center of the city.
