JUNE 15th, 1999. Time: 8:10 pm. Duration: 1h10m. During the flight TAM 825 from SBGO [Santa Genoveva Airport, Goiânia] to SBCG [Campo Grande Airport], seven members of the flight crew observed, for an hour and ten minutes, an unidentified flying object in the form of a very bright light. strong and white, “like 8 Boeing headlights”. According to the witnesses who reported the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the sky was “100% clear” and it was possible to see the UFO at the same altitude as flight TAM 825, 35,000ft, following the aircraft at a higher speed in the “2 o’clock” position in relation to the airliner.

Following are the sighting observations made by one of the crew witnesses on board:

“After the aircraft took off from SBGO, when crossing 10,000ft, an object with intense light was spotted by the entire crew. The ACC BR [Area Control Center-Brasília] and ACC CW [Area Control Center-Curitiba] were informed by the radio but they reported that there was no traffic and contact.

The aircraft climbed and stabilized at FL 350. During the whole [..] during about 01:00 hours of flight, the object was observed by the crew.

At 132 NM [nautical miles] from SBCG the object slowed down and began to change color to blue, red and white until it disappeared from view.

Obs. The light was intense but pulsating.“
