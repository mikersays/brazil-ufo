MARCH 24th, 1993. 06:00 am. Duration: 10 minutes. A woman from Curitiba, who “already observed UFOs in 1987”, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object moving vertically “from bottom to top” at high altitude in the western region of the city. According to her description, the UFO was round, with a golden and shiny color, it moved at “sharp” speed and left a “luminous trail”. As for its size, the UFO was “inferior to the Sun”, from the perspective of those who observe it in the sky. The weather was partly cloudy when the sighting occurred with the naked eye.
