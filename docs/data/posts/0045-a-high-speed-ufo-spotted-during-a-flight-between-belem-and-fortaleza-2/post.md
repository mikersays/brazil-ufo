# A high speed UFO spotted during a flight between Belém and Fortaleza [2010]

*2020-06-05T23:35:15-03:00*

SEPT 23rd, 2010. Time: 5:30 am. Duration: 01 minute. During the flight GLO 1201, in the stretch between the city of Belém, State of Pará, and the city of Fortaleza, State of Ceará, a pilot and two other witnesses spotted an unidentified flying object with a strong light, no defined shape and “larger than the apparent size of Venus” when seen in the sky. The UFO was traveling at a speed “close to that of a jet”, at an altitude of approximately 10,000ft above the GLO 1201 aircraft. According to the pilot’s information, the visibility conditions were good, “open sky “, and the UFO was going the Southwest/Northeast direction, and left no tracks, or trails while deslocating.
