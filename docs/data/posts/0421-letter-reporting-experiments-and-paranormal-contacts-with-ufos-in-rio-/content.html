SEPT 19th, 1985. In a letter addressed to the Ministry of Aeronautics, from the city of São José do Rio Preto, State of São Paulo, a man writes to report “paranormal contacts with unidentified flying objects” during and after a seminar taken place in Rio Branco-AC.

Following is the translation of the letter:
São José do Rio Preto, September 19, 1985.
Your Excellency Minister of Aeronautics
We are pleased to contact you, to discuss a matter concerning an operation carried out by SNI and Aeronautics, during the first ADESG seminar held in November 1981 in the city of Rio Branco in the state of Acre.
In November 1981, we as employees of the Federal University of Acre were invited by ADESG to make the recordings of the seminar. During the first half of November when the seminar was taking place, SNI carried out a ‘conditioning’ with us, it was an ‘Operation of a confidential nature’ on the streets of Rio Branco.
This conditioning led us to come into contact with the UFO Phenomenon, that is, we witnessed an Unidentified Flying Object and thus it aroused in us the paranormal through a type of telepathic contact that started to happen through the provocation by the UFOs of successive discharges of electricity in the cloudless atmosphere, at night.
Air Force officers and SNI agents participated in the Operation; I don’t know how they found out that, in me, it would trigger such paranormal conditions.
We were taken over by the phenomenon empire until very recently; we have already undergone psychiatric and psychotherapeutic treatment and have been able to withstand these paranormal contacts through neuroleptic drugs.
Today, after three years of contact with the phenomenon, we have learned to live with the unknown; we no longer need medication because the paranormal has become bearable, but contacts through electrical discharges continue to answer my questions.
We have communicated EMFA and now we are communicating the Air Force that led us to the phenomenon; we are aware of the existence of the SIOANI created confidentially by the FAB, which becomes the SYSTEM OF INVESTIGATION OF UNIDENTIFIED AIR OBJECTS.
Since 1982 we have dedicated ourselves to research and now we request the possibility to research the phenomenon together with SIOANI in an official way. Would this be possible, since I am officially connected to the phenomenon?
Without further ado, we are grateful for your attention and look forward to communicating with you.
Kind regards,
EJG / São José do Rio Preto-SP.
