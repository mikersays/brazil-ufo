MARCH 20th, 1997. Time: 19h40. Duration: 20 minutes. From the window of an apartment in Taguatinga Norte, Distrito Federal, seven people observed several unidentified flying objects, round in shape and white in color, hovering together. According to the witnesses, the UFOs remained stationary but rotating fast on their own axes during the sighting. The objects were approximately “5 meters in diameter”. Also according to the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witnesses stated: “The objects revolve around their own axis and illuminate an apartment building at CNB [Comercial Norte B]”. The sighting was made with the naked eye and under a cloudy sky with rain.
