FEB 19th, 1993. Time: 20:00. Duration: 30 minutes. In Pinheirinho, Curitiba, State of Paraná, five people observed a small, round, white and yellow UFO between the South and the East regions of the city. According to the witnesses, the unidentified flying object moved at a slow speed in a straight path. The sighting occurred in a cloudy weather and with the naked eye.
