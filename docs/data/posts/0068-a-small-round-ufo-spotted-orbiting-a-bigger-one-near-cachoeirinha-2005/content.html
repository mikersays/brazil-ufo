JAN 26th, 2005. Time: 10:30 am. Duration: 5 minutes. In Cachoeirinha, a city in the state of Rio Grande do Sul, a witness spotted two unidentified flying objects under clear skies and favorable visibility. According to the witness, the two UFOs were round and bright, and looked like the planet Venus. The objects also moved in a peculiar way: the “smaller object, grayish in color, [was] more opaque, with circular movements, following the movement of the larger one, orbiting it.”
