August 12, 1986. Time: 6:45 pm. The ACC-BS [Area Control Center of Brasília-DF] received a call from a military man reporting that “04 aircrafts were seeing lights in very high positions”. According to the report, the aircraft that observed the unidentified flying objects were the SC 881, PT KPB, TF-536 and SC 144. All in different locations, in the Southeast region of Brazil.

The pilots of the SC 114 and TF 536 aircraft reported their sightings.

Duration: 5 minutes. The SC 114 pilot reported that he first observed the unidentified flying objects at 18:50, in the “3-hour position of the aircraft and very far away”. First, the witness “believed” it was a “reflection of sunlight”, but the object changed “from white to opaque”. The UFO, still according to the pilot’s report, carried out a “trajectory in the vertical direction”.

Duration: 3 minutes. The TF 536 pilot reported that he first observed two UFOs at 18:40, with one white UFO “at 12 o’clock, 30° above the horizon, and the second [UFO] to the right of the aircraft. According to the pilot, the UFOs were “like a star, increasing in size, forming a halo (as if burning); [also] there was sunlight illuminating the halo.” In the pilot’s description, the second UFO only left a small trail of displacement and both UFOs performed horizontal trajectories.
