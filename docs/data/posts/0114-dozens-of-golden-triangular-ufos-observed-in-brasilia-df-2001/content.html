FEB 10th, 2001. Time: 11:17 pm. Duration: 2 minutes. Two people residing in Lago Sul, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, spotted dozens of golden triangular UFOs at an altitude of 12,000ft, making zigzag movements and sometimes going off unexpectedly. According to the witnesses’ account, the UFOs in the firmament appeared to be “bigger than star” in size. The UFO was observed with the naked eye under good visibility conditions, and no condensation trails were observed.
