SEPT 6th, 2001. Time: 19h14pm. Duration: 2 minutes. In the city of Gioânia, State of Goiás, two witnesses spotted an unidentified flying object, the “size of 2 stars” in the firmament, with blue color and at high speed. The UFO appeared to be at an altitude of 30km and moving from East to West. According to the witnesses’ report, the sighting took place under favorable visibility conditions. The COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report provides even more details given by the witness: “He cannot identify the shape because it looked like a car headlight in his direction (without a defined shape). Altitude taken as a reference [is] the flight altitude of aircrafts he usually observes (Approximately 3 times the flight altitude of a Boeing).
