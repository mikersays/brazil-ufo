# A large round yellow UFO was spotted in Porto Alegre-RS [1996]

*2020-06-29T14:35:25-03:00*

MARCH 18th, 1996. Time: 17h50. Duration: 2 minutes. In the north of the Guaíba River, in Porto Alegre area, State of Rio Grande do Sul, on a cloudless afternoon, a person observed round and yellow UFO, with the size “close to the size we see the Moon”. According to the witness, the unidentified flying object remained “stopped” most of the time, but then it moved in the North-South direction, at an altitude estimated by the witness as “very high”. The stationary UFO was filmed by the observer, but the file with the video was not made available in this file case.
