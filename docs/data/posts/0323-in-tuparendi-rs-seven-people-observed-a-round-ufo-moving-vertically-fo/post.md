# In Tuparendi-RS, seven people observed a round UFO moving vertically for 2 hours [1994]

*2020-07-04T11:32:53-03:00*

MARCH 20th, 1994. 19:30. Duration: 2 hours. Seven people in the city of Tuparendi, State of Rio Grande do Sul, spotted an unidentified flying object, small in size, round in shape, and with colors ranging from lilac, yellow to red. The UFO, based on the report, was at a high altitude and was flying at slow speed without leaving trails in the western position of the city. According to the witness, they watched the sighting from her home along with six others with the naked eye at night with a clear sky. According to them, the UFO carried out vertical trajectories. The COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] informs that the event was filmed, but the video file of this sighting was not made available.
