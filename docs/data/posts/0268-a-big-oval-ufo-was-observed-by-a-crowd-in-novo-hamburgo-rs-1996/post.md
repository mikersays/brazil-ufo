# A big oval UFO was observed by a crowd in Novo Hamburgo-RS [1996]

*2020-06-29T14:07:38-03:00*

MARCH 20th, 1996. Time: 19h30. Duration: ongoing at the moment of the report. In the Ideal neighborhood, in Novo Hamburgo, State of Rio Grande do Sul, on a cloudy sky night with no visible stars, several people observed with the naked eye a stationary UFO “larger than an airplane” and an “almost oval shape with lights on its side”. The unidentified flying object was “shiny white” and its altitude could not be estimated by the witnesses. There were no reports of observed condensation trails or photographs taken of the sighting.
