# Several round white UFOs observed by eight people in Ceilândia-DF [1996]*

*2020-06-25T21:21:24-03:00*

Interrelated with case ARX 469

DEC 7th, 1996. Time: 8:25 pm. Duration: 30 minutes. Eight people in the Ceilândia region, Distrito Federal-DF, observed with the naked eye, several unidentified flying objects. They were “white, round, and formed by small lights”, following in a circular trajectory. According to the witnesses, the UFOS were about 3 meters in diameter, and “were in the North”, 15km apart and at an altitude also estimated at approximately 15km. Observations in the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] show that the witness communicated the sighting while it was happening: “It happens at this moment, coming out of clouds, lights varying in size forming a circle”.
