# A pilot observed a stationary multicolor UFO between Cuiabá-MT and São Paulo-SP [1995]

*2020-06-29T22:10:46-03:00*

NOV 11th, 1995. Time: 19h09. Duration: 40 minutes. A pilot of the PT-OFD and three other people, while flying from Cuiabá [MT] to São Paulo [SP], observed for 40 minutes an unidentified flying object, that was stationary, with the form of green, red and white lights. According to the pilot’s report, the UFO was at FL350 altitude  [35,000ft] and about 200 miles [?] from the aircraft with the witnesses. Still according to the pilot, photographs were taken of the unknown and distant object that was observed with the naked eye. The report from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also states that: “This is the PT-OFD pilot flying from SBCY TO SBSP, FL230, speed 230kt, type BR 9 FL”.
