# APP-BRASÍLIA-DF obtained a radar contact with an unidentified object [1991]

*2020-07-07T23:14:18-03:00*

CODA – Centro de Operações de Defesa Aeroespacial [Aerospace Defense Operation Center]

Brasília-DF, 19 APRIL 91

From: CODA Commander

To: 6SC

Subject: occurrence transcript.

I – I transcribe below the occurrence related to the service from April 17, 91 to and April 18, 91.

II – COPM I (IS HERNANDEZ) informed CODA that APP BRASÍLIA obtained a radar contact with an unidentified object, at approximately 08: 10am. Such an object was detected between radial 040 and 180 of GAMA. moving away at high speed at 50NM from Brasilia, southbound, the first contact occurred at 08:10 as mentioned above, however several others happened until * 08: 47z, observed in the raw video of APP BR.

Transcript:

00:44Z – “Received” UFO “report broadcast by COPM1 (transmitted by Sgt …) with the following content:” The circular or triangular object, in yellow, white and red, was observed at 23:10Z, by “Pampa Branco” [FAB aircraft] that had taken off from the Anápolis Air Base (SBAN) for a mission in the area “golden”. Apparently, such an object was at the same level as the element (FL200). The observation time was 50 minutes. The pilots did not confirm the estimated distance from the UFO to the observed aircraft; however, they reported that, apparently, the object kept the same distance in relation to the “Ace” (leader of the Pampa Branco), and, that was, the object, observed during the whole time of the mission disappearing soon after. […] Weather conditions: good weather. Object observed with the naked eye. “1S […].
