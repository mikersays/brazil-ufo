# Two pilots reported 4 ‘balls of fire’ during a flight between Porto Velho-RO and Cuiabá-MT [1993]

*2020-07-04T22:13:34-03:00*

JUNE 21st, 1993. Time: 19:50. Duration: not determined. The pilot and co-pilot of the a Boeing 767 from brazilian air company VARIG spotted four UFOs twice, with the naked eye and in a clear sky, when flying over the region between Porto Velho-RO and Campo Grande-MS, on the SBPV-SBCY route [from Porto Velho-RO to Cuiabá-MT]. According to the witnesses’ report, the objects resembled “four orange fireballs”, positioned between “10 and 11 hours” below the aircraft, in “wing position”.

Complementary data from the report made by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] reports that the: “Information [was] given by the sergeant […] (ACC-CD operator) who received the information from the aircraft commander. The VASP 767 commander reported:” They seemed to be tongues of fire from four fighters when they open after combustion “.
