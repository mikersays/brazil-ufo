DEC 19th, 2002. Time: 9:50 am. Duration: 90 minutes. In Sobradinho, a town in the Distrito Federal, two witnesses using binoculars spotted an unidentified flying object with the appearance of a bright star and the shape of a “balloon”. The UFO was stationary and at “very high” altitude and it remained in this mode for about 90 minutes, leaving no visible trails or trails, according to witnesses who observed it in good visibility conditions and in the “open sky”.
