# Pilot and Co-pilot saw a high speed UFO in front of their aircraft near Manaus [2010]

*2020-06-05T22:57:49-03:00*

NOV 13th, 2010. Time: 05:20 am. Duration: approximately 5 minutes. A pilot and co-pilot of flight GLO 1131, between Boa Vista, State of Roraima and Manaus, State of Amazonas, spotted an unidentified flying object when they were 75 nautical miles from Manaus and at approximately 23,000ft of altitude. According to the witnesses’ report, the UFO consisted of a very strong light that traveled at high speed, making maneuvers above and in front of its aircraft, and heading towards the Southwest direction. The following are additional comments from the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report: “It was reported to ACC-AZ, by GOL 1131, that it spotted a UFO when crossing FL 230 to FL 370, on RDL 152/75 NM MNS , with a Southwest direction. It was a very strong light, of high speed. Above and in front of your aircraft. “
