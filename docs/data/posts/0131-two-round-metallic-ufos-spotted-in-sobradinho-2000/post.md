# Two round metallic UFOs spotted in Sobradinho [2000]

*2020-06-14T22:53:01-03:00*

JUNE 11th, 2000. Time: 17h00 pm. Duration: 10 minutes. Two witnesses at the Polo de Cinema, in Sobradinho, Distrito Federal, observed two round metallic UFOs, flying slowly at an altitude “higher than airplanes” as one of the witnesses described. The shiny UFOs were seen in favorable visibility and climatic conditions moving towards the North direction without leaving traces of condensation. No film or photographs of the event were reported.
