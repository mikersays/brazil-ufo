JULY 13th, 2000. Time: 3:20 pm. Duration: 5 minutes. A witness residing in the center of the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object in the form of a “cigar” and white color. The UFO flew at a very low speed and at an altitude of 1km. The object that was sighted under good visibility conditions, moved from North to South without leaving trails of condensation or emitting any audible noise to the witness of the sighting.
