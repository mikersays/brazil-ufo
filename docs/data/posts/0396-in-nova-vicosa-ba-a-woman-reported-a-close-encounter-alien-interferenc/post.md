# In Nova Viçosa-BA, a woman reported a close encounter, alien interference and rumors of sex with extraterrestrials [1987]

*2020-07-10T17:47:55-03:00*

FEB 26th, 1987. Time: 10:30 pm. Duration: 6 hours. A couple and two friends in the city of Nova Viçosa, State of Bahia, had a close contact with several UFOs that were positioned 20 meters from them. According to the witnesses, the UFOs were shaped like a “plate”, emitted the colors red, yellow and blue, and were “faster than a bullet”. Also according to the reporting witness, the unidentified flying objects flew “in a straight line, towards the sea, more precisely, towards a ship. (According to the witness, the ship does research for Petrobras).

## ADDITIONAL COMMENTS PROVIDED BY THE WITNESS: 

## The information was given by the STF-RF Sgt […]. The observer informed that the mayor of PRADO-BA, also saw objects and transmitted them to SBSV. It was also recorded that at 14:00 on 05 March 87, his neighbor saw an object with the shape of a boomerang, black in color, “flying” towards the “ship” doing research for PETROBRAS.

## It was reported that the husband drew a firearm, and it did not work; the car (BUGGY) did not start.

## The observer also reported that the lights flashed and when they reached the ship they multiplied and stopped flashing. The luminosity of the objects was sufficient to illuminate the entire ship.

## The lady says she is “terrified” because her maid and two other girls had sex with 3 crew members, and noticed that the 3 had a red spot as if it were a tattoo or mark, on the left leg, next to the groin. She said that the crew, around 25 people, did not eat, refused to eat and were “furious” to the point of being violent. “
