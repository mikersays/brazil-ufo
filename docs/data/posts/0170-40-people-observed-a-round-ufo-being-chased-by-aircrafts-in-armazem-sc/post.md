# 40 people observed a round UFO being chased by aircrafts in Armazém-SC [1999]

*2020-06-18T14:00:23-03:00*

FEB 14th, 1999. Time: 22h00. Duration: 4 minutes. In the region of São José dos Macacos, in Armazém, State of Santa Catarina, a group of about 40 people spotted an unidentified, small, round flying object emitting yellow light. The UFO, according to witnesses, flies at low speed but at high altitude, going north and back, as if it were looking for something on the ground.

More details on the sighting can be read in the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report observations as follows:

“An hour after the UFO was seen, 2 aircraft appeared practically following it from a distance. These two aircraft were seen and heard by all present. After the UFO left the viewers’ view towards the north, the aircraft were still heard almost during all morning. The UFO sometimes emitted a flash of light directed at the ground (kind of a beacon0 that came to light up. “
