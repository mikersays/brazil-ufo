MAY 24th, 2001. Time 7:10 pm. Duration: 30 seconds. Near the São Gonçalo Canal, in the city of Pelotas, State of Rio Grande do Sul, a witness spotted with the naked eye and also with the use of binoculars, an unidentified flying object in the shape of a green hang-glider and the size of a football field. The UFO was flying at high speed in vertical motion and was seen from a low altitude of 700 meters. Also according to the witness’s report, the object was heading south and was observed in good visibility. The COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report also states that “in November 1999 the same observer [the witness] had already reported another sighting.”
