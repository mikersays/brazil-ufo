# A round star-like UFO reported in Curitiba-PR [1992]

*2020-07-06T15:57:28-03:00*

DEC 2nd, 1992. Time: 20:00. Duration: 20 seconds. In Curitiba, State of Paraná, three people spotted an unidentified flying object with a round shape, “slightly larger than a star”, as seen in the sky. According to the witnesses, the UFO was a “continuous white light” at “very high speed”, flying towards the South of Curitiba. The witness estimated the UFO’s altitude at approximately 45,000 ft. The object was observed with the naked eye in a cloud-free sky.
