AUG 4th, 1992. Time: 6:30 pm. Duration: 2 hours. In the Pilarzinho region, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, six people observed “two white balls of light”, alternating speed at 200 meters of altitude. According to the witnesses who observed the two UFOs from a distance of 1 km, they were performing circular movements while flying close to each other. The sighting occurred with the sky closed and the naked eye.
