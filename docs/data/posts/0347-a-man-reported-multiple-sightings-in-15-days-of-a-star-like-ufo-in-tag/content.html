NOV 7th, 1992. 20:50. Duration: 110 minutes. A man in the region of Taguatinga, Distrito Federal, reported that he has been constantly seeing an unidentified flying object for fifteen days. Together with two other people, the witness observed the sighting of this report with binoculars and, according to him, the UFO was round, with the appearance of a star, but as if it were “four headlights together” at an altitude of approximately 1,000 meters. According to him, it was like a “lamp, without color; satin” at very slow speed. “The witness stated that he observed the UFO moving in circles in the western region of the city. The sighting was observed with a very cloudy sky. Complementary observation in the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “After 22:00 it disappeared – with fast speed, it disappeared.”
