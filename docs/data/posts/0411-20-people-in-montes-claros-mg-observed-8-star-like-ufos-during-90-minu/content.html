June 5th, 1986. Time: 12:00. Duration: 90 minutes. In Montes Claros, State of Minas Gerais, about 20 people observed, for an hour and a half, 8 [eight] unidentified flying objects in the shape of a “star; like a planet (Venus) – fixed light point”, four of which were smaller. The UFOs were silver in color and flew slowly in formation of “Cruzeiro do Sul” [Southern Star], and then, leaving the formation and making random movements. The sighting was done in good visibility conditions. Complementary Data: “At 12:20Z, Montes Claros [MG] called us via TF2 / 348 {CV R.} informing about “unidentified flying objects in their vicinity, approximately 07 or 08 objects. Major M. “CC” was called in to take action. at 13:18 there was another call saying that the objects were gone”.
