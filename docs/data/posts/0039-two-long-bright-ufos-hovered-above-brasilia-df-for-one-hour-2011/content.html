JULY 30th, 2011. At about noon, two witnesses residing in the Cruzeiro Novo neighborhood, in Brasília-DF, observed for sixty minutes two unidentified flying objects hovering over the city at an airplane’s cruising altitude. According to the report, the two bright white UFOs were smaller and longer than an average plane and were heading slowly towards the Lago Sul direction. One of the witnesses informed COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], “that they saw two bright spots in the distance around noon. The elongated dots were moving in a random fashion and disappearing a few times.”
