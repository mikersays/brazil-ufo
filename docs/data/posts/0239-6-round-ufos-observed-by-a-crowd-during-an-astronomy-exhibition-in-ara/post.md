# 6 round UFOs observed by a crowd during an astronomy exhibition in Araçatuba-SP [1996]

*2020-06-28T00:04:52-03:00*

Interrelated with cases 474 and 456

AUG 17th 1996. Time: 5:27 pm. Duration: 60 minutes. During an astronomy exhibition in the city of Araçatuba, State of São Paulo, several people observed six unidentified flying objects, moving “in all directions, varied trajectories” for approximately one hour. According to witnesses, the UFOs were round in shape, gray in color, and moved at variable speeds at an altitude of about 5km. The event was observed with the naked eye and in good visibility.
