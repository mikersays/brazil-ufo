JAN 15th, 2015. In Curitiba, Paraná, at 19h51pm a mechanical engineer with knowledge in UFO field research and four more people spotted, with good visibility, two unidentified metallic objects, oval in shape, with about 50 meters of size each, and traveling at about 100km/h. According to witnesses, the objects were at an altitude of approximately 3 km, and, during the 10 minutes of the sighting, they repeatedly moved closer and further away from each other. The UFOs went from the vicinity of the Oscar Niemayer Museum to the city center of Curitiba, in a trajectory reported as from east to west. One of the witnesses recorded the video sighting on his/her cell phone and attached it to the COMDABRA’s [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report, but this video file was not made available in the Declassified Files by the military.
