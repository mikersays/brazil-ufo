APRIL 10th, 2015. Five people spotted a bright multicolored spherical UFO in Brasília-DF, at 02h10am, moving from NE to SW. The ocurrence was oberved by the witnesses for about one minute under a clear sky and good weather. The COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] filled the report with information given by one of the witnesses who is a military. The representative also wrote his observations in the report: “Telephone contact was made with APP-BR and this agency questioned some aircraft flying in the region, but no one saw the fact reported by the Captain.”
