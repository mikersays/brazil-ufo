# An oval multicolor UFO spotted at very low speed in Samambaia Sul-DF [1996]

*2020-06-28T10:04:05-03:00*

JUNE 6th, 1996. 18h30 pm. Duration: 30 minutes. When arriving at her residence in Samabaia Sul, Federal District, a woman observed an unidentified flying object 500 meters high. According to the witness, the UFO was also spotted by at least four other people nearby. The object was oval, white, green and red, about the size of “a helicopter approximately” and it was flying at a “slow, almost stopped” speed. The unknown object was observed with the naked eye, under good visibility conditions and followed a trajectory in the South / North direction.
