JUNE 5th, 1986. Time: 20:00. Duration: not determined. A resident of the city of Bandeirante, in the north of Paraná, called CINDACTA II [Second Integrated Air Defense and Air Traffic Control Center], an agency of the Ministry of Aeronautics, to report the sighting of an unidentified flying object with an “oval” and flattened shape “emitting a” green and red light sequence “. The sighting, according to the witness, was also accompanied by other people.
