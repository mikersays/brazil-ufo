# Two people saw a large round white UFO hovering over São Paulo [1999]

*2020-06-15T22:37:59-03:00*

DEC 13th, 1999. Time: 04h40 am. Duration: 40 minutes. In São Paulo-SP, under favorable weather conditions, two people saw a large round white UFO, resembling a white ball, invested by red light and green lights. According to the witnesses, the object was spotted traveling at low speed, but it remained stationary most of the time. There was no record of condensation trails or photographs taken by the witness.
