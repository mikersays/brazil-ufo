JAN 7th, 2015. Time: 20h30. The Salvador Approach Control (APP-SV) obtained the information that “a federal highway policeman had spotted an illuminated UFO going up and down in the sky over the city of Alagoinhas, State of Bahia, at around 20h30 pm”. According to the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] based on the information from the APP-SV, “after several attempts, there was no contact to obtain further details about the fact”.
