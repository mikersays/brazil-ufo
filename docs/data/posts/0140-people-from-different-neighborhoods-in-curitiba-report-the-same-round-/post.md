# People from different neighborhoods in Curitiba report the same round orange UFO [2000]*

*2020-06-15T19:50:04-03:00*

*Interrelated case: [see case ARX.0.0.570 “A round orange UFO hovered over Curitiba [2000]“*

JAN 13th, 2000. Time: 00h20 am. Duration: 1 hour. This report of the sighting of a round orange UFO in the Pilarzinho neighborhood was not the only one in Curitiba on the same night and in the same time frame [see case ARX.0.0.570 “A round orange UFO hovered over Curitiba [2000]“. In Pilarzinho, just like in Xaxim neighborhood, the witness reported the sighting of an unidentified round flying object, orange-colored moving northwards. The witness informed that the weather in that particular night. The sky was partly cloudy.
