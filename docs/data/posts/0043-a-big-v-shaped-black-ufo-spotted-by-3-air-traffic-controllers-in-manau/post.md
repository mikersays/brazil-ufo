# A big “V” shaped black UFO spotted by 3 air traffic controllers in Manaus [2010]

*2020-06-05T22:02:47-03:00*

NOV 15, 2010. At 18h55pm, during 15 minutes, two military operators and a meteorologist working at Eduardo Gomes Airport’s Control Tower spotted a large, dark unidentified flying object with a “V” shape and moving at high speed in the Northeast / Southwest direction. According to the observers’ report, the UFO traveled “apparently above the flight level of an ordinary aircraft, leaving behind what appeared to be condensation trails.

Following the additional observations of the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report: “TWR-EG operator, called informing that he had seen a UFO flying to the Southwest sector of SBEG [Manaus Airport]. and reported that the object apparently flew above normal levels for aircraft, [and also] cited that the object was dark in color, “V” shaped and that it appeared to be larger than a Boeing 747 and that he saw two traces of condensation trail. TWR-EG operators and the meteorologist spotted the object”.
