SEPT 18th, 2010. Time: 16h36pm. Duration: 02m06s. On an open and cloudless day, a resident of Alegrete, Rio Grande do Sul, spotted an unidentified flying object with a round shape and silver color, flying at low speed. The UFO was about 2km from the witness who described its size as larger than a car. The object flew slowly at an altitude of approximately 3km and moved from Northeast to Southeast. The report states that the UFO witness recorded the sighting on film, but the file was not made available. According to the additional observations in the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report: “There was no registration record on the fuselage. The light at the top of the UFO spun emitting” flashes. “There were also lights at the bottom. More appearances on other days. “
