JAN 5th, 1994. 00:30 am. Duration: not determined. A group of people in the region of Praia de Caiobá, coast of the State of Paraná, spotted an unidentified flying object moving in a trajectory that was perpendicular to the beach. The UFO was round and had an orange-red color and, according to the reporting witness, “the object appeared to alternately light up and go out”. Also according to the witness’s description, the UFO left a “whitish trail at the rear”. The sighting was done with the naked eye, with no records in photographs or filming.
