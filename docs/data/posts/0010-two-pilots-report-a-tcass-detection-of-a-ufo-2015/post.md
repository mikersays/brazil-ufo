# Two pilots report a TCAS’ detection of a UFO [2015]

*2020-05-30T18:32:00-03:00*

MAY 9th, 2015. At 18h04pm, two pilots from the same brazilian air company reported an unidentified flying object – detected by the TCAS (Traffic Collision Avoidance System) of their plane – moving at a speed approximately of 450 km/h and heading Northeast from the State of Rio de Janeiro’s airspace. Still according to the report, the event lasted five minutes and it was only detected by the TCAS.
