December 27th, 1983. Time: 00:57. Duration: not determined. An operator from the Control Tower at the Campinas Airport, in the State of São Paulo, reported that he received “many phone calls about an unidentified object in his North / Northeast sector”. The witness stated that he had seen some lights, but that he was unable to estimate what they were, but obtained information that the UFO was about 13NM [nautical miles] from Campinas. Complementary Data: “At 04:08 the OI requested video recording, which was done from 0410 to 0420. A meteorological observation letter was requested and the operator informed that it was ‘Nil’. At 04:32 I lost radar contact with the runway, leaving only small ‘plots’ in the sector “.
