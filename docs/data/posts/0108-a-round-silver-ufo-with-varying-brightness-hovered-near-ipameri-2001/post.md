# A round silver UFO with varying brightness hovered near Ipameri [2001]**

*2020-06-13T00:47:24-03:00*

MARCH 23rd, 2001. Time: 03:30 pm. Duration: 1h30m. In Catalão, State of Goiás, several people observed a round silver UFO with varying brightness, in a stationary position near the city of Ipameri *[Goiás, which is, from Catalão’s perspective, in the same direction of the city of Anápolis [GO] and near Paracatu [MG], where similar objects were also seen in the same day at very close times see cases of files ARX.0.0.602, ARX.0.0.603 and in Brasília 2 days later ARX.0.0.604]*. According to the witness, the sighting lasted about an hour and thirty minutes under good visibility conditions, with some clouds being observed near the object.
