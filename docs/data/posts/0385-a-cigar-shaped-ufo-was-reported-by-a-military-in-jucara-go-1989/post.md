# A cigar-shaped UFO was reported by a military in Juçara-GO [1989]

*2020-07-09T12:27:53-03:00*

DEC 21st, 1989. Time: 15:00. Duration: 2 hours. ” It was observed by the 2nd TEN of EB […], a cylindrical object with intense brightness. He stated that the object was at the vertical of Juçara, State of Goiás and that it remained static for approximately 2 hours. The same [Lieutenant] informed that he would film the UFO. At 11:13 pm we received a call from TWR AN [Anápolis Base Control Tower-GO] informing that we saw a bright object with intense light, which evolved from RDL 300 to RD 210 approximately in a downward attitude (the TWR AN operators reported) “

Cf. ARQUIVO NACIONAL (Brasil). Sistema de Informações do Arquivo Nacional (SIAN). Fundo ‘Objeto Voador Não Identificado (OVNI)’.
