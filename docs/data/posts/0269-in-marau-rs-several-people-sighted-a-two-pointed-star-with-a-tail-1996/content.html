MARCH 19th, 1996. Time: 20h30. Duration: not determined. In the city of Marau, state of Rio Grande do Sul, several people spotted an unidentified flying object in the western sector of the city. According to the witness who reported the case to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the UFO looked like a “two-pointed star with a tail”. The sighting was observed with the naked eye by several people, with no reports of photographs taken or footage made of the sighting.
