# A vertical multicolor UFO was spotted in Brasília-DF [1991]

*2020-07-07T22:09:58-03:00*

APRIL 26th, 1991. Time: 19:20. Duration: not determined. A woman in Brasília, Distrito Federal, when in the company of another person, noticed an unidentified flying object “due to its strong light” that it emitted. According to the witness, the UFO was “vertical” in shape, but with the appearance of “a small star of strong brightness”, with yellow, red and bluish colors on the edges. The UFO remained stationary while it was observed, at “Noroeste in the position AZ 16 Norte”, flying low and “close to the Bike Path / Embrapa”. The sighting was carried out in clear skies and with the naked eye.
