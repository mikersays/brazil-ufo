# A high number of calls forces a Fire Dept Chief report a UFO sight to the Air Force [2003]

*2020-06-11T12:52:37-03:00*

APRIL 26th, 2003. 04:05 am. In the city of Belo Horizonte, State of Minas Gerais, a captain of the Fire Department reported to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] the communication of several people reporting that they had spotted an unidentified flying object in Belo Horizonte:

“The Captain […] called to report the occurrence of many reports about unidentified objects in the air space of Belo Horizonte. Personally he did not see anything and he was unable to specify what was being seen.”
