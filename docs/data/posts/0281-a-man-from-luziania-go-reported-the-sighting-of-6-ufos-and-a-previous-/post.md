# A man from Luziânia-GO reported the sighting of 6 UFOs and a previous contact [1995]

*2020-06-29T21:10:23-03:00*

DEC 1st, 1995. Time: 15h48. Duration: 10 minutes. A man from Luziânia, State of Goiás, reported that he spotted about six unidentified flying objects performing circular movements in the vicinity of the Industrial Sector of Luziânia. According to the witness, the UFOs observed were round, with colors between metallic gray and ice white. They were about 2.5m high and were very quiet. The UFOs, according to the observer’s description, remained close to each other and at a high altitude. The witness reported that the first time he noticed the presence of UFOs was in March of that same year of 1995. Also according to the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witness stated that: “he receives defense messages in the sense of protection of the Planet Earth. Possible attacks using rays from solar energy. “
