- 04- DESCRIPTION: Luminous body

- 05- COLOR: Yellow, changing to red

- 06- SHAPE: Inverted plate, with a sharp apex forming an almost triangular figure.

- 07-APPARENT SIZE: 1.30 to 1.40m

- 09-SPEED: Low, at the beginning of the travel, gradual increase until reaching great speed

- 10-ALTITUDE: 30m (estimated)

- 11-DISTANCE: 50m (estimated)

- 12-OBSERVER (S): Mrs. AMÉLIA MARTINS DA SILVA
