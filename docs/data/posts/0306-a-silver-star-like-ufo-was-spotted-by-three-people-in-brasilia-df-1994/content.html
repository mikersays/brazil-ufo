AUG 8th, 1994. Time: 18:54. Duration: 30 seconds. In the city of Brasilia, Distrito Federal, three people spotted an unidentified flying object at high altitude in the shape of a silver star. According to the witness who reported the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the UFO was flying “very fast” towards the North. The sighting was carried out in a partially cloudy sky and with the naked eye. There were no reports of photographs or footage taken of the event.
