# In Samambaia-DF 2 people spotted a UFO moving “up and sideways”[1992]

*2020-07-06T16:16:52-03:00*

NOV 7th, 1992. 20:47. Duration: 30 minutes. In Samambaia region, Distrito Federal, two people observed, with the naked eye and in a cloudy sky, an unidentified flying object, moving “up and sideways”. The UFO, according to the witnesses, was round with the appearance of “a ping-pong ball”, and had an incandescent glow. The unknown object was observed flying fast about 10 km away from the observers going in the direction of the city of Goiânia-GO.
