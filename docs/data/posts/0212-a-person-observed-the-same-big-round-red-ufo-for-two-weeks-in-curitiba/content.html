MAY 5th, 1997. Time: 20h00. Duration: “many hours”. “For two weeks, around 8:00 pm, from his home window” at Jardim Ipê, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, a person observed an unidentified flying object with a round shape and red-orange color. According to the witness, from his perspective, the UFO appeared to be “bigger than the moon in the sky” and positioned itself to the Northeast. The sighting was done with the naked eye and in good conditions of visibility, with a cloudless sky.
