# Two spherical ufos spotted at incredible speed in Ivaiporã. [2015]

*2020-05-28T23:31:06-03:00*

AUGUST 31st, 2015 – In downtown Ivaiporã, a small city in the state of Paraná, at 7:45pm, a civil servant observed two white and spherical objects deslocating about 10 miles apart from each other, and at an altitude of approximately 15 miles high. The two unidentified flying objects, according to the report given to COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], were heading North at an approximate speed of 1,000 mph. Still according to the report, “the two objects then seemed to merged into one another”.
