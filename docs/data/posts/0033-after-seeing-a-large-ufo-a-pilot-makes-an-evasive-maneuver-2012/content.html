MAY 8th, 2012. 11:10 am. Pilot’s report of the flight of PTB-2207 from SBRP [Ribeirão Preto Airport], to SBPA [Porto Alegre Airport]. His plane was traveling between the cities of Lages, (State of Santa Catarina) and Caxias do Sul (State of Rio Grande do Sul), when he spotted a large round black UFO slowly moving towards the Southwest direction at an altitude of approximately 32,000ft and about 10 NM [nautical miles] from the eyewitness’ plane. The COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report also states that the pilot “made a left turn, authorized by the ACC-CW [Curitiba].” The same document informs that “the ACC-CW did not observe other traffic in the sector” at that moment.
