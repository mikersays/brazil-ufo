# Three people spotted a spinning UFO hovering near Boa Viagem in Recife [1998]

*2020-06-19T19:15:16-03:00*

JUN 22nd, 1998. Time: 00h48 am. Duration: 30 seconds. In the Boa Viagem neighborhood in Recife, State of Pernambuco, three people observed an unidentified flying object in the form of a multicolored conical necklace, resembling a star at an altitude of 2,500m. According to the report, the UFO remained stationary and spinning around itself when it was observed.

In addition to what was said, the following observations were made in the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “As mentioned above, the observer likes to observe the sky and he informed that he never saw this type of object. He reported [to us] what happened during the same” [sighting- as it was happening].
