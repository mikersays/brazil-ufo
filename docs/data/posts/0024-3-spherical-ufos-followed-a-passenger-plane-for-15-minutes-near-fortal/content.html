FEB 13th, 2013. At the end of the flight that left Manaus for Fortaleza, a pilot reported that 80 nautical miles to reach Fortaleza Airport, at an altitude of 28,000 ft, they spotted 3 luminous spheres following their aircraft. The report was made, according to the file consulted, through the telephone with the ACC-RE [in Recife]. The unidentified flying objects moved at approximately 700 km/h, and they were yellow in color, and spherical in shape. The sighting lasted about 15 minutes and happened at 4:40 am under good weather conditions.
