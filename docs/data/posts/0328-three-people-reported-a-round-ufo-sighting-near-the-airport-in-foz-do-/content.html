JAN 13th, 1994. Time: 20:40. Duration: 25 minutes. Three people reported a sighting in the vicinity of the “SBFI” [Foz do Iguaçu Airport, State of Paraná]. According to the description, the UFO was round, large, but varied in size and remained stationary during the time of the observation. The unknown object had an intense blue-green color and was positioned at “medium” height. The sighting occurred with the naked eye and under unstable weather with light rain.
