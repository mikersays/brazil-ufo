APRIL 20th, 2012. At 11.05 pm, in the southeastern region of the city of Belo Horizonte, State of Minas Gerais, near Serra do Curral, on a night of good visibility, a witness spotted three large white UFOs from his apartment window, with indefinite form and moving at great speed. The three UFOs were at an altitude of approximately 10 km and, according to the report, they left no trace of any kind when traveling. The sighting lasted two seconds.
