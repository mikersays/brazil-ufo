AUG 12th, 2000. Time: 19h15pm. Duration: 10 minutes. In the Champagnat neighborhood in the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, a witness spotted two small unidentified flying objects in the form of a white “cartridge”. According to the report, the UFOs were separated by a distance of 4,000ft between them and moved in the East / West direction at a slow speed and at an estimated altitude of 10,000ft. The sighting was made, according to the witness, under good visibility conditions, with “scattered clouds” being noted at the observation site.
