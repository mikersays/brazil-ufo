MARCH 21st, 1996. Time: 20h04. Duration: 40 minutes. A round UFO with white lights and about 20 meters in size was spotted flying fast in the city of Cachoeirinha, State of Rio Grande do Sul. According to the 5 people who observed the unknown object, it was at an altitude of approximately 1,000 meters heading North, then making a curve around itself. The sighting was carried out by the naked eye and in a clear sky. The witness who reported the case to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] said it was “Impressive! It was the first time he saw it”.
