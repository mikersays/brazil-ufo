July 12th, 2014. Halfway between Juazeiro do Norte Airport and Fortaleza Airport, both in the State of Ceará, 2 people, including the pilot of the flight PTWRG aircraft, observed at 8:00 pm, under a clear sky and good visibility, a low-speed movement of a white and red unidentified flying object with a circular shape. The sighting lasted fifteen minutes. The relatively large object, according to witnesses, was approximately 9,000 feet high and about 15 MN [nautical miles] away from the observers’ plane. In the report of the same pilot, who also informed having seen this type of light before, there are observations on the report collected by the representative of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The PTWRG from SBJU to SBFZ saw a red light below a white that resembled the beacon light of an airplane. He considered that he has little knowledge about UFOs “.
