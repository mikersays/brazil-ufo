# A pilot reported a large UFO casting beams of light in his direction near Uberaba-MG [1986]

*2020-07-13T10:40:27-03:00*

AUG 5th, 1986. Time: 19:46. Duration: not determined. The aircraft PT-JTZ, on the route between the cities of Rio de Janeiro-RJ and Uberaba-MG, contacted the DPV-UR [Uberaba-MG Flight Protection Department] asking “if there were any aircraft close to the ‘FRANCA position’ at FL 180 [18.000ft]” and got a negative answer. According to the pilot of the PT-JTZ, the unidentified flying object had a round shape, large size, white color of strong intensity and remained stationary while observed. The pilot also stated that “the object did not look like any celestial star and cast strong beams of flashing lights in his direction”.
