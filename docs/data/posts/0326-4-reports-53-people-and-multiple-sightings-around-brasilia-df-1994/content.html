Between 18:00 and 21:42 on February 2nd, 1994, 53 people from 4 different locations in the Distrito Federal region observed an unidentified flying object moving very slowly, and sometimes remaining stationary.

The reported sightings were made from:  Novo Cruzeiro district in Brasília-DF; Cidade Ocidental-GO; Gama Oeste-DF; and Valparaíso-GO.

– 20 people in Cruzeiro Novo, Brasília, Distrito Federal, between 18:00 and 18:30, observed an unknown object in the shape of a “stretched binocular, changing position in an East-South direction” at high altitude. According to the witness who reported the event to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] the UFO was green, yellow and red, and it was large. It seemed like a “kite” in the sky.

– 11 people in the South of Cidade Ocidental-GO from 18:15 to 18:42 observed a UFO “stationary, reflecting the sun” and moving at “low speed” in a straight path.

– 15 people in Gama Oeste, Federal District from 18:45 to 20:00, observed a UFO in slow speed, with a sparkling “star” appearance, but “bigger than a star”, flying at a great altitude in an East-West trajectory.

– 7 people in Valparaíso-GO, on the “road that goes to Luziânia-GO”, between 19:20 and 19:30 saw a UFO reflecting the sun and “moving very slowly on the horizon”, at about 1km altitude. According to the description, the UFO was round and made a “straight ascending trajectory, in the South direction”.

All observations reported were made with clear skies and with good visibility conditions.
