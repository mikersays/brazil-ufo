# Three people reported sighting and close encounter with 2 red star-like UFOs in Curitiba [1999]

*2020-06-18T19:14:21-03:00*

JAN 20th, 1999. Time: 11h00pm. Duration: 30 seconds. In the Santa Cândida neighborhood, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, three people spotted and had close eye contact with two UFOs in the shape of red stars. With about twenty centimeters in diameter, the red orbs were only three feet away from the witnesses. After that the UFOs  zigzagged, reached the ground, emitted a low humming noise, left dark condensation trails and finally headed north.
