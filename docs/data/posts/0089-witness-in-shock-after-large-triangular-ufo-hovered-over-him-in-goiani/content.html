MARCH 12th, 2002. Time: 16h50 pm. Duration: 2 min and 30s. A witness residing in Bairro Setor Park Anhanguera, in the city of Goiânia, State of Goiás, spotted an unidentified triangular flying object of a dark opaque color, with a size equivalent to a “standard lot”, approximately 10m x 30m. According to the report, the UFO stood still, hovering just 60 meters away above the witness, and when it moved it made sudden movements. The witness also reported that he studies UFOs on his own. In the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The witness declared that he was quite astonished during the moments after the observation of the phenomenon. He said he was still not willing to provide information to others except the Air Force.”
