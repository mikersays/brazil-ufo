MARCH 19th, 1994. Time: 00:15 am. Duration: 6 minutes. In Curitiba, State of Paraná, a witness reported an unidentified flying object, with the appearance of a star, however, flying at a slow speed. The UFO, according to the description, was “like a point” predominantly blue, going towards the center of the city, South of the observer. The UFO was at a high altitude in a “straight and uniform trajectory, always in the same direction”  leaving no traces of condensation and “becoming more distant [until it] lost its luster”. The sighting was made with the naked eye in a clear sky.
