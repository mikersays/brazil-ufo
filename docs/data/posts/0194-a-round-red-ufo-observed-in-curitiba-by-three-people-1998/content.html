FEB 10th, 1998. Time: 9:00 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. Under a partially cloudy sky, in the neighborhood of Água Verde, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, three people observed a round red UFO similar to a star. According to witnesses, the unidentified flying object was at low speed, but at a “very high” altitude on its North/South trajectory. They couldn’t inform the approximate altitude of the object they sighted.
