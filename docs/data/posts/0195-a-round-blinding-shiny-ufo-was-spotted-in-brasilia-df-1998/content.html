JAN 15th, 1998. Time: 00h40 am. Duration: 5 minutes. In Brasília, Distrito Federal, an unidentified flying object with the “size of an airplane” was spotted by six people on a clear night. According to the witnesses, the big UFO was round, shiny and remained “stopped” most of the time at an altitude of 500 meters. And when he moved, he did it at a “slow speed” towards the North in a straight line. The witness who reported to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] emphasized how “blinding” the UFO light was.
