DEC 14th, 1994. 04h00 am. Duration: not determined. In Brasília, Distrito Federal, a military man and four more people observed an unidentified flying object with the shape of a blue star. According to the information from the military witness, the UFO was sighted in the vicinity of Lago Sul in Brasília, and was observed with clear skies and good visibility conditions. The witness was unable to estimate the altitude and speed of the blue star sighted in that region south of Brasilia.
