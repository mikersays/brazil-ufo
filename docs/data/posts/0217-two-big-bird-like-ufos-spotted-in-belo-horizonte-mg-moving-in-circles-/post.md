# Two big bird-like UFOs spotted in Belo Horizonte-MG moving in circles [1997]

*2020-06-24T17:01:18-03:00*

MARCH 28th, 1997. Time: 00h:25am. Duration: 30 minutes. In Delfim Moreira neighborhood, in Belo Horizonte, State of Minas Gerais, nine people spotted, with the naked eye, two unidentified flying objects moving fast at an altitude of approximately 500 meters. According to the witnesses, the UFOs were shaped like “a bird”. One of them was large and the other one small. The large UFO was positioned in front of the small UFO and both were white in color and performed circular movements. Also according to the report given to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], firstly, the witnesses began to spot the two UFOs “from the balcony of their apartments, and then the people went up a mountain” to observe the unknown objects. The entire sighting occurred “after the Corpus Christi parade” when they saw “a quick light at [during a] break”.
