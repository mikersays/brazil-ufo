# Three people saw a gray UFO expelling golden smoke in Medianeiras [1999]

*2020-06-16T17:51:07-03:00*

OCT 10th, 1999. Time: 8:30 pm. Duration: 25 minutes. In the city of Medianeiras, State of Paraná, three people spotted an unidentified flying object with indefinite shape and gray color at an altitude of 35,000ft. The UFO, according to the witnesses, left traces of golden smoke and was seen heading west. The sighting took place under good conditions of visibility without photographs taken by the observers.
