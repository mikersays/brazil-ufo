JUNE 2nd, 1998. Time: 12h03. Duration: 10 minutes. In the Park Way neighborhood, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, one person spotted an unidentified flying object with a round shape and white color, moving approximately at a “plane’s altitude”. According to the witness, the UFO, which had the appearance of a “white dot”, was flying fast from the South towards the North. The witness observed the UFO with the naked eye and under a clear sky, with good visibility.
