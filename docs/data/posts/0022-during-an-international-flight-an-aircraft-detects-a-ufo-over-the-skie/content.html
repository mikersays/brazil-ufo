JUN 19th, 2013. At 12:30 pm, in the vicinity of Manaus, Amazonas, the aircraft of flight AVA 0086, which traveled from São Paulo-SP to Bogotá, Colombia, reported having identified for three minutes through of its radar system the movement of an unidentified flying object at about 12 NM [Nautical Miles] of flight AVA 0086. The observations on the report collected by the representative of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] still provides the following information : “The aircraft reported to ACC-AZ that it spotted traffic on its radar at 1 o’clock and 2 o’clock positions at 12 NM [nautical miles] and shortly afterwards it disappeared, close to the Medle position. ACC-AZ reported that, at that moment, there was no traffic close to the aircraft. The COpM-4 OIV service also reported that there was no traffic close to the aircraft. “
