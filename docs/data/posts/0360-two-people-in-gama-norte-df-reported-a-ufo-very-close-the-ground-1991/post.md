# Two people in Gama Norte-DF reported a UFO very close the ground [1991]

*2020-07-07T22:51:55-03:00*

APRIL 19th, 1991. Time: 22:15. Duration: not determined. Two residents of the Gama Norte region, Distrito Federal, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object with a star shape and green, blue and red colors, blinking alternately. According to the witnesses, the UFO was “close to the ground” at “slow or stopped” speed. The observers saw the UFO at an estimated distance of 15 km horizontally, in good visibility and with the naked eye. Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “At 2:10am, the same [reporting witness] called to inform that the object was moving slowly”.
