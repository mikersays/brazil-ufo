MARCH 30th, 1991. Time: 21:15. Duration: 20 minutes. From the window of his apartment in Brasilia, Distrito Federal, a man noticed an unidentified flying object flying at high speed over a television tower and about 4 km high. According to the reporting observer, 15 people also saw the UFO which was round in shape, large and orange in color. The sighting was carried out under conditions of visibility considered good, with the naked eye and with an approximate [horizontal] distance from the UFO of about 5 km.

Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The object initially performed a horizontal flight; it stopped for about 10 minutes and started a vertical flight until it became small and then disappeared”.
