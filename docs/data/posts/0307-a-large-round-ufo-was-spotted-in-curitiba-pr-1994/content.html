AUG 8th, 1994. Time: 19:55. Duration: 18 minutes. In the Bacacheri region, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, two people observed an unidentified flying object, with a round shape, large size and of a strong white color, at an altitude of about 8,000 meters. According to the information from the reporting witness, the UFO was observed at a horizontal distance of about 15km and remained stationary during the sighting.
