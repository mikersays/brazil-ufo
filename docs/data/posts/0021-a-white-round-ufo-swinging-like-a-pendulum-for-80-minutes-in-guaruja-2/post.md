# A round white UFO spotted swinging like a pendulum for 80 min in Guarujá [2014]

*2020-06-01T15:56:49-03:00*

MAY 4th. 2014. During a clear afternoon, at Praia da Enseada do Guarujá, State of São Paulo, from the window of her apartment at 04h20pm, a witness observed, for an hour and twenty minutes, an unidentified flying object with a round shape and of a strong white color, at an altitude “much lower than [the] altitude of the plane” making her think it was a helicopter. The object was “at the altitude of a helicopter”, but the witness ruled out this possibility because the object remained “still” and seemed to “swing like a pendulum”. Also in the report collected by the representative of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] there is information that the witness recorded the sighting in photo and video, however such records were not made available in the file of this report.
