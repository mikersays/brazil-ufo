DEC, 12th, 1990. Time: 22:45. Duration: 45 seconds. Three people in Brasília, Distrito Federal, spotted two unidentified flying objects, round white, “star-sized” and leaving a trail when moving. According to the witnesses, one of the UFOs was sharper than the smaller one. The UFOs positioned themselves on top of the Brasilia tower, always close to each other. The trajectory of the two unknown objects was Northeast-Southwest, having been observed in a sky with few clouds and with the naked eye and binoculars. Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “[the] object approaching the observer, [revealed a] greater luminosity”. The reporting witness informed that she has observed UFOs before.
