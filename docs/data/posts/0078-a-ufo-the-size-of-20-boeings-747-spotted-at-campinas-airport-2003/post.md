# A UFO the size of 20 Boeings 747 spotted at Campinas Airport [2003]

*2020-06-11T13:30:52-03:00*

APRIL 26th, 2003. Time: 03h00am. Duration: 20 seconds. At the Campinas Airport, State of São Paulo, five people spotted an unidentified flying object in the shape of a reddish circle and the size described as twenty (20) times larger than a Boeing 747. Also according to the witness’s report to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the UFO was at a low altitude and it was flying at a “very fast” speed and it was also observed under good visibility.
