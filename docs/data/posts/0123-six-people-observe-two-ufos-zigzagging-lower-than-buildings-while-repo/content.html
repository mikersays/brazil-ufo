NOV 5th, 2000. Time: 00:05. Duration: 15 minutes. In Juvevê, a district of Curitiba, State of Paraná, six (6) people saw two (2) unidentified flying objects in the form of luminous dots and yellowish white color, flying at a slow speed and at a very low altitude, “below the buildings’ “. The UFOs were making erratic movements and zigzags.

The report to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] was given during the sighting, as the information shows below:

“The observation was underway at the time of the communication. […] she reported that the objects looked like they were” dancing “and one of them descended below the buildings, getting to be confused with the lights of those. Her sons and her husband were present (doctor and university professor) “.
