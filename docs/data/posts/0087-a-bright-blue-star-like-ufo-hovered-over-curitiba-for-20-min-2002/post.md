# Three bright blue star-like UFOs hovered over Curitiba for 20 min [2002]

*2020-06-11T18:06:49-03:00*

JUNE 16th, 2002. Time: 6:30 pm. Duration: 20 minutes. In Alto da XV district, city of Curitiba, State of Parana, a witness saw three (3) unidentified flying objects shaped like bright blue stars. The UFOs, according to the witness’ report, were stationary in a region south of the city center of Curitiba. The sighting of the two UFOs was carried out under conditions of visibility described as “good” from the center of the city. There were no records of trails or signs of condensation left in the displacement of the sighted objects.
