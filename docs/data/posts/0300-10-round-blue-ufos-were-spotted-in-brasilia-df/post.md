# 10 round blue UFOs were spotted in Brasília-DF

*2020-07-01T16:40:27-03:00*

NOV 20th, 1994. Time: 23:20. Duration: 5 minutes. From the Asa Norte region in Brasília, Distrito Federal, one person spotted ten small unidentified flying objects, with circular shapes and light blue color. According to the witness, the UFOs were flying close to each other at an altitude of about 500 meters. The sighting was carried out in cloudy sky with the naked eye. There were no reports of photographs or filming of the event.
