# During a flight to Manaus-AM a pilot reported a continuous UFO sighting [1994]

*2020-07-01T19:34:05-03:00*

Military internal communication report informing the sighting of an unidentified flying object by a pilot with the transcription of the report by the ACC-MU.

1 – Synthesis of occurrences with unidentified flying objects.

– VRG 302, from Santarém to Manaus, reported having seen a luminous object between 140NM and 50NM in Manaus.

– The appendix, part.n9-033 / ATS, with the transcription of the report in the book of occurrence of the ÀCC-MU, contains further details of the fact.

2 – GENERAL OBSERVATIONS CONCERNING INFORMATION:

The information regarding the flyover authorization was acquired from the ACC Manaus.

REGIONAL FLIGHT PROTECTION SERVICE

DETACHMENT OF FLIGHT PROTECTION EDUARDO GOMES

Part N9 033 / ATS

From the Chief To Mr. Chief of DPVEG

Manaus, September 12, 1994.

Subject: Transcription of Occurrence

I transcribe, the following occurrence verified in the LRO of ACC-MU, on September 6, 94 – 3rd Shift.

“I inform you that the VRG302 from SBSN [SANTARÉM-PA] to SBEG [MANAUS-AM] reported between 0014Z and 0023Z seeing an unidentified luminous object from FL310 until part of its descent for landing between 140NM and 70NM. I inform that the only known traffic was TBA501 from SBEG to SBBE, previously vectored by the APP to get rid of the VRG302. Even after its traffic was overcome, the VRG302 continued to spot the object. He later detailed (between 0100Z and 0109Z) that the sighted object was not captured as a target on his on-board radar, nor by the APP , and that the dimension of its light was, at its strongest, the equivalent of three or four times greater than the brightest star seen by the crew. He also explained that the sky was clear (without a cloud) and that when he approached the light, it became static and diminished in size, then disappeared, but which reappeared in front of him (between 30NM and 40NM) and disappeared again; this, according to Commander FROTA, was repeated a few times until he was no longer seen gives”. PFR.

Service team: 3S BCT SERAFIM, 3S BCT MIRANDA, 3S JÂNIO and 3S GAMA.

3. I transcribe, the following occurrence verified in the LRO of APP-MN, on 05.09.94, 3rd Shift: “The VRG302 reported seeing 50NM and 11HS position still unidentified. Information not established radar contact”.

4. Service team: IS BCT KLAIC, 3S GILBERTO and 3S ALDRIN.

R.A.da Silva. / Ten – Esp – CTA / Ch. of the ATS Organs ”
