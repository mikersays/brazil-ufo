# A round orange UFO was seen stopping and changing its course in Curitiba [1997]

*2020-06-24T21:44:55-03:00*

MARCH 6th, 1997. Time: 00h20 am. Duration: 10 minutes. In Curitiba, State of Paraná, a person spotted an unidentified flying object, round and orange, at a high altitude. According to the witness, the UFO was “very high”, so that “it looked close to the stars”. The object was initially on the East-West trajectory, but then “the object stopped for a while and then changed direction to the Northeast”. The witness observed the event with the naked eye, under a clear sky and did not observe any condensation trails left by the UFO.
