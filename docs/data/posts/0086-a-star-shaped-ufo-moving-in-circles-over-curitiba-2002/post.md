# A star-shaped UFO moving in circles over Curitiba [2002]

*2020-06-11T17:55:02-03:00*

AUG 8th, 2002. 10:48 pm. Duration: 25 seconds. In the Batel neighborhood of Curitiba, State of Paraná, a university professor, using binoculars, spotted an unidentified red star-shaped flying object, moving in circles, flying at an altitude of approximately 5,000ft and heading West from the East. The sighting of the unidentified flying object was carried out under conditions of visibility described as “good”.
