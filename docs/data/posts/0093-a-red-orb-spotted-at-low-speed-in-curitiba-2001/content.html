DEC 7th, 2001. Time: 8:30 pm. Duration: 20 minutes. In São Brás neighborhood, in Curitiba, Paraná, the witness saw a red orb at an approximate distance of 18 NM [nautical miles] – 33km, moving at low speed. The UFO appeared to be at an altitude of 5,000ft and alternated small movements with stops in its South/Southeast trajectory. Also according to the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witness is a former student and member of the Aeroclube School of Aviation. The sighting was carried out under good visibility conditions.
