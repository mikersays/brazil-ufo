APRIL 15th, 2001. Time: 10:29 am. Duration: 13 minutes. A witness living in the city of Dourados, State of Mato Grosso do Sul, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object hovering at an altitude of approximately 30,000ft and at a distance of 20km from the observer. The UFO shifted its shape, after being oval at first, and emitted a white light. The object hovered over the western sector of Dourados, and it was observed under good visibility and also recorded on video, but the video file was not made available.
