# Three people observed a spherical UFO rise vertically in Curitiba-PR [1995]

*2020-06-30T00:11:25-03:00*

MAY 17th, 1995. Time: 05h30 am. Duration: one hour. Three people of the same family observed an unidentified flying object “moving away from the stars and rising vertically” for an hour. According to the witness who reported the sighting, the UFO was spherical, “twice as large as the planet Venus” as seen in the sky, and “bright blue”. The UFO moved “at 4 km altitude, at. 45° in relation to the observer, and more than 5,000 meters in a straight line, horizontally”, in the “northwest position”. The sighting took place in clear skies and with the naked eye.
