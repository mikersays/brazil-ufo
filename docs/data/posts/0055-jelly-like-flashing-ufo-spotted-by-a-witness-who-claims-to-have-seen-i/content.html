DEC 23rd, 2007. Time: 19h22pm. Duration: 10 seconds. A witness residing in the Parque Dez neighborhood, in the city of Manaus, State of Amazonas, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object, that was “slow, [and] practically did not move”. The UFO was clear and luminous, being observed in a partially cloudy sky, and performed “a small lateral movement”. The witness also told COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] that he had some knowledge about UFOs and provided more details about the sighting, as can be read below:
“Informed that there were 2 clear and luminous flashes, the second being more intense. They occurred below the clouds with a small and slow lateral displacement. He also informed that the object did not have a defined shape and that it did not appear to have a firm consistency, appearing to be somewhat gelatinous, jelly-like form. He added that he had information, he did not say the source, that a similar event had occurred in Piauí and Portugal. He also said that it was not the first time that he had seen these flashes and that he had already reported the fact on another occasion.” * (see other report given by the same witness at this link: BR DFANBSB ARX.0.0.661)
