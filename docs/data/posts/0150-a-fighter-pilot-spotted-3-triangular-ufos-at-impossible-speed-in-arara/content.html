*Interrelated with the case ARX.0.0.558*

SEPT 25th, 1999. Time: 01h00am. Duration: 3 minutes. When he was 20NM [nautical miles] from the city of Araraquara, State of São Paulo, and at 35,000ft altitude with an F100 fighter, a pilot spotted three triangular UFOs flying in line, at an altitude higher than that of the fighter and “amazingly fast”. The unidentified objects were larger than the F100 and were positioned according to it. UFOs emitted an extremely white and bright light and left a yellow trail like a “thread of fire” before disappearing.

Also according to the observations of the witnesses who performed the sighting reported to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]:

“The traffic was spotted at one o’clock in relation to the ANV’s bow [200 *]. With an “amazing” speed, it started moving at eleven o’clock. At that moment, according to the pilot, several alarms sounded on the plane. After that, the pilot called the ACC BSB [Air Control Center of Brasilia] to inform the situation, as soon as the message started, the three traffic disappeared with an “amazing speed” leaving a thin trail of fire on the horizon.
