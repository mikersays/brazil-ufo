# In Brasília-DF, three people observed a white UFO moving vertically [1989]

*2020-07-10T12:12:32-03:00*

Brasília [Núcleo Bandeirante], Distrito Federal. Date: March 29, 1989. Time: 08:30 am. Duration: one hour. Three people observed an unidentified flying object at about 2.000m altitude and in the western position of the city. According to the reporting witness, the UFO had a light white color, flying slowly making vertical movements. Also according to the witnesses, it was not possible to estimate the size of the UFO that was seen with the naked eye and in good visibility conditions.
