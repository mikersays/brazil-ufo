OCT 22nd, 2000. Time: 14h20pm. Time: 15 minutes. In downtown Curitiba three people observed two round silver shiny UFOs flying fast at an altitude of 10km and going towards the South region “near Santa Cândida”. The UFOs made no noise and left no condensation trails. There were no video recordings or photographs registered. The visibility conditions were good at the time of sighting.
