# UFO spotted at sunrise in Guarapuava [2003]

*2020-06-11T11:44:16-03:00*

JULY 27th, 2003. Time: 4:00 am. Duration: 15 minutes. In the city of Guarapuava, State of Paraná, a local resident spotted a small, yellowish white unidentified flying object at great speed. The witness was unable to specify the size or altitude of the object that was on the horizon from the perspective of the observer. The witness also stated that the UFO left a clear condensation trail and that “the object was spotted during the sunrise. A filter was used on the lens. If necessary, send the photos”. It was reported that the witness took 8 digital photos in a row, but this material was not made available with this file.
