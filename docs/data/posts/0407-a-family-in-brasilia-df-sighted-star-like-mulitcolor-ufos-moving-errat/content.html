May 26th, 1986. Time: 2:30pm. Duration: 3 minutes. In Brasília, four people from the same family reported the sighting of “4 or 5 unidentified flying objects”, in the form of “blinking stars”. According to the witnesses, the UFOs had “faint, undefined colors, silver and white, changing color. The unknown objects flew at slow speed and in “disorderly formation”, moving “to the right from the point of view of observation” , in a position of about “60° to 80° in relation to the horizon”. The sighting was carried out with binoculars in good weather and visibility. Observation of the report: “video recording was not necessary because nothing unusual was observed in the radar covers”.
