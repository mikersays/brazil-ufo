JAN 13th, 2000. Time: 23h50pm. Duration: 01 hour. In the Xaxim neighborhood, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, under a partially cloudy sky, several witnesses observed an unidentified round flying object with an orange color. According to the witnesses’ reports, the UFO remained stationary for as long as it was observed. The COMDABRA’s [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report tells that: “He informed that many nieghbors in his street were also watching.”
