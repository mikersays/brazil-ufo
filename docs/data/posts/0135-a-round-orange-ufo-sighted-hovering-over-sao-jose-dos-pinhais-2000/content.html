APRIL 23rd, 2000. Time: 10:43 pm. Duration: 30 minutes. In the city of São José dos Pinhais, State of Paraná, on a clear night with good visibility, a witness observed an unidentified flying object, with a round shape and an orange color, alternating movement and stops before heading East fastly. There was no report of photo or video recording of the sighting.

To COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] the witness also reported that:

“He observed the object moving towards East, and then Serra do Mar. After standing for 20 minutes, the object moved at considerable speed, then it disappeared.”
