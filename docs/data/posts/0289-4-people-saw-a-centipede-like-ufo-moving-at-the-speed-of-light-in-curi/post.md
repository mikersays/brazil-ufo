# 4 people saw a centipede-like UFO moving at the speed of light in Curitiba-PR [1995]

*2020-06-30T18:30:01-03:00*

MAY 15th, 1995. Time: 20h10. Duration: 3 to 5 minutes. Four people in the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, observed three “centipede-shaped points of light, sometimes compressing, sometimes stretching”. The unidentified flying objects performed “radical color changes”, and were initially “stationary, and sometimes as fast as light”. According to the witnesses, the unknown objects remained close to each other, and “well above the flight level of aircrafts and balloons”, making vertical movements above the central region of Curitiba. The sighting was performed by the naked eye in a partially cloudy sky.
