JAN 26th, 2001. Time: 08h36 pm. Duration: 10 minutes. In the Capão do Imbuia neighborhood, Curitiba, State of Paraná, two witnesses reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object in the form of a reddish-white point of light, with a “faster than an airplane” speed. The UFO flew at an altitude of approximately 200ft in variable movements, heading north. According to the witness, this reported sighting was not his fisrt one: “I started watching a month ago.”
