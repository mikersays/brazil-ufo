# The airport personnel spotted a star-like UFO twice at Ponta Grossa Airport [1997]

*2020-06-23T21:33:56-03:00*

MAY 6th, 1997. Time: 22h20. Duration: 10 to 15 minutes. Three people working at the Ponta Grossa Airport, State of Paraná, observed a star-like UFO at 2,000ft of altitude, flying slowly near one of the airstrips. The unknown object had the appearance of a star changing colors from amber to pink and red, and heading Southwest. It was observed by the naked eye and, according to the report, that was the second time they saw this kind of phenomenon in that region.
