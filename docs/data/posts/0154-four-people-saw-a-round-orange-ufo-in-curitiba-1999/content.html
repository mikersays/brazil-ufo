AUG 1st, 1999. Time: 11h00 pm. Duration: 3 minutes. In the Champagnat neighborhood, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, four people spotted an unidentified flying object with the shape of a round orange light, on a clear night of good visibility. From the perspective of the four witnesses, the UFO, from a distance, was observed with a silhouette “bigger than [a] star” seen in the firmament. The object moved at a fast speed, going from North to South and leaving light colored condensation trails.
