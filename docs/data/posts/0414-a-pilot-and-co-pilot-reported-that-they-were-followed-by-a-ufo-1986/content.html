August 28, 1986. Time: 20:40. Duration: 40 minutes. Two pilots from two different planes flying between the region of Franca [State of São Paulo] and Triângulo Mineiro [State of Minas Gerais], reported sightings of an unidentified flying object. According to the Brasília-DF Approach Control report, TAM’s TF-585 flight, at around 9:00 pm, “was seeing unidentified flying objects near its aircraft when it was flying over Uberaba, in the state of Minas Gerais. A pilot from another aircraft, PP-EFC, spotted around 8:00 pm the “light” in the same position.

The TF-585 pilot reported that he noticed “an object in the aircraft’s 3-hour position; before Araguari”, State of Minas Gerais. The UFO was “similar to a landing gear headlight”, white in color and flying at an estimated speed of 400 km/h, following the TF-585. The UFO followed the pilot’s aircraft while “quickly varying in level and the light flashed when the Bandeirante [TF-585] flashed the lights.” The pilot reported that, with him, the sighting was also made by the co-pilot and 11 other people on the aircraft.

The TF-585 co-pilot reported that the UFO had an oval shape, was the size of a Bandeirante model airplane [like the TF-585] and that it was about 5km away from his aircraft and that the same unknown object “maintained the 3-hour position for 40 minutes – heading 195° and moved away in São Joaquim da Barra [State of São Paulo]”. Like the pilot, his TF-585 colleague also reported that “when the Bandeirante turned the lights on and off, the object did the same”, that is, it copied various actions of the TAM aircraft.
