# Red star-like UFO spotted by ten people hovering over Brasília-DF [2002]

*2020-06-11T21:30:37-03:00*

JAN 24th, 2002. Time: 9:35 pm. Duration: “constant”. In Gama Sul, in the city of Brasília, Federal District, ten people spotted an unidentified flying object in the shape of a red star. The UFO remained stationary and had a “dot” aspect from a distance, but when it moved it made zigzag movements. The witness claimed to have recorded the sighting in photos, but these records were not made available with the present file. The report also informs that the conditions of visibility during the event were described as good and that there were no reports of condensation trails.
