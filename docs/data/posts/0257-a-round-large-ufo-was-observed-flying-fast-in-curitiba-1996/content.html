INTERRELATED with cases 431 and 432
MARCH 26th, 1996. Time: 9:00 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. On a clear night in Curitiba, State of Paraná, an unidentified flying object was observed by three people moving from West to North of the city with “fast speed”. According to the witnesses, the UFO was round, large, red in color and it was “quite high” and “very far” from the observers. Also according to the witness who reported the sighting, it was carried out with the naked eye and with a clear sky.
