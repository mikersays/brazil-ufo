MARCH 8, 2001. Time: 5:32 pm. Two witnesses residing in Sobradinho, in the Federal District, spotted an unidentified flying object with a strong brightness and colors oscillating between blue and red. According to the witnesses, the UFO moved slowly from North to South, leaving no trace of condensation, without making noise or interfering with electrical and electronic devices. There were no records in photos or videos, but witnesses reported that the UFO was observed through binoculars under good visibility conditions.
