JAN 17th, 2001. Time: 9:00 pm. Duration: 2 nights in a row. A witness residing in Guará I, Distrito Federal, spotted a large, unidentified flying object in the shape of a balloon emitting several strong colors. According to the witness’s account, the UFO was stationary and remained this way most of the time. The sighting took place two nights in a row in Guará I. The UFO was seen with the naked eye in good visibility, and no condensation trails were observed.
