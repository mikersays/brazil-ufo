DEC 12th, 1997. Time: 8h13 pm. Duration: 50 seconds. Two people in the Champagnat neighborhood, Curitiba, Paraná, observed an oval, blue and yellow UFO “standing as if it were floating” over the western region of the city. According to the witnesses, the UFO aspect was “bigger than that of a star” being observed in the firmament, it was “similar to a fog with blue/yellow luminosity”. The unknown object hovered at a low altitude of about 70 meters, “more or less 27 floors” and the sighting took place at a place approximately 10 km away from where the observers were.
