# An oval zigzagging UFO was observed in Curitiba for 1 hour [1996]

*2020-06-28T21:37:23-03:00*

MARCH 26th, 1996. Time: 21h00. Duration: one hour. In Curitiba, State of Paraná, three people spotted an oval UFO, alternating silver, red and blue colors. According to the witnesses, the unknown object was small and zigzagged, moving at “very fast” speed. The witness who reported the sighting was unable to estimate the altitude of the sighted UFO, pointing out only that it was “quite high”. The event was observed with the naked eye and in “good weather”.
