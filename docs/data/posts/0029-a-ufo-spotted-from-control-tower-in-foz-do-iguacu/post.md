# A UFO spotted from Control Tower in Foz do Iguaçu [2013]

*2020-06-03T20:48:11-03:00*

DEC 3rd, 2013. At 8:53 pm, a military in the Control Tower at the Foz do Iguaçu Airport, Paraná, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object at an altitude of approximately 25,000ft and about 100NM [nautical miles] from the point where the eyewitness was. The UFO was heading the Southeast direction, and for thirty minutes, the object that had the appearance of “lights, apparently red dots”, according to the military, also presented itself as “lights with green dots or light blue”, in a movement that alternated acceleration and stop.
