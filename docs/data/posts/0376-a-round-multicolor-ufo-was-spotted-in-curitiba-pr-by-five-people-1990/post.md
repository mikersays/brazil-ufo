# A round multicolor UFO was spotted in Curitiba-PR by five people [1990]

*2020-07-08T17:40:02-03:00*

JULY 7th, 1990. Time: 19:10. Duration: 30 minutes. In Curitiba, State of Paraná, five people observed an unidentified flying object of small round shape with “two white lights, one red, and in the center one orange spinning light”. At the beginning of the sighting, the UFO was stationary, but then it moved to the Northeast, slowly at first and then accelerating. The witness reported that the UFO was approximately 2,000 meters high, and about 2,000 meters [horizontally] distant from observers. The unknown object was observed in good weather and visibility conditions. The sighting was carried out with the naked eye.
