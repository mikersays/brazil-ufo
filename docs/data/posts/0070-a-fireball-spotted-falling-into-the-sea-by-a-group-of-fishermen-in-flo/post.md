# A fireball spotted falling into the sea by a group of fishermen in Florianópolis [2004]

*2020-06-10T18:11:11-03:00*

MAY 18th, 2004. Time: 8:30 am. Duration: 01 minute. An unidentified flying object was seen in front of Praia do Campeche, city of Florianópolis, State of Santa Catarina, for approximately 1 minute. According to the witness, the UFO was a “yellow fireball” was “the size of a car” and was approximately 1 km away from the observer. In addition to the witness, several fishermen also spotted the object going on a North/South trajectory and “falling into the sea near Ilha do Campeche”.
