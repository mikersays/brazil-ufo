# A spherical UFO spotted flying over federal government buildings in Brasília-DF [2001]+

*2020-06-13T12:13:08-03:00*

MARCH 8, 2001. Time: 5:30 pm. Duration: 10 minutes. Two witnesses residing in Lago Sul, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, spotted a spherical white UFO flying slowly over the center of the Federal Capital, just above the Espalanada dos Ministérios and the Supreme Court of Justice building. According to the witnesses, the UFO moved slowly towards the South, leaving no trace of condensation, emitting no noise or not interfering with electrical and electronic devices. The UFO was observed with the naked eye under good visibility conditions, despite partly cloudy skies. NOTE: the ARX file 0.0.599 contains a similar report of sighting on the same day, in the same region and at the same time.
