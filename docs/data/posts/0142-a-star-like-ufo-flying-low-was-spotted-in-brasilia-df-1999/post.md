# A star-like UFO flying low was spotted in Brasília-DF [1999]

*2020-06-15T21:22:32-03:00*

DEC 23rd, 1999. Time: 5:30 am. Duration: 40 minutes. In the industrial areal of Brasília,  Federal District, a sighting of an unidentified flying white object, similar to a star and with the size of “half a car”, was reported. The UFO was stationary at an altitude of approximately 1,000m at a point about 45km from where the observer was according to his report. The weather conditions were favorable for observing the sky with the naked eye at the day and place specified in the witness report to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], who also told them that “The first sighting was a red light. Right after that, a bright it was white light that remained for 40 minutes to 1 hour.”
