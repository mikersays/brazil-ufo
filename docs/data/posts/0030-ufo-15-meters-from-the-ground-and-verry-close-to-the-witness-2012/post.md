# UFO 15 meters from the ground and very close to the witness [2012]

*2020-06-04T19:20:15-03:00*

OCT 8th, 2012. At about 3 pm in the Ipanema neighborhood, city of Porto Alegre, State of Rio Grande do Sul, an unidentified flying object of approximately 20 meters in size was sighted for 20 seconds by a witness who described it as having an elliptical form and silver color. The UFO was going straight in the Southeast direction at about 6km/h, and at a very low altitude, of only 15 meters from the ground, and also very close to the witness – about 20 meters from him and left a trail of white condensation.
