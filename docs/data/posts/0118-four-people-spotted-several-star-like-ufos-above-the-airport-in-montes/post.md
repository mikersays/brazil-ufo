# Four people spotted several star-like UFOs above the airport in Montes Claros [2001]

*2020-06-14T00:39:38-03:00*

JAN 3rd, 2001. Time: 9:30 pm. Duration: about 10 minutes. In Montes Claros, State of Minas Gerais, four witnesses observed several unidentified flying objects at an estimated altitude of 2,000 meters. The UFOs looked like red stars and were very close to each other, going down to the ground in vertical motion until they got close to the head 12 of the airstrip, in the northwest sector [of the Airport]. The objects remained stationary most of the time, but when they moved away, they did it at a high speed.
