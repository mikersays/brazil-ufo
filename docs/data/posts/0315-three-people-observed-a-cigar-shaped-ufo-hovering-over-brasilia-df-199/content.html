MAY 23rd, 1994. Time: 9:00 pm. Duration: more than 1 hour. In the Asa Norte region, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, three people observed, with the naked eye and with binoculars, the moves of an unidentified flying object “just above the clouds”. According to the witnesses, the UFO was shaped like “a pen” being first in the vertical position and then in the horizontal position. The unknown object had blue, red and yellow colors, and it was about 5 km away from the observers. The sighting took place in a sky with few clouds and, when He saw the UFO for the first time, the witness “thought it was a plane”, but he changed his mind because he realized “it was stopped for 30 minutes”.
