# A UFO was spotted by a pilot and the Control Tower in Recife-PE [1978]

*2020-07-17T12:27:02-03:00*

APRIL 2nd, 1978. Recife-PE. 

## Translation of the report:

“At approximately 00:10Z, a luminous object was seen by the two operators of this TWR [Recife Airport, Pernambuco] in the East sector of the Aerodrome. The plane PT-TYO (Boeing 727 from Transbrasil) which was starting the takeoff at that moment, was asked to make a left turn and approach the unidentified object. The PT-TYO spotted the object, but when it got closer to the sector, the object mysteriously disappeared without a trace.”
