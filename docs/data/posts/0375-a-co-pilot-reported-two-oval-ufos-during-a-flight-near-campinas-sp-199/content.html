JULY 21st, 1990. Time: 10:30 pm. Duration: 5 minutes. A co-pilot on flight VARIG 658 over the region of Campinas, State of São Paulo, reported the sighting of two oval UFOs at low speed. According to the five witnesses of the sighting aboard the plane, the two UFOs remained at an altitude of 16,000ft and constantly in the position between 3-hours and 4-hours compared to VARIG 658. The sighting was carried out with the naked eye and in good visibility conditions. Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The reporting witness occupied the position of co-pilot of VRG 658 at the time of the event”.
