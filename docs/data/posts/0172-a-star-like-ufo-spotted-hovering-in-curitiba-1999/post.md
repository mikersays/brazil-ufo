# A star-like UFO spotted hovering in Curitiba [1999]

*2020-06-18T17:20:14-03:00*

FEB 4th, 1999. Time: 8:00 pm. Duration: 2 minutes. Three people observed an unidentified flying object in the form of a white star in the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná. According to the witness who provided information to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the UFO remained stationary during most of the sighting and, thus, observed from a distance, it looked like a “common star” in the sky, positioned close to the horizon .
