# Four aircrafts detected and reported a bright light shaped UFO in Campinas [2015]

*2020-05-29T23:02:24-03:00*

JULY the 8th, 2015. “Around 00h50 AM, the São Paulo Approach Control supervisor [APP-SP] called informing that approximately four aircrafts had reported a UFO in the Northwest sector of Campinas [SP] (Radial 050 / 08NM). The aircraft were flying between levels FL140-FL170 [14.000 to 17.000] and the UFO was sighted between levels FL120-FL130 [12.000 to 13.000 feet] “, wrote the COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] to complete the case file . The object was descibed as a bright light and the weather conditions at that time were good. The report was given by a military from the APP-SP, and also states, according to the witnesses, that the visual contact lasted twenty minutes and its motion could be dectected by the TCAS (Traffic Collision Avoidance System) of the airplanes.
