# A flickering light chases an airliner with no radar detection near Natal [2006]

*2020-06-08T21:44:38-03:00*

MAY 28th, 2006. Time: 2:53 am. Duration: 12 minutes. The sky was clear when the flight VARIG2348 (from Recife, State of Pernambuco, to Natal, state of Rio Grande do Norte) crossed the FL120 [12,000ft] to FL040 [4.000ft] at a distance of 40NM [nautical miles] South of Natal. During a descent procedure, between 02:53 and 03:05, the pilot saw a variant light in his 3 o’clock position. The pilot reported that this UFO followed his aircraft, however, “for radar presentation, there was nothing, no other traffic in that sector”, as it was observed in the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report.
