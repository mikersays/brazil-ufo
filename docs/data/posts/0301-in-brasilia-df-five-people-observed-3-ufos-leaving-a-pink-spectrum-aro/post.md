# In Brasília-DF, five people observed 3 UFOs leaving a pink spectrum around them [1994]

*2020-07-01T17:43:08-03:00*

NOV 11th, 1994. 21:40. Duration: 30 minutes. In the Asa Norte region in Brasília, Distrito Federal, five people observed three large, luminous, white UFOs at a high altitude. The witness who reported the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] stated that “he was on the sidewalk of the store where she works, when she looked at the sky and saw something strange”. The three UFOs, according to the witnesses, moved in circular trajectories, being observed with the naked eye in a cloudy sky. The COMDABRA report also informs that they were: “3 bright lights, [with] the sky turning pink around these 3 lights. The phenomenon was observed by more people, who called COPM1 describing the same characteristics”.
