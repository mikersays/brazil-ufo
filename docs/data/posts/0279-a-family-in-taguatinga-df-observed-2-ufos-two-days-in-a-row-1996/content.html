JAN 31st (11h00) and FEB 1st (10h40), 1996. Duration: 10 minutes. Three people of the same family in the region of Taguatinga, Federal District, close to the Catholic College of Taguatinga, observed two UFOs in the shape of a star for two days in a row, emitting a silver color, about 3km high, and positioned “to the south of Brasília”. According to the witnesses’ reports, the two unidentified objects were flying slowly and separately without leaving a trace. They were moving in opposite directions between East and West in the city. The sighting of the UFOs by the three witnesses took place on a clear day and with the naked eye.
