AUG 29th, 1998. Time: 20h30 pm. Duration:  not determined. In the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, a person residing in the Xaxim neighborhood spotted an unidentified flying object in an elongated shape and gray color. The witness was not able to estimate the altitude or the speed of the UFO, which, according to her report, headed for the North direction coming from the South. The sighting was carried out on a clear night with good visibility, with no markings or trails of condensation left on the object’s trajectory.
