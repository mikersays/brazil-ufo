# A letter inquiring about the capture of strange beings in Varginha-MG [1996]

*2020-06-28T18:31:33-03:00*

Correspondence sent by the Ufological Group of Guarujá, State of São Paulo, to the commander of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], on April 24, 1996, by the Group’s president, Mr. Edison Boaventura Júnior, “inquiring about the case of the capture of strange creatures in Varginha, State of Minas Gerais.

“TO CONDABRA

A/C COMMANDER

São Paulo-SP

On June 1st, 1995, we took the initiative to write a letter of n. 95/113 (the copy of which is attached), but we have not received a written response to what we would like this Command to provide.

On the other hand, we started an excellent telephone relationship with Colonel Coelho and Colonel Ortiz, who are working on a project that aims to catalog and exchange with civilian groups about the UFO or UFO phenomenon.

As we agreed, I am sending you 3 SUPYSÁUA newsletters n. 37, 38 and 39. We hope you enjoy their content. We warn that the cases published in this journal have the purpose of reaching the public interest, in a clear, simple and objective way. The Bulletin is not very technical, as it would make reading massive and heavy, unlike our research, which is based on a methodology based on the physical reality that surrounds us.

On April 5th we were in Varginha-MG participating in an important meeting, which brought together several UFO groups and some military personnel. The main theme of the meeting revolved around the capture of strange “creatures”. Part of this episode has already been broadcast in newspapers and television, at local, national and international levels. According to some witnesses, the Fire Brigade, the Civil / Military Police and the Army would be involved in this plot.

Shortly after my return from Varginha, I contacted Cel. Coelho informing him that our group was in charge of preparing a letter to CONDABRA requesting his opinion on this case. Cel. Coelho advised us to send the letter directly to you. that would take the appropriate measures, clarifying the fact. We would like to know if there is a cover-up policy in this case or in facts related to the OVNI or UFO phenomenon, researched and cataloged by this Command.

We await your response and communicate that we are open to dialogue, and we are at your disposal for any information about our studies.

Greetings,

Edison Boaventura Júnior / President “
