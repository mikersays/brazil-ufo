# An oval spinning UFO spotted in Brasilía-DF [1999]

*2020-06-17T22:19:20-03:00*

MAY 29th, 1999. Time: 11:00 pm. Duration: 30 seconds. In the Asa Norte district, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, two people from the same family spotted an unidentified flying object with an oval shape, white color and rotating motion. From the perspective that the two witnesses had in relation to the position of the UFO, and its altitude, the object appeared to be “larger than the Moon” seen in the sky.

According to the testimony given to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] by one of the witnesses, it was observed that he: “He was on the balcony of the apartment when the object appeared. He called his wife who also observed the object, and it lasted more or less 30 seconds. “
