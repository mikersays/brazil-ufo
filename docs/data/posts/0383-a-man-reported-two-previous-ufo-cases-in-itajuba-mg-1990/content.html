Year: 1990.

*Report collected on July 27, 1997. At 19:35.

According to a resident of the Taguatinga Sul region, Distrito Federal, he spotted two unidentified flying objects, round in shape, white, at a speed “faster than a Boeing 737” and emitting a sound that sounded like “sonar noise. “. The sighting lasted about 1 minute and was made with the naked eye and clear sky, from the window of his apartment on the eighth floor of a building. The UFOs, according to the witness, were at a higher altitude than commercial planes and crossed in their trajectories “from south to north and from east to west”. According to additional notes on the back of the report made by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], there is information about another UFO event that occurred in 1985: “Spherical object; material not analyzed; diameter: 35 cm; weight: 13.580g. Itajubá-MG, on Sept/1/1985, at 10 pm, at Rua Andrade Santiago. “
