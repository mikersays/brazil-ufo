# A man spotted a white elongated UFO in Belo Horizonte-MG [1994]

*2020-07-01T15:49:01-03:00*

DEC 3, 1994. Time: 14:14. Duration: 1 minute. In the neighborhood of Nova Lima, in Belo Horizonte, State of Minas Gerais, a person spotted an unidentified flying object of elongated shape and white color, flying at high speed. According to the witness, the UFO was small and was at a high altitude moving in the South-North direction of the city. The sighting took place with the naked eye, on an afternoon of clear sky and good visibility. There were no photographs or footage of the event.
