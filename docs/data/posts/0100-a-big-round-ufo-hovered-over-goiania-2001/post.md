# A big round UFO hovered over Goiânia [2001]

*2020-06-12T19:50:34-03:00*

APRIL 28th, 2001. Time: 7:30 am. Duration: 40 seconds. A witness in the Vila Nova district, city of Goiânia, State of Goiás, saw a round orange UFO hovering about 20 km away. The object was, according to the witness, the size of 2 Boeing 737s and hovering in the eastern sector of the city. The witness made the sighting under good visibility conditions and filmed what happened, but the video record was not made available in this file.
