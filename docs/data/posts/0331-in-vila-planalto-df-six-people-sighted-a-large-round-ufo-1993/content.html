OCT 9th, 1993. Time: 20:00. Duration: 15 minutes. Six people in Vila Planalto, Brasília, Distrito Federal, observed for fifteen minutes a large, round UFO, with red, yellow and green colors, flying at low altitude and slow speed. According to the witnesses, the unknown object made a vertical trajectory south of Vila Planalto, and 2 km away from the observer. The sighting took place under good visibility conditions and was carried out with the naked eye
