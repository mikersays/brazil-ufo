JULY 21st, 1994. Time: 9:51 pm. Duration: Not determined. A couple in the region of Prado Velho, Curitiba, State of Paraná, reported a sighting of two unidentified flying objects in the shape of “bright white dots (like stars), one small and the other large”. According to the witnesses’ reports, as collected by the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the objects flew slowly and came to be at a distance of “6 meters in relation to the observer” and followed in a straight path. Also according to the report, the sighting occurred at a moment when one of the witnesses contacted the Air Force, and “had been observed for some time” before that. According to additional information in the COMDABRA’s report, “there was no radar detection” of the objects seen by the couple in Curitiba on that clear night.
