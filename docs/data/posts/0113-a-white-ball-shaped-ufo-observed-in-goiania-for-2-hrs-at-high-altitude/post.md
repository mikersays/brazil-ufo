# A white ball-shaped UFO observed in Goiânia for more than 2hrs [2001]

*2020-06-13T13:18:26-03:00*

MARCH 4th, 2001. Time: 6:30 pm. Duration: 2 hours and 30 minutes. A witness in the Celina Parque district, city of Goiânia, State of Goiás, saw an unidentified large white flying object, moving in a high altitude. The “ball-shaped” UFO performed, according to the witness’s account, a North / South trajectory. The witness made the sighting with the naked eye under good visibility conditions, without having reported any record of condensation trails.
