# Three aircrafts report the same UFO between Quixadá-CE and João Pessoa-PB [2014]

*2020-05-31T21:48:10-03:00*

JAN 24th, 2014. One big white UFO was sighted by three different flying aircrafts in two locations within 50 minutes.

FIRST SIGHT: at 09h10am, the aircraft of flight GLO 9109 from Brasília to Fortaleza, at 09h10am, in descent to the airport of Fortaleza, Ceará, reported by its TCAS (Traffic Collision Avoidance System) a fast object, in a zigzag movement, and at a dangerous low altitude of 300 ft, which forced the GLO 9109 pilot to make an evasive maneuver to avoid collision with the UFO.

SECOND SIGHT: at 9:15 am, the aircraft on flight ONE 6372 from Guarulhos to Fortaleza, at 9:15 am, descending to Fortaleza, Ceará, also reported the same occurrence on its TCAS and at an altitude of 350 ft.

THIRD SIGHT: at 09h53am, the PRXVA flight from Alagoas Aerodrome, at 09h53 am, to João Pessoa Airport, PB, reported a visual contact with the unidentified flying object, in a dangerous proximity of 300ft, and moving up at supersonic speed, according to the pilot’s report who was also forced to perform an evasive maneuver to avoid collision with the white UFO at 10.5000 ft.
