# 15 people observed a disc shaped UFO flying slow over Vila Planalto-DF [1994]

*2020-07-03T18:04:59-03:00*

MAY 10th, 1994. Time: 16:30. Duration: 1 hour. In Vila Planalto, Distrito Federal, fifteen people observed an unidentified flying object shaped like a disc, with blue, green and red colors. According to the witnesses, the UFO was flying at a very slow speed “almost stopping”, moving from side to side in a circular trajectory. According to the observers, the sighting was done with the naked eye by most people, but the witness who reported the sighting claimed to have used 50x [zoom] binoculars. The sky was clear and cloudless.
