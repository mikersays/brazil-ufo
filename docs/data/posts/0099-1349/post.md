# Several white dots spotted leaving large red trails over Rio de Janeiro [2001]

*2020-06-12T19:34:46-03:00*

MAY 2nd, 2001. Time: 9:50 pm. Duration: 40 minutes. Two witnesses in Leblon, city of Rio de Janeiro-RJ, saw several lights in the form of white dots, alternating slow movements and stopped at about 2 km. The UFOs were observed under good visibility conditions and, according to the witnesses, left large trails of red and luminous condensation. The objects were going south, towards the Jacarepaguá neighborhood. In the observations of the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report, the witnesses also informed that the objects were like “several red lights falling like drops from the main point (white).”
