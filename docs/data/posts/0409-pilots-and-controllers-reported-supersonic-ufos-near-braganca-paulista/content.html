MAY 27th, 1986. Time: 23:36. Duration: not determined. An aircraft pilot flying from São Paulo-SP to Varginha-MG, the TF 402, having taken off at 22:07, reported having seen a “bright light, in its 9-hour position” about 33NM from the city of Bragança Paulista-SP. At that same time, according to a report, the “ACC-BS [Brasília-DF] was capturing a primary “plot” with a speed of 813 kt [1,540 km / h] and a course of 208 degrees in that position”. Another aircraft, the flight “VARIG RG256A [SBGR / SBCF – São Paulo-Guarulhos Airport to Belo Horizonte-Confins Airport – FL330, DEP 23:18]” reported sightings in the same region, even asking for permission to divert of the route to avoid collision with the UFO. Complementary data: “Transcript of occurrence posted in the Chief Controller’s assistant book: At 11:36 pm RG 256 (GR / CF) reported to ACC (F8) that it was being accompanied by lights on the BGC / BHZ (UR1) section. Such a situation it lasted until 11:49 pm, without obtaining radar contact with those objects.” The ACC-BS did not capture anything on the radar, nor did the APP-BH. At 00:20 TWR-BH (Sct MA) reported me that he saw green, red and white lights, approximately over Carlos Prates city (near Belo Horizonte-MG]. […] At 00:03, FAB 2177 (laboratory aircraft) took off from SBBH [Aeroporto da Pampulha-BH-MG] and, when questioned by APP-BH, reported seeing 2 balloons. RG 256A, after Confins took off for SBBR as RG256B, nothing reporting on that stretch.”

Cf. ARQUIVO NACIONAL (Brasil). Sistema de Informações do Arquivo Nacional (SIAN). Fundo ‘Objeto Voador Não Identificado (OVNI)’. Código de Referência BR DFANBSB ARX.0.0.246. “Objetos voadores não identificados”. Brasília, DF. 1952 a 2015. Último acesso realizado em 27/05/2020.
