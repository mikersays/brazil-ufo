# A flight controller had visual contact with a UFO over Londrina Airport [2013]

*2020-06-01T21:18:41-03:00*

MAY 6th, 2013. A flight controller at the Londrina Airport, Paraná, from 6:36 pm to 6:41 pm, observed an unidentified flying object shaped like an amber yellow light, whose horizontal movement made him think it was an airplane. The witness reported that the object was headed south-east, but did not emit a sound or leave a trail or smoke behind.
