# Three star-like UFOs at high speed sighted in Irati-PR [1999]

*2020-06-18T15:06:11-03:00*

FEB 11th, 1999. Time: 06h00am. Duration: 5 minutes. In Irati, State of Paraná, on a clear day, four people saw three white UFOs, shaped like a star, flying at high speed and at an altitude of approximately 10,000m. According to the witnesses, the unidentified objects changed their trajectories during the time they were observed. Firstly, they were seen going from West to East. Then from North to South. And finally from East to West. The UFOs emitted a bright light and also varied the distances between them as they moved without leaving trails of condensation or smoke traces. There was no record on film or photos.
