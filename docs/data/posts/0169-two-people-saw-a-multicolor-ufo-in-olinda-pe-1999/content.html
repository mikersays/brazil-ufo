FEB 18th, 1999. Time: 00h30, Duration: 10 minutes. In Rio Doce region, in Olinda, State of Pernambuco, two unidentified flying objects were spotted by two people in a clear sky night. According to the observers, the UFO was large and shaped like a multicolored spotlight, like a prism. The flying object flew at slow speed and moved in a straight line towards the East.
