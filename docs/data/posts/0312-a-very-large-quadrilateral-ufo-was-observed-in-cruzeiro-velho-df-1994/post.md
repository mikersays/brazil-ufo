# A ‘very large quadrilateral’ UFO was observed in Cruzeiro Velho-DF [1994]

*2020-07-03T00:25:39-03:00*

JUN 25th, 1994. Time: 21:10. Duration: 30 minutes. In the Cruzeiro Velho region, Distrito Federal, two people observed an unidentified flying object at high altitude flying at a slow speed from the Northeast to Southeast direction. According to the witnesses, the UFO was shaped like a “very large quadrilateral” and had the “color of fire”. The sighting took place during a clear night and was performed with the naked eye. No filming or photographs were taken of the event.
