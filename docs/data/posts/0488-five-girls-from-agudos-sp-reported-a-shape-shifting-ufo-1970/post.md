# Five girls from Agudos-SP reported a shape-shifting UFO [1970]

*2020-07-19T18:49:03-03:00*

## Report from the Security Information Division [of the 4th Air Zone Command] to the Ministry of Aeronautics.

AUG 21st, 1970. Time: 23: 00hs. Five university students from Agudos-SP, who “used to travel every night to Bauru-SP” to attend classes at Faculdade Educacional de Bauru, saw “a strange light on the night of August 21st, 1970, at 23:00 hs.

The five students were traveling in the same car, and one of the girls in the back seat on the left side noticed a red light on Marechal Rondon highway, on the stretch “shortly after the YY Farm”. According to this witness, “the light was opaque, did not emit light rays, and had a biconvex shape”. The girl warned the other four in the car to see the object too. The driver then parked the car to observe. “They noticed with surprise that the light decreased in size and changed in shape, presenting itself as a small sphere and then growing, as if it were going to approach the vehicle”.

Naturally scared, they insisted that the driver should “put the car in motion”, but she “wanted to stay there, to see what that object was”. However, after the insistence of frightened colleagues, she decided to continue traveling with the car at a slow speed.

All of them noticed “that the light went parallel to the vehicle, keeping a certain distance and at a low altitude because [the girls] could see it without having to lift their heads”.

The moment they arrived at the entrance to the Agudos-SP, they “parked once more. According to witnesses,” the object has become very close thisa time. Its shape elongated, giving the impression to be now a rectangle. Terrified, they turn towards the road that leads to Agudos and the object followed, in parallel, the Marechal Rondon highway. Bel. O.G.”
