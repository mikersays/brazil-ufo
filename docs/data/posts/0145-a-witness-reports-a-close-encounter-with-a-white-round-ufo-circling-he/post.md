# A witness reports a white round UFO circling her house [1999]

*2020-06-15T23:10:09-03:00*

NOV 23rd, 1999. Time: 04h30 pm. Duration: 40 minutes. In Ceilândia Norte, Distrito Federal, three people spotted a UFO, and at least one of them had a close eye contact with the white round object just 200 meters away. The UFO was seen at an altitude of 4,000 meters with “jet” speed, but then it was reported traveling around the house of one of the witnesses who described it as having the size of a “bicycle wheel” when it got closer to her. The object headed north and it was observed under good conditions of visibility. The witness’ report to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also stated that:

“It was seen by a housewife about 200 meters from the object. It had a round shape and in the center there was a white and bright light, and around it was yellow, green and blue alternately. With that, two more people were called to verify what happened. “
