# A bright blue UFO seen in Curitiba [2003]

*2020-06-11T14:42:17-03:00*

FEB 27th, 2003. Time; 5 pm. Duration: 40 seconds. In the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, a witness in the neighborhood of Portão reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object in the shape of a bright blue star. The UFO was apparently stopped, but then was  seen flying towards the Northeast. The witness reported that the visibility conditions were good, without the presence of clouds, and that there was also no visible record of sound or trail emitted by the sighted object.
