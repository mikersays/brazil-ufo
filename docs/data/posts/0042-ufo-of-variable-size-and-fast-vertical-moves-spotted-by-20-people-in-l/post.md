# UFO of “variable size” and fast vertical moves spotted by 20 people in Londrina [2011]

*2020-06-05T21:28:49-03:00*

MAR 16th, 2011. Time: 11:00 am. For 90 minutes, on the Campus of the State University of Londrina, in the State of Paraná, about 20 people spotted a bright white UFO of “variable size”, which alternated vertical movements at high speed with stops along its trajectory. According to the witnesses, the visibility conditions were good and the object left no trails, tracks or displacement marks. There is information that the sighting was filmed, but the video file was not made available. Additional observations in the COMDABRA’s [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report state that this information was passed on by APP-LO Supervision [Londrina Approach Control] and the latter reported that “the sighting was approximately on Radial 300 to 7NM [nautical miles] from Londrina; other people also saw it “
