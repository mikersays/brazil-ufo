# A fast bluish green UFO spotted in Foz do Iguaçu-PR [1997]

*2020-06-24T19:46:12-03:00*

MARCH, 14th, 1997. Time: 19h30. Duration: 10 seconds. In Foz do Iguaçu, Paraná State, four people observed an unidentified flying object with a round shape and a bluish green color. According to the witness’s, the UFO flew at a “very fast” speed and was “above the clouds” and its appearance was that of being “the size of the Moon” when observed in the firmament. The witness was unable to inform the exact position in which the big object was sighted, also reporting that she observed the UFO with the naked eye and under a cloudy sky.
