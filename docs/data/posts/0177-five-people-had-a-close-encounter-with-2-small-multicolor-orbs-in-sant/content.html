NOV 21st, 1998. Time: 09h20 pm. Duration: 30 seconds. In Medianeira, a district of Santa Maria, State of Rio Grande do Sul, five people observed two small UFOs only 60m away from them. The two unidentified flying objects were round with green, red and yellow colors. The UFOs were like orbs with the size of a golf ball. According to the witnesses, the objects moved in a straight line, stopped at a distance of sixty meters from where the five people were, and then went south. The COMDABRA’s [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report also informs that : “Only one object was initially observed, and after a while, the second appeared away from the first and approached the other.”
