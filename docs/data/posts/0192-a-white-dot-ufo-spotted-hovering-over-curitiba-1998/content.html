MARCH 6th, 1998. Time: 6h10 am. In the Água Verde neighborhood, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, four people spotted an unidentified flying object that remained “practically stopped” during the entire time it was observed. The UFO was white and had the appearance of a “dot”. According to the four witnesses, the object was positioned in the East and it was seen moving West. According to the witnesses’ report, the sky was clear when the sighting happened. The report made by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], also contains the following information: “The object was observed by the building’s doorman and by 2 more neighbors who passed by at the time. The sighting was still ongoing during the reception of this data.”
