# Two beam-of-light UFOs spotted in Pelotas-RS [1996]

*2020-06-26T11:33:14-03:00*

NOV 30th, 1996. Time: 9:20 pm. Duration: 1 minute. “Around 9:20 pm, on the patio of his residence” in Pelotas, State of Rio Grande do Sul, two people watched with the naked eye, in a clear sky, two unidentified flying objects, 2m from each other, following a rectilinear trajectory in the West-East direction. The two UFOs “had no definite shape – [they were] a beam of light” the size of a very bright and small star. Their colors varied: one was yellow, the other blue and white, and their “speed: faster than a normal aircraft”. There were no reports of condensation trails, video recording or photographs taken of the sighting.
