APRIL 28th, 2001. Time: 10:00 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. In the Cabral district, city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, two witnesses spotted with the naked eye and also with the use of binoculars a yellow and red star-like UFO, in a rotating movement. The UFO was flying straight towards the Northeast. In the observations of the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] the witness “reported that the observed object revolved around its own axis and was also observed by his wife.”
