MARCH 23rd, 1996. Time: 17h10. Duration: 25 minutes. Four people in the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, from the window of an apartment, spotted a UFO at about 3 km altitude, and distant from the observers horizontally about 10km. According to  the witnesses, the unidentified flying object was round, and its color “varied from white to black”. The UFO flew at “very slow” speed and remained in the eastern sector of the city. The sighting was performed “with the naked eye and a clear sky”.
