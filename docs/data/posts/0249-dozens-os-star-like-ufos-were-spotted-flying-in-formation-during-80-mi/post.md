# Dozens os star-like UFOs were spotted flying in formation during 80 min in Planaltina-GO [1996]

*2020-06-28T17:59:35-03:00*

APRIL 28th, 1996. Time: 21h00. Duration: 80 minutes. In the region of Lagoa Formosa, in Planaltina, Goiás, three people observed several unidentified flying objects [according to the report, about 100 objects were spotted], going fast, “moving close to each other, always maintaining their relative position”. The UFOs had the “shape and intense brightness of stars” and were observed moving towards the city of Unaí [MG], south of the city of Planaltina [GO]. The sighting was made, according to information from the witness, with clear skies and with the naked eye. According to the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “It was informed by the observer that the objects were flying in a scalene trapezoid formation together”
