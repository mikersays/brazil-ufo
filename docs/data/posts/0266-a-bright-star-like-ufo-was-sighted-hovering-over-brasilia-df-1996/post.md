# A bright star-like UFO was sighted hovering over Brasília-DF [1996]

*2020-06-29T12:24:35-03:00*

MARCH 21st, 1996. Time: 19h51. Duration: 30 minutes. In the Plano Piloto region, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, two people followed the trajectory of an unidentified flying object in the form of “a star – but brighter” and  with a size “larger than a star”. According to the witness who reported the sighting, the UFO was at the “airplanes” altitude, but “stationary” amidst few clouds in a clear sky. It was also reported that the UFO moved “from the Pilot Plan to Guará” in the Distrito Federal.
