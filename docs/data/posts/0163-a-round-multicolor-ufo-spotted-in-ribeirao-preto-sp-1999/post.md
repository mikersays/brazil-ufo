# A round multicolor UFO spotted in Ribeirão Preto-SP [1999]

*2020-06-17T23:50:15-03:00*

APRIL, 5th, 1999. Time: 05h20 am. Duration: 40 minutes. A resident of Ribeirão Preto, State of São Paulo, reported seeing an unidentified flying object with a round shape and colors ranging from orange, red and blue. According to the witness, the object remained stationary most of the time, and from her position – in relation to the observed UFO and the object’s altitude – it seemed that the object was “the same size as the Moon”, as seen in the firmament. Additional observations in the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] state that it was: “Moon-sized object, positioned above it as if it formed a number” 8 “(eight). The sighting took place in the backyard of the witness’ house.”
