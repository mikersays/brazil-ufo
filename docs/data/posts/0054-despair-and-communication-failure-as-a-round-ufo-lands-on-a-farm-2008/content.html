JULY 28th, 2008. Time: 8:45 pm. For 25 minutes, the residents of a farm near the city of Camapuã, State of Mato Grosso do Sul, became desperate when they witnessed an unidentified flying object landing and then taking off just 1 km away from them. The UFO was round, emitted many lights and it was observed under good conditions of visibility and weather. The COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], also informs that a retired military from the Aeronautics “contacted OPO-CG [Operational Permanence Officer of Campo Grande Air Base, State of Mato Grosso do Sul] and informed him that the residents of a farm near the municipality of Camapuã [MS] had reported  to him the presence of a UFO landing and taking off in the vicinity of the farm. He also reported that the incident caused despair to the residents. Loss of communications (phone / cell phone) was also reported ” .
