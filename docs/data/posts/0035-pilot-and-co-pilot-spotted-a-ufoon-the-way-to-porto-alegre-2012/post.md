# Pilot and co-pilot spotted a UFO on the way to Porto Alegre [2012]

*2020-06-05T00:05:15-03:00*

MAR 25th, 2012. Flying from Antonio Carlos Jobim Airport in Rio de Janeiro-RJ to Salgado Filho Airport in Porto Alegre-RS, the pilot and the co-pilot of flight GLO 1949 spotted an unidentified flying object about 30 meters long in size, resembling a dark balloon and traveling at a speed of over 700km/h. Witnesses spotted the UFO from a distance of approximately 5 km. The trajectory of the object that was at an estimated altitude of 38,000ft went in the Northeast direction.
