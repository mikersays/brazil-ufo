MAR 11, 2007. Time: 9:00 pm. Duration: 01 hour. In the neighborhood of Bairro Alto, in the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, a witness reported the sighting of four unidentified oval flying objects, larger than an airplane and an opalescent aspect [varied colors in a milky aspect]. The UFOs, according to the witness, flew during one entire hour with slow speed at about 3 km of altitude, moving at random and making zigzags. No interference on electrical and telephony devices was reported, nor condensation trails seen. Additional observations in the report of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also informed that: “Four points of lights, in zigzag; remained close, with different orbits, for approximately 01 hour.”
