DEC 4th, 2014. At 03h00am, an aeronautical station operator at the Cascavel Aerodrome, Paraná, reported two sightings of the same round UFO of strong and intense orange color with approximately 30 cm in size, in the interval of ten minutes, traveling towards the aerodrome after making a left turn. The object moved in an East / West direction, with an approximate speed of 180km / h and 10 NM from Cascavel.
