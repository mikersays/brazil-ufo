# Photographs taken near the Paraguay River revealed 3 stationary UFOs [1980]

*2020-07-14T22:44:51-03:00*

DEC, 12th, 1980. Assunção, Paraguai / and Paranaguá, State of Paraná, Brazil. A man sent photos taken by a friend [see photos of case in ARX.0.0.202] when they were on a tour in Paraguay, at a location about 12 km from the capital Asunción. According to the man’s report, a resident of Paranaguá, State of Paraná, with his friend were waiting for a boat to take them to the indigenous territory of the Maku People and before going, they decided to gather people to take some pictures of the group. Some pictures were taken and when they were developed they noticed three unidentified flying objects.

Later that day, already in Maku territory, and with other tourists, a person from this other group (an American gentleman) noticed “three unidentified flying objects flying in formation and equidistant”, which according to the witness’s report, “appeared in the skies with amazing speed and extraordinary precision of flight, plunging in a parable over the indigenous territory “of the Maku. According to the report, only this man had noticed the three ships static in that place and then moved, because the other people were distracted talking to each other. The same man stated that, before seeing the three UFOs, he had the impression of being observed.
