DEC 12th, 2001. Time: 8:40 pm. Duration: 20 minutes. Two witnesses in Bairro Alto, Curitiba, State of Paraná, spotted an unidentified flying object shaped like a “horizontal yellow line”, of size “slightly larger than a star” from the perspective of witnesses’ observation. According to the report, the UFO remained stationary in the eastern sector of Curitiba and it was notably different: “The object stood out from the stars because it was more horizontal,” said one witness.
