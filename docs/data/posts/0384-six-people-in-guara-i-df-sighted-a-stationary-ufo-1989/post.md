# Six people in Guará I – DF sighted a stationary UFO [1989]

*2020-07-09T09:22:50-03:00*

OCT 30th, 1989. Time: 10:30 pm. Duration: 20 minutes. Six people observed an unidentified flying object in the region of Guará I, Federal District, hovering at about 200 meters high. According to the witnesses, the UFO was a “luminous ball, bigger than a football” with red, green and yellow colors. The object was positioned in the West and seemed to leave a greenish trail. The sighting was done with binoculars and in a clear sky.
