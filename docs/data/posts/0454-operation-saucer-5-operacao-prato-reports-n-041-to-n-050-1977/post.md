# OPERATION SAUCER #5 [Operação Prato]  reports n.041 to n.050 [1977]

*2020-07-16T13:57:58-03:00*

- 01. REG. n. 041

- 02. PLACE: Baía do Sol – Mosqueiro-PA

- 03. DATE / TIME: 22 Nov 77 – at 05:18 hours

- 04. DESCRIPTION: Light bodies (2)

- 05. COLOR: Matte yellow

- 06. SHAPE: Undefined (in sight)

- 07. APPARENT SIZE: 2 and 3 cm, respectively

- 08. MOVEMENT: Element A: Straight path. Element B: Irregular (curve)

- 09. SPEED: Low (200 km / h), sometimes with constant acceleration peaks.

- 10. ALTITUDE: 1.500 m (estimated)

- 11. DISTANCE: 1,500 m (estimated)

- 12. OBSERVER (S): 1S Q.AT MT FLÁVIO and the civilians UBIRATAN PINON FRIAS, Mrs. F.M. FREITAS COSTA and Ms. CELIA MONTENEGRO

- 13. NOTE: Direction of travel. Element A: South / North. Element B: South / Northeast. Element B when reaching the distance of 300m (spacing) cut the bow of B to the left. Then he returned to the Northeast (new bow cut).

- 14. ANNEX: Copy of a sketch and six photographs.

- 01. REG. n. 042

- 02. PLACE: Baia do Marajó – Air Route: Soure / Belém (CESSNA 206)

- 03. DATE / TIME: 23 Nov 77 – at C9: 30 hours

- 04. DESCRIPTION: Aeromobile (UFO)

- 05. COLOR: Light gray (metallic-matte)

- 06. SHAPE: Oval

- 07. APPARENT SIZE: 1, 50m

- 08 MOVEMENT: Straight path, in the Southeast / East direction

- 09. SPEED: Low (-100Km / h)

- 10. ALTITUDE: 30m (aircraft altitude: 70m)

- 11. DISTANCE: 3,000m (estimated)

- 12. OBSERVER (S): Mr. IVALDO VIEGAS PAKTOJA

- 13. NOTE: The rapporteur had taken off from an airfield near the city of Soure-PA; it moved about 4,000m away when it observed below its flight level an oval shaped UFO. He deviated his course to Marajó Island and resumed his course, continuing.

- 14. ANNEX: Copy of 01 (one) sketch.

- 01. REG. n. 043

- 02. PLACE: COLARES-PA

- 03. DATE / TIME: 23 NOV 77 – at 09:30 and 10:30 hours

- 04. DESCRIPTION: Aeromobile (UFO)

- 05. COLOR: Red,  metallico shine on top

- 06. SHAPE: Oval (cylindrical)

- 07. APPARENT SIZE : 1m x 0,50m diameter

- 09. SPEED: Large (bigger than a reaction plane)

- 10. ALTITUDE: 1,200m (estimated)

- 11.DISTANCE: 1,500m (estimated)

- 12. OBSERVER (S): Mr. ANTONIO FERREIRA

- 13. NOTE: The UFO moved undulating (like a leaf in the wind), stopped, rotated around itself and moved fast as if [it were] receiving impulses. After several evolutions, its speed increased even more, disappearing towards the East.
