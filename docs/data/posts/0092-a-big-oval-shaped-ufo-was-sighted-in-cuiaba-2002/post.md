# A big oval-shaped UFO was sighted in Cuiabá-MT [2002]

*2020-06-11T22:04:34-03:00*

JAN 6th, 2002. Time: 00h00. Duration: 10 minutes. A witness residing in the Centro Sul neighboorhood of Cuiabá, State of Mato Grosso, spotted an unidentified flying object, oval-shaped, about 50 meters in size and white. The UFO alternated movements and stops. The visibility conditions at the place and time were not satisfactory. The sky was covered with clouds. When the UFO moved, it reached an estimated speed of 200 km/h in motion, according to the witness. The UFO remained at an altitude of approximately 2 km, with a distance of 4km from the observer. In the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] the witness also informs that, for him, “the sight of the referred object was occasional, as he was curious about the sight”, and only after the observer confirmed “it was not an optical effect “, he said he “decided to registerthrough my amateur camera. I took 04 photos, but only 02 confirmed the vision.”
