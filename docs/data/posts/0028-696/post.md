# Three UFOs filmed moving at random [2013]

*2020-06-03T19:32:59-03:00*

OCT 6th, 2013. Around 1:44 am in the city of Santo Antônio da Patrulha, State of Rio Grande do Sul, a journalist spotted and recorded on video [see below] three white round UFO’s, apparently small in size and varying the speed as they moved at random, “crossing and grouping in a single focus”. The UFO’s were about 200 meters high and above a high ground, and at a distance of approximately 1,200 meters from the eyewitness. The report of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also informs that the sighting took place in conditions of visibility somewhat impaired by the existing fog at the time and place of the event. The video recording [in 2 parts] with a total duration of 7m31s was attached to the report and made available to the public.
