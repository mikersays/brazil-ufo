Interrelated case with ARX.0.0.506

DEC 26th, 1997. Time: 16h05pm. Duration: 2 hours. Five people observed several unidentified flying objects in the form of bright white dots, near the Bacacheri Airport in Curitiba, Paraná State. According to the witnesses, the UFOs were small and varied in speed as they moved “around” the direction of the Sun from the perspective of the observers. Also according to the witnesses of the sighting, the conditions of visibility at that time were good, with clear sky. They also claimed to have observed light-colored displacement trails left by the objects.
