APRIL 23rd, 1996. Time: 19h10. Duration: 20 minutes. When flying over the south coast of the State of São Paulo, a pilot and a co-pilot of the PT-321 aircraft noticed that two unidentified flying objects were following the plane from about 60 NM away. According to the witnesses, they were at an altitude of 16,000ft, when they saw “2 points of light that moved symmetrically” and in the “nine o’clock position in relation to the aircraft” [south]. One UFO had a strong red light and the other a weak white, and they moved at a speed of 250kt [462km/h]. The sighting was done with the naked eye and the visibility conditions were described as good.
