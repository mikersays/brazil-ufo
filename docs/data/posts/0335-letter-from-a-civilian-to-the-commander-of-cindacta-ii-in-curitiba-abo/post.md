# Letter from a civilian to the Commander of CINDACTA II in Curitiba about UFOs [1993]

*2020-07-06T00:05:39-03:00*

MARCH 15th, 1993.

“Letter from a civilian to the Commander of CINDACTA II in Curitiba, State of Paraná. 

Subject: Unidentified flying objects.

Curitiba, April 9, 1993.

To Cindacta II,

Av. Mayor Erasto Gaertner, 1000

Curitiba PR

Dear Mrs.:

On  [erased] at [erased] (dawn of [erased]), with a variation of no more than 00:05 more or less, I was in the north of [erased], in the region of Rio Macuco (Fazenda Macuco), with approximate coordinates of 50 18 30 W and 11 15 00 S, with four more comrades, when, from west to east, a luminous point passed, comparable to the largest visible “stars”. Maybe bigger. Appearing at a much lower altitude than any artificial satellite or artifact resulting from the space race and in orbit. It moved very fast. After 30 seconds, two aircrafts the size of a passenger/cargo Boeing passed, side by side, both in the same direction, north south, with no more than a few kilometers apart. Maybe 2 or 3, if that. Exactly over our heads, they cross the trajectory of the “previous object”. We could see the aircraft with surprising clarity, as they were fully illuminated, that is, with all the lights on, with the one on the right, immediately afterwards, turning off the lights, and returning to normal lighting. It was still very dark on the ground and the moon was almost in waning quarter (03/15/93). Then we lost sight of them because we were in a clearing inside the forest. The Fact itself may not be of greater significance, after a more consistent analysis, but, thinking it may be of some use, I leave this testimony.

Best regards,

O.E.K.

Curitiba-PR “
