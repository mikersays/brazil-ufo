# Three people saw a cigar-shaped UFO in Curitiba [1999]

*2020-06-17T00:13:21-03:00*

OCT 10th, 1999. Time: 00h30. Duration: 4 seconds. Three people in the Batel neighborhood in Curitiba, State of Paraná, spotted an unidentified flying object shaped like a cigar with 4 meters in size. According to witnesses, the UFO flew at a low altitude, 50m, and its color oscillated between gray and fluorescent green. The object was approximately 1km away from the observers, but close enough for them to perceive a vibration from the UFO. Soon after being noticed at low speed, the object proceeded very fast towards the South.
