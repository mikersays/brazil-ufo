APRIL 4th, 1989. Time: 17:15. Duration: 30 minutes. When he was “watching a parachuting exhibition” from the window of his apartment, a man with his family observed an unidentified flying object with a cylindrical shape, silver color and slow speed. According to the reporting witness, the object was about 2,000 meters of altitude, making vertical trajectories of approximately 20 meters. The sighting was carried out with the naked eye and under good observation conditions.

Cf. ARQUIVO NACIONAL (Brasil). Sistema de Informações do Arquivo Nacional (SIAN). Fundo ‘Objeto Voador Não Identificado (OVNI)’.
