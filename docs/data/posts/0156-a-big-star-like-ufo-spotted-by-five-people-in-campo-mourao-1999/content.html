JULY 7th, 1999. Time: 8:00 pm. In Campo Mourão, State of Paraná, five people observed a big star-like UFO, with colors varying between orange and yellow. According to the witnesses, the unidentified flying object remained stationary in the western direction for the entire period of  the sighting, about 40km away from the observers. The weather conditions were good and the communication to report the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] was made at the time of the event.
