# Bright white zigzagging UFO sighted in Manaus – AM [2015]

*2020-05-28T23:31:04-03:00*

SEPTEMBER 4th, 2015. From 11h25pm to 11h37pm at Ponta Pelada Airport in the city of Manaus, Amazonas, an air traffic controller and two military, one of them a FAB pilot [Brazilian Air Force], saw an unidentified flying object, which looked like a white bright light, zigzagging after being stationary for a while at about 3.000ft, positioned right at the takeoff extension area. According to the sargeant, also an air traffic controller  [the one who gave the report to COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], “that was not the first time this light was observed”. The witness also reported that the weather conditions were great that particular night, with clear sky.
