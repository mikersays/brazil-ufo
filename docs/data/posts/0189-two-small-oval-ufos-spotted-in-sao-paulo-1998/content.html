APRIL 30th, 1998. Time: 06h15 am. Duration: 30 seconds. A resident of the Interlagos region, in the city of São Paulo, SP, spotted two unidentified flying objects and managed to take photographs of them. According to the witness, the two UFOs were stationary, and were small, oval and white. The objects appeared to be “bigger than stars” from the perspective of those who observe them in the sky. The difference, besides the size, was the clear condensation trails left by it when it moved. The photographs taken by the witness were not made available in this file.
