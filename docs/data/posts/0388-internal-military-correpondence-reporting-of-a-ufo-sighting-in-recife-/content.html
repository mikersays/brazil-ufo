JUNE 4th, 1989. Recife, Pernambuco. Internal military correspondence communicating the reporting of a UFO sighting in that region.

“DEPARTMENT OF FLIGHT PROTECTION  OF RECIFE – AREA CONTROL CENTER

Recife, 06 June 89.

From: the Chief

To : Chief of DPV-RFA

Subject: Transcription of occurrence

I – I transcribe for the proper purposes, occurrence contained in the ACC Recife Record Book, on 06/04/89, shift 15: 00/21: 15 UTC.

II – It was reported by PT L317 SBSV / SBSP F350, the presence of a UFO, flying in the same direction, position two-hours, approximately at 05 (five) NM (nautical miles) on its side. After the traffic was confirmed as not known by the Center, more details were requested and the position was informed: 1509.1S / 04012.8 [erased] and that the UFO was approximately at the FL100 level; later, the pilot referred to the UFO tale “A STRONG MOBILE BLUE LIGHT”.
[…] 1TEN ESP CTA / Head of Air Traffic Section. “
