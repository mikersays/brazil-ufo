# A lamp-like UFO was observed by teachers and students in Taguatinga-DF [1992]

*2020-07-06T16:09:15-03:00*

NOV 18th, 1992. Time: 20:45. Duration: 30 minutes. “Teachers and students of the Centro Educacional de Candangolândia”, a school in Taguatinga, Distrito Federal, spotted an unidentified flying object, similar to “a street lamp”, with a “constant light” color. The UFO, according to the witnesses, remained stationary, at high altitude and in the western position in relation to observers who were in the Candangolândia region. The sighting was made with the naked eye and in rainy weather, “lightning and heavy clouds”. Complementary observation from the COMDABRA’s report: “The moon disappeared at 20:49.”
