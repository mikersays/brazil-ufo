# Two Air Force pilots reported a white light UFO flying above ‘Mach .8’ near Santa Maria-RS [1993]

*2020-07-04T19:10:02-03:00*

SEPT 16th, 1993. Time 20:44. Duration: 3 minutes. During a training mission of the “3rd / 10th GAv – Centauro Squadron”, [located at Santa Maria Air Base] for interception missions by the Brazilian Air Force Fighter Aviation, two pilots observed an unidentified flying object for 3 minutes. According to the pilots’ description, the UFO was a white light that moved at a speed “above Mach .8” [close to the speed of sound]. Also according to the witnesses, the UFO moved from North to South and “passed the aircraft vertically” at an altitude of FL400 [40,000 feet] and at a distance of 10,000 feet. Also according to the observations in the report made by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “During the mission, no evidence or primary plot was seen at the time the pilot informed about the UFO in flight”.
