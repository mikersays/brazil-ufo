# A family observed two big round UFOs from a car near Curitiba-PR [1995]

*2020-06-29T23:11:04-03:00*

SEPT 9th, 1995. Time: 18h50. Duration: 3 to 4 minutes. Four people of the same family observed two unidentified flying objects from “a car on the BR277 road, in the coast-Curitiba direction, in the Guatupê region, close to the Police Academy.” According to the witnesses, the UFOs were round “with yellow lights around them and a strong reddish light centered at the bottom”. The unknown objects were “initially stopped [distant 5km from each other], then they turned off the lights, went upwards, came back and disappeared in the eastern direction”, coming from the West, at an altitude of about 2,000 meters. The objects were between 30 and 40 meters in diameter, emitted a “strong sound, similar to that of an airplane” and were about 500 meters away from witnesses in the car. The sighting took place on a clear night and with the naked eye.
