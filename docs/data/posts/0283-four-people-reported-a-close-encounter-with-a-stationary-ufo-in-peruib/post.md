# Four people reported a close encounter with a stationary UFO in Peruíbe-SP [1995]

*2020-06-29T22:50:45-03:00*

OCT 14th, 1995. Time: 19h00. Duration: 30 minutes. On the beach in Peruíbe, on the south coast of the State of São Paulo, four people observed an unidentified flying object “coming out of the sea” about 100 meters away. According to the witnesses, the UFO was oval, white, appeared to be four centimeters at first and then increased in size “gradually”. The unknown object remained stationary during the sighting at about 100 meters high. The sighting was made with the naked eye, with cloudy sky. No photographs of the sighting were taken.
