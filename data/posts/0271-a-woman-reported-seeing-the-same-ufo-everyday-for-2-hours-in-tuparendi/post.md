# A woman reported seeing the same ufo everyday for 2 hours in Tuparendi-RS [1996]

*2020-06-29T15:02:21-03:00*

MARCH 5th, 1996. Time: 21h00 pm. “Everyday, during 2 hours”, in Tuparendi, State of Rio Grande do Sul, a woman says she observes a round multicolor UFO. According to the witness, the unidentified flying object, observed “from a distance, would be the size of a football”, changing colors, “from lilac to yellow and red”. The unknown object performs a vertical trajectory, “appearing high and descending slowly”. The sighting informed in this report was seen together with six more people, with the naked eye and it was also filmed, but the video was not made available.
