# Stationary UFO started to move when the plane approached [2012]

*2020-06-04T20:05:35-03:00*

JUL 18, 2012. Near Porto Trombetas Airport, city of Oriximiná, in the State of Pará, a pilot of an aircraft in plain flight spotted an unidentified flying object of amber color and undefined shape. It was apparently small and was at low speed and far from the eyewitness about 3 NM [nautical miles]. The pilot reported that, during the five minutes of observation under good visibility conditions, the object initially appeared to be stationary and that it began to move slowly as the plane approached, without leaving visible traces or causing interference on electronics, or even without being noticed by the TCAS (Traffic Collision Avoidance System) of your aircraft.
