OCT 14th, 1999. Time: 01:40pm. In Cabo de Santo Agostinho, State of Pernambuco, a witness spotted a shiny metallic UFO with a round shape, similar to the “new moon”. According to the witness, the unknown object was “very large” in size and flew horizontally at high speed [“jets speed”] going from the Southeast towards the ocean. Also according to the sighting reported to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witness stated: “It could be an object in the form of a plate maneuvering (as if standing on its side).”
