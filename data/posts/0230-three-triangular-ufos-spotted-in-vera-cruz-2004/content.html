This infomation let us deduce in which one of the Vera Cruz cities in Brazil [there are 5 in Brazil] the sighting took place.

DEC 19th, 2004. 1:00 am. Duration: 2 minutes. In the city of Vera Cruz, *State of Rio Grande do Sul*, on a clear night, three people observed 3 unidentified flying objects shaped like triangles and white in color. According to the witnesses of the sighting, the UFOs were flying at “very fast” speed and were following a straight path in a North / South direction. According to the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] the witnesses also stated that: “The objects formed a triangle with equal sides and moved together. The individual reports that the lights stopped and moved together.”
