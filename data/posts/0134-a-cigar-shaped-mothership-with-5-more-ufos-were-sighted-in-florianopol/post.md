# A cigar-shaped mothership with 5 more UFOs were sighted in Florianópolis [2000]

*2020-06-14T23:57:39-03:00*

MAY 7th, 2000. Time: 11:40 pm. Duration: 10 minutes. In Florianópolis, State of Santa Catarina, six observers in the city center spotted six unidentified flying objects in yellow and white colors, and ellipsoid/cigar shapes. According to the witnesses, the UFOs were “the size of a Boeing”, moving “very fast” at an altitude of approximately 9,000 meters, and heading North to South without leaving any trace of condensation. There were no reports of sightings recorded in photos or videos.

“There were two apparitions, the first time two discoid objects were seen with bright light that varied from yellow to white, describing an” S “shaped trajectory. Soon after performing this maneuver very quickly, the objects gathered in rectilinear motion and disappeared.

After a few seconds, four more objects appeared, moving in a straight line in a “V” formation. During this displacement, two smaller objects moved away and approached the larger cigar-shaped object “.
