# Two round UFOs spotted hovering over Sobradinho I-DF [1999]

*2020-06-18T09:31:10-03:00*

MARCH 19th, 1999. Time: 16h15, Duration: 3 minutes. In Sobradinho I, Distrito Federal, two unidentified flying objects were observed by two people under good visibility conditions. According to the witnesses, the two round UFOs were red and silver, and were stationary during the whole sighting. They took no photographs and reported no traces of condensation from the observed objects.
