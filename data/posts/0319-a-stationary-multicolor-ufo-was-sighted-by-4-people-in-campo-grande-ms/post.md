# A stationary multicolor UFO was sighted by 4 people in Campo Grande-MS [1994]

*2020-07-03T23:12:49-03:00*

MAY 1st, 1994. Time: 23:40. Duration: 1 hour. Four people in Campo Grande, State of Mato Grosso do Sul, spotted an unidentified flying object that was “stationary”, and had “color variations”. The UFO was shaped like a “small luminous point, barely larger than a normal star” and with red, green and orange colors. According to the witnesses, the object was observed with binoculars and it was not possible to accurately estimate the distance between the observers and the object that was in the Southwest of Campo Grande. Also according to the observations in the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The UFO was first observed by CINDACTA and then reported to the DSV-CG. The [military in charge] reported that a similar event was observed two weeks ago. The bright spot flickered all the time. “
