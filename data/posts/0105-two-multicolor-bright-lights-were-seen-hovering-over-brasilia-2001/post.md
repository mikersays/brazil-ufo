# Two multicolor bright lights were seen hovering over Brasília [2001]**

*2020-06-12T22:54:24-03:00*

MARCH 25th, 2001. Time: 04h09pm. Duration: 35 seconds. In Brasília, Distrito Federal, four (4) witnesses spotted two (2) unidentified flying objects looking like multicolor lights. The UFOs hovered above the southeastern sector of Brasília and about 2 km apart from each other. The bright objects were observed under good visibility conditions and no photos or videos were made. There were no reports of trails or condensation marks left by the UFOs in their trajectory either.
