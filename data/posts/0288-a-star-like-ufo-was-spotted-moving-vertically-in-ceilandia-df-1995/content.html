MAY 16th, 1995. Time: 20h05. Duration: not determined. In Ceilandia, Distrito Federal, several people spotted an unidentified flying object with a round shape, red in color and appeared to be “the size of the Morning Star” when seen in the sky. According to witnesses, the UFO was moving vertically, descending slowly from a high altitude. The sighting was carried out with binoculars in a clear sky.
