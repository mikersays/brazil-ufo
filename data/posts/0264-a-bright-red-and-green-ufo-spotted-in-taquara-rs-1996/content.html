MARCH 21st, 1996. Time: 01h30 am. Duration: 90 minutes. In the region of Taquara, State of Rio Grande do Sul, three people spotted an unidentified flying object in the shape of a bright star, in red and green colors. According to the witnesses’ report, the UFO flew at “very slow” speed, following a straight path, when it was observed “in the opposite direction from the East”, that is, in the West direction. The witnesses saw the UFO with the naked eye and in a clear sky, and they were unable to estimate the altitude of the unknown object.
