JAN 17th, 2005. Time: 9:00 pm. Duration: 5 minutes. At a distance of 50NM [nautical miles] southwest of Curitiba with flight VRG8638, the pilot of the aircraft spotted three unidentified flying objects in an indefinite and white color, which, under a clear sky with good visibility, seemed stationary in an estimated altitude above 36,000ft. The observations made in the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report describe the details given by the pilot in reference to the case: “Informed by the pilot of the VRG8638 that, on his left, he saw 3 white lights that kept flashing every 10 seconds for about five minutes. There was no aircraft traffic nearby. The VRG was on the FL360 and saw the lights above him.”
