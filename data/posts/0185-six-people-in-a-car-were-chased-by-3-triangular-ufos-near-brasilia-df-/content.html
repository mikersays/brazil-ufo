JUNE 5th, 1998. Time: 23h15 pm. Duration: 2 hours. When they were between kilometers 17 and 19 of highway BR040, near Brasília, Distrito Federal, six people in a car noticed that they were being followed by three triangular gray UFOs, about 700 meters away. The unidentified flying objects emitted green and red lights and started to follow the car’s speed of approximately 100km/h. According to the witnesses, the objects were about 3 meters long and were at an altitude of 5 meters in relation to the ground, keeping an 8-meter distance from each other.

The following complementary observations were made in the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] informing the sighting details: “The vehicle stopped and the objects stopped next to the vehicle. According to the declarant, the objects still remained close to her residence. This at the time of this report (01: 31z)” i.e. This report was communicated to COMDABRA at the same time the sighting was happening.
