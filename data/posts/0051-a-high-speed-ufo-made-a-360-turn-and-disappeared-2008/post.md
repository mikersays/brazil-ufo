# A high speed UFO made a 360° turn and disappeared [2008]

*2020-06-07T13:29:07-03:00*

DEC 18th, 2008. TIME: 9:00 pm. Duration: 30 seconds. A flight attendant, off duty, spotted a rond unidentified flying object with intense white color [among other undefined colors] from its residence in the city of Florianópolis, State of Santa Catarina. The UFO was flying at an altitude just below 30,000ft. According to the witness, the unknown object was traveling at a high speed towards the direction of the observer, when it took a 360° degree turn and suddenly disappeared. The report also informs that the object left abnormal trails of light color when it moved, first from West to East and then from South to North.°
