# A huge crab-like UFO hovered at a low altitude in Curitiba [2000]

*2020-06-14T22:23:49-03:00*

JULY 2nd, 2000. Time: 6:00 am. Duration: 20 seconds. On the Alexandra-Mainhos Road, when he was returning from his duty at the Caoibá Hospital, a doctor spotted an unidentified red flying object “the size of a transatlantic” similar to a “crab” with “claws” and at very high speed. The UFO passed only 400 meters from the observer when flying at a low altitude [of 300 meters] towards the western sector of Caiobá. The sighting occurred under good visibility conditions

The information provided to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also shows that:

“The observer perceived the approach of a light coming from the Serra do Mar. When it got closer, the light proved to be a huge object and, for a few moments, it was stationary in a position of 90° from the observer, moving then at a very high speed towards the coast. According to the observer, the object was very large, bigger than a Boeing 747, and emitted a reddish light and from which hung claws, like those of a crab. The sighting occurred when he returned from duty at the Hospital Caiobá. “
