# A round multicolor UFO was seen flying slow in Santa Cruz do Sul-RS [1996]

*2020-06-29T18:11:40-03:00*

MARCH 20th, 1996. Time: 19h30. Duration: ongoing during the report. On a night of clear sky and good visibility, an entire neighborhood in the city of Santa Cruz do Sul, State of Rio Grande do Sul, observed a UFO with a round shape, “three times the size of a star” as seen in the sky. According to the witnesses, the unidentified flying object was multicolored and flew with a slow speed and at a high altitude. The UFO was sighted in the western direction of the city, following a straight path parallel to the horizon.
