# Several UFOs reported by 4 cities in São Paulo in the same night [1982]

*2020-07-14T14:55:55-03:00*

MARCH 6th, 1982. Time: 9:15 pm. A military policeman from the city of Marília, State of São Paulo, communicated via telegram to CINDACTA that at 9:15 pm on March 6, 1982 that unidentified flying objects had been observed in the cities of Presidente Prudente, Regente Feijó, Rancharia, and finally in the city of Marília, all in São Paulo state. According to the reporting witness, the UFOs moved quickly, emitting beams of light, without making any noise. After flying over the first three cities mentioned above, the UFOs moved towards Marília, having been observed by several military policemen in all of the cities mentioned.
