# A man in Goiânia-GO reported a close encounter with a UFO and 2 beings in his backyard [1990]”

*2020-07-08T16:10:55-03:00*

OCT 30th, 1990. Time: 15:40. Duration: 30 minutes. A resident of Goiânia, State of Goiás, reported having had two UFO experiences between October 1989 and October 1990. In the second, the witness claimed to be “3 meters” away from a UFO that landed in the backyard of his house. According to the witness, the unidentified flying object that “approached from the North” in a straight line, had a rectangular shape, size 5×3 [15 mt2], brown color and slow speed. The witness also stated that there were two beings with the “disk”, which “destroyed the flowers in the garden; they stained and left an unidentified substance”. The UFO made a “tin-like noise” and “left two stones with radiation”; “two stones and rust marks on the plants and white spots”.
