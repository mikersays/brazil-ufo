# Five oval UFOs sighted over São Paulo-SP for an entire hour [2014]

*2020-05-31T15:53:31-03:00*

DEC 20th, 2014. For an entire hour, from 11:00 pm to 12:00 am, in the Cambuci neighborhood, city of São Paulo-SP, two people observed five (5) unidentified flying objects with oval shape and in red, white and blue lights that varied in intensity. The objects were the size of an airliner, but faster than this aeroplane, keeping the approximate distance of 1 km between themselves. The witnesses who reported the event to the representative of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], stated that the objects sighted were at a distance of approximately 20 km from the point where they were, moving along the same trajectory, and alternating with ascents and descents, in the south/north direction. The witness also reported the use of non-professional binoculars in the observation of unidentified flying objects.
