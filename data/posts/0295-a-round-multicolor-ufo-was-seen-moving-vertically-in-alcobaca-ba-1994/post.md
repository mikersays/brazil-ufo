# A round multicolor UFO was seen moving vertically in Alcobaça-BA [1994]

*2020-06-30T23:40:40-03:00*

DEC 14th, 1994. Time: 01:15 am. Duration: 20 minutes. In the city of Alcobaça, in the south of Bahia, two people observed a round UFO “bigger than a star”, varying in colors and speed. According to the witness who reported the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the UFO was in the East, about 39km from the nearby city of Caravelas, in a restricted area of ​​the Brazilian Navy, and it was seen performing vertical maneuvers.
