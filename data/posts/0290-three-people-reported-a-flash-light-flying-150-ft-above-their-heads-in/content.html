APRIL 2nd, 1995. Time: 21h55. Duration: 3 seconds. Three people in the Bacacheri region, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, observed “a flash” about 50 meters above them. According to the description given by the witness, the object had no defined shape or size. The witness was only able to inform that the flash of light passed over them in a straight path towards the west. According to the report, the sky was clear and starry when the sighting took place in that region.
