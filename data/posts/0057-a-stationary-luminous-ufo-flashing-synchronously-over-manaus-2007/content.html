SEPT 24, 2007. Time: 7:00 pm. Duration: 60 seconds. A resident of Parque Dez, in the city of Manaus, State of Amazonas, contacted the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] to report the sighting of a flashing white UFO, apparently stationary in the sky. According to the witness, the weather conditions and visibility were good. In the official report of the sighting, the following observations were made by the UFO observer: “The object presented itself without movement, seemed to stand still and emitted white light flashing with certain synchronism in the unknown interval with a total duration of the event of about 50 seconds. After this interval, it simply disappeared into the sky. The informant, according to his own knowledge, tried using the google earth software to estimate the geographic position of the apparition according to the following data: 030439S 0600009W “
