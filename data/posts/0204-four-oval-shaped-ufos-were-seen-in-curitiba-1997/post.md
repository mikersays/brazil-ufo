# Four oval shaped UFOs were seen in Curitiba [1997]

*2020-06-23T11:20:45-03:00*

AUG 27th, 1997. Time: 14h30 pm. Duration: more than 16 minutes. In the Pinheirinho region, Curitiba, State of Paraná, a person observed four oval shaped, very bright blue and white UFOs above her residence. According to the witness, the unknown objects moved very slowly at a high altitude and in a “straight path”. According to the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witness called to report the sighting while it was still happening.
