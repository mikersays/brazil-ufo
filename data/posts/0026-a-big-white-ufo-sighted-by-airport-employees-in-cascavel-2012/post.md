# A big white UFO sighted by airport employees in Cascavel [2012]

*2020-06-02T11:03:44-03:00*

DEC 24th, 2012. At 04h10am, when there was no aircraft traffic according to the knowledge of the ACC-CW [Curitiba Area Control Center], an aeronautical station operator and other employees at Cascavel Airport, Paraná, had visual contact with an unidentified circular flying object of bright white color, larger than a big aircraft. The object was observed during a period of two minutes and under good visibility conditions. According to the report of the aeronautical station operator, the UFO was sighted 7 km southwest of the airport, and was traveling at approximately 700 km / h, at an altitude of 20,000 feet and heading the west direction.
