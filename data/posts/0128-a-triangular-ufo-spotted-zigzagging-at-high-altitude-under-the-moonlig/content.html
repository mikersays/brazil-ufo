JULY 30th, 2000. Time: 9:55 pm. Duration: 30 seconds. Under great conditions of visibility in the Santa Cândida district, Curitiba, State of Paraná, two people observed a triangular white UFO heading South. The unidentified flying object was spotted zigzagging at high altitude when the witnesses were observing the Moon. The UFO didn’t make any noise and left no condensation trails. There were no video records or photographs.
