FEB 15th, 1991. Time: 12:20. Duration: 15 minutes. In Osasco, State of São Paulo, five people spotted an unidentified flying object at about 1,500m altitude in the southeastern direction. The UFO was round, with an intense red color and carried out vertical and horizontal trajectories. The object was sighted at approximately 3km from the witnesses, under a partially cloudy sky and with the naked eye. Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The fact was repeated hours later (hours after the first event in the same region) emitted a green color together with the aforementioned red.”

Cf. ARQUIVO NACIONAL (Brasil). Sistema de Informações do Arquivo Nacional (SIAN). Fundo ‘Objeto Voador Não Identificado (OVNI)’.
