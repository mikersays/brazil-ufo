Translation of the reported case and delivery receipt:

“Mr. V.B., a lawyer, resident at  […] n. […], Tatuapé-SP-SP, which delivered, upon receipt, a KODAK Super 8mm color film, with a projection duration of approximately 4 minutes, stating that it was filmed from the street in front of the aforementioned residence by his brother P.A.B., 53 years sales manager. The footage is, according to Mr. V., of a strange object that hovered in the sky, at 02:30 am on December 30, 1978, and there may be a possibility of being a UFO. The machine used was a SUPER 8mm, with a Zoom lens with a magnification capacity of 8 times. The assignment of the film was made for research and is a copy, since the original is in the possession of Mr. P.”
