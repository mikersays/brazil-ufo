# A stationary UFO spotted in Recife-PE [1972]

*2020-07-18T22:39:58-03:00*

NOV 31st, 1972. Translation of a telegraphed message sent from a Police Department in Recife-PE to the Ministry of Aeronautics.

## “I inform that from 02:00 am to 04:00 am in the morning, during a party at America Club in this city [Recife], people of credibility […] and many other people saw a strange object in an ovoid shape, luminous, which stayed stationary in space for a long time, quickly disappearing. Greetings. / JMC 1TEN – D.P./ “
