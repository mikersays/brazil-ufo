JAN 6th, 1993. Time: 22:10. Duration: 30 minutes. A witness in the Ceilândia Sul region, Distrito Federal, reported the sighting of two oval UFOs: one dark red and the other yellowish white. The two unknown objects were initially seen hovering, but then they moved, oscillating their speeds and keeping “some distance” from each other. The two UFOs flew at an altitude of about 1,500 meters and were observed [horizontally] from approximately 3 km. According to the reporting witness, the sighting was carried out together with 15 more people, with the naked eye and in a partially cloudy sky.
