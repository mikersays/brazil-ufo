# Two people sighted a star-like UFO zigzagging in Gioânia-GO

*2020-07-01T17:58:11-03:00*

NOV 11th, 1994. Time: 05:30. Duration: seconds. A person in Goiânia, State of Goiás, reported to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] an unidentified flying object that she had “observed ascending and zigzagging at 5:30 am, in the Eastern position, towards the North in reference to the observer, as if it were a star taking an oval shape”. Also according to the witness’ report, she had no way of “specifying the distance” from her to the object nor the altitude. The shiny silver-colored oval shaped UFO moved very quickly. The sighting was accompanied by one more person, and was carried out with good visibility conditions and tih the naked eye.
