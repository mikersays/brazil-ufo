JUNE 6th, 2000. Time: 11:50 pm. Duration: 7 minutes. On a clear night in the center of Colombo, State of Paraná, three people observed an unidentified flying object in the shape of a “crescent moon” and orange color hovering “just above the horizon”. The UFO emitted a very clear orange light, but this light “decreased in intensity as if smoke was involved in the object”. The witness who informed the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also reported having some knowledge about Ufology.
