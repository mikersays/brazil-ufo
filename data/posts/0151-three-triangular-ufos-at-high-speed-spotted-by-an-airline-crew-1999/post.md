# Three triangular UFOs at high speed spotted by an airline crew [1999]*

*2020-06-17T14:18:48-03:00*

*Interrelated case with ARX.0.0.559*

SEPT 25, 1999. Time: 1:45 am. Duration: 3-5 minutes. A pilot, a co-pilot and a flight attendant on flight TAM 809, en route from Fortaleza to São Paulo, at an altitude of 35,000ft, spotted 3 triangular UFOs, emitting a clear light at about 211NM [nautical miles] from SCR [Santa Cruz Air Base, in Rio de Janeiro-RJ] in bow 225. The unidentified objects were at an altitude higher than the TAM plane, about 70,000ft, and traveling “approximately 200° course”, at a speed described by witnesses as “faster than TAM 809”. Visibility conditions during sighting were described as “great, clear sky, moon, good ground visibility”.

To the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the flight attendant also made the following statements: “Objects in a somewhat inflated diamond formation. (Concerned about possible leakage to the press)”
