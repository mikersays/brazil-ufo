# Fifteen round UFOs spotted zigzagging in Curitiba [1998]

*2020-06-19T18:47:10-03:00*

** JUNE 30th, 1998. Time: 8:20 pm – Date and time of the communication of the sighting made to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command].

In the Fazendinha neighborhood in Curitiba, State of Paraná, ten (10) people spotted (15) unidentified flying objects amid a rain that fell in the region during the event. The 15 UFOs were round in shape, with red and orange colors and in different sizes, some large and others small. According to the witnesses, the objects flew at an altitude of 10,000ft, at high speed and zigzagged, before going South. Photographs were taken but were not made available.
