DEC 13th, 1994. Time: 04:00 am. Duration: not determined. From the window of her apartment in Asa Sul de Brasília, Distrito Federal, a woman with four other people observed, with the naked eye, an unidentified flying object with a round shape and silver color, flying at low speed. According to witnesses, the UFO was positioned right where “the Sun [normally is] at 08:00am” performing vertical and horizontal trajectories.
