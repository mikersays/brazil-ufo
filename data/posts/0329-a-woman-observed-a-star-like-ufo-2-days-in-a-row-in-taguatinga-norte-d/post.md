# A woman observed a star-like UFO 2 days in a row in Taguatinga Norte-DF [1994]

*2020-07-04T17:33:22-03:00*

JAN 8th and JAN 9th, 1994. 11:05 am. Duration: 7 hours in the 1st day. A woman from Taguatinga Norte, Distrito Federal, reported that she and four other people spotted unidentified flying objects for two in a row. One UFO on the 8th of January and two UFOs on the 9th of January. According to the description made by the reporting witness, the objects approached in the position where the sun is seen, and their shapes had the appearance of a bright star. The witnesses were unable to estimate the altitude, the distance or the size of the objects sighted. The sighting took place in good visibility.
