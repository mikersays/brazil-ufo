OCTOBER 8th, 2011. From the balcony of his residence, in the Boa Viagem neighborhood, city of Recife, State of Pernambuco, an aircraft pilot spotted an unidentified flying object with intense light, moving above the Boa Viagem beach, from 5:25pm to 5:30pm, and performing an unusual maneuver. The witness promptly reported the sighting to the ACC-Recife [Recife-PE Area Control Center] which registered it.
According to the document filled by COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witness: “REPORTED THAT HE SAW A STRONG LIGHT IN A PARALLEL ROUTE TO THE BOA VIAGEM BEACH IN THE NORTH DIRECTION, APPARENTLY AS FAST AS A COMMON AIRCRAFT. HOWEVER, AFTER APPROACHING AVENIDA BOA VIAGEM, 4520 (NEXT TO THE CASTLE) TURNED RIGHT AT A HIGH SPEED AND MAINTAINED ITS INTENSE LIGHT UNTIL IT SINKED IN THE HORIZON. “

Cf. ARQUIVO NACIONAL (Brasil). Sistema de Informações do Arquivo Nacional (SIAN). Fundo ‘Objeto Voador Não Identificado (OVNI)’.
