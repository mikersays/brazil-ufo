MARCH 23rd, 2001. Time: 16h00pm. Duration: 2 hours. At an elevation in the city of Paracatu, Minas Gerais, several people spotted a round shiny white UFO hovering in the western sector of the city. According to the witnesses, the brightly lit UFO was at low altitude, “very close” to the witnesses and remained stationary for two hours, being observed in clear skies under good visibility conditions. The witnesses also informed COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] that “the city radio is announcing the UFO observation to everyone”.
