# A round gray and red UFO hovered at low altitude in Curitiba [2001]

*2020-06-13T10:47:22-03:00*

MARCH 11th, 2001. Time: 19h15pm. Duration: 15 minutes. In the neighborhood of Guabirotuba, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, a witness spotted a round gray and red UFO, hovering at about 45 meters of altitude. According to the sighting report, the object remained stationary during the fifteen minutes it was sighted, leaving no trace of condensation. The reported event was observed under good visibility conditions, in a cloudless sky. There was no record of electrical and electronic interference during the sighting.
