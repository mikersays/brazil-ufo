OCT 23rd, 1992. Time: 02:06 am. Duration: 2 hours. A student from Curitiba, State of Paraná, observed an unidentified flying object appearing “among the clouds” and going back and forth in its straight trajectory. According to the witness, the UFO was spherical, “helicopter-sized”, “yellow and red, in circular motion”. The unknown object moved at high speed at an altitude estimated at 3 km South of Curitiba. The sighting occurred in a foggy and cloudy sky , and carried out with the naked eye. Complementary observation of the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report: “The informant contradicted himself several times. First he said that he was called José to SGT Cândido. The object was not seen in the PPI. He provided the telephone number of his father’s boss.”
