APRIL 28th, 2001. Time: 5:00 am. Duration: 20 seconds. In the Bairro Alto district, Curitiba, State of Paraná, four (4) people saw a bright round UFO “five hundred times bigger than a star”. The UFO was standing still at an altitude “lower than stars and satellites”. The sighting took place under good visibility and with the naked eye. No condensation trails have been reported. Other observations from the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] inform that witnesses considered the UFO “completely different from stars, satellites or airplanes, according to the observer who gave the report.”
