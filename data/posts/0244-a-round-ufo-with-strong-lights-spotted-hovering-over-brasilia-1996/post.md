# A round UFO with strong lights spotted hovering over Brasília [1996]

*2020-06-28T11:29:21-03:00*

MAY 25th, 1996. Time: 18h50. Duration: 40 minutes. Six people from the window of an apartment in Brasilia, Distrito Federal, observed an unidentified flying object “the size of an airplane”, with a “round shape and strong white lights. According to one of the witnesses who reported to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the UFO remained stationary at an altitude of approximately 5km, and it was observed from a distance [horizontal] of about 15km. Also according to the witness, the UFO remained in the southern sector of the city and then moved in a straight path. The sighting took place with the naked eye and was filmed and photographed, however the photos and videos were not made available in this file.
