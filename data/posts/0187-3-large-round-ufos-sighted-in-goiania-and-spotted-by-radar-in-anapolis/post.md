# 3 large round UFOs sighted in Goiânia and spotted by radar in Anápolis [1998]

*2020-06-20T20:00:56-03:00*

MAY 24th, 1998. Time: 00h00. Duration: 01m25s. In Goiânia, State of Goiás, five people observed three large round UFOs. According to the witnesses, the unidentified flying objects were blue and luminous, but sometimes changed colors. The three objects flew “very fast” and performed circular and triangular movements, keeping about 800m of distance from each other. The five witnesses claimed that UFOs were making a loud and deep “whistle” like noise. The three objects were seen going in the West direction when performing circular movements.

The COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also informs that:

“1) The observer was very enthusiastic and guaranteed that it was not a spotlight.

2) The APP-AN [Approach Control of Anápolis Air Base-GO] operator reported that in the sector in which the objects were seen, three plots appeared on the terminal’s radar.”
