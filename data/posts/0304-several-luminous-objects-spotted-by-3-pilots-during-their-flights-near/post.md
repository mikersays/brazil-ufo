# “Several luminous objects” spotted by 3 pilots during their flights near Fortaleza-CE [1994]

*2020-07-01T18:59:08-03:00*

Military internal communication report informing the sighting of an unidentified flying object by three different pilots during their flights near Fortaleza [CE].

“Occurrence Report N ° 16/94

From: Chief of C.O.D.A. To: 6th SC

Brasília, September 29th, 1994.

OCCURRENCE SUMMARY REGISTERED IN THE C.O.D.A. SERVICE BOOK 

Service of September 23, 1994

22:08Z-COpM II info PL-LLS reported having observed an unidentified flying object when flying (the PT-LLS) from SBNT [Natal-RN] / SBFZ [Fortaleza-CE]. The object was 45NM from the VOR FZ. It was like several luminous objects between FL 400/500 levels. Then, the PT-LOF that flew from Terezina to SBFZ, when on the SBFZ radial 260, it observed at 64NM what it thought was a large meteorite. Finally, the PT-OFL that flew about 20NM from the PT-LOF (behind it) saw a light trail pass. All of this happened at around 9:48 pm. More precision in the information was not possible due to the lack of knowledge by COpM3 regarding the questionnaire forms. AEDA aware.

JCR -Ten-Cel-AV / Head of CODA.
