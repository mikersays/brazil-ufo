AUG 2nd, 1994. Time: 18:07. Duration: 5 seconds. A commercial pilot from Goiânia-GO, reported seeing a UFO when he was “in charge of the [aircraft] PT-ESA on August 2nd, 1994, at 18:07, on the RD010/145NM [nautical miles] from BRS [Brasília], at bow 190°, FL 080. According to the pilot’s description, “the object resembled an aircraft with a disintegrating nose. It crossed from right to left at descending altitude.” The witness was unable to determine precisely how far he was from the sighted object, but stated that it was higher than his plane. The sighting occurred when PT-ESA was flying over the Minaçu-GO region, on RD010°/145NM BRS.

Still according to the pilot, the UFO, besides being “like an aircraft disintegrating”, it seemed to suffer this ‘incident’ from its “oval tail” on. Regarding the size, the UFO appeared to be “the size of a Boeing’s fuselage”. Its color was “blue-green, becoming reddish-yellow”. Its speed was “compatible with that of a jet plane” and when it passed in the West-East direction it left a trail “similar to an asteroid”.

The pilot also stated that “there were 6 people on board but they were not alerted at the time, as the phenomenon occurred very quickly” and that “at first, he did not think it was a UFO, but an aircraft in disintegration and after [he thought it was] an asteroid “.
