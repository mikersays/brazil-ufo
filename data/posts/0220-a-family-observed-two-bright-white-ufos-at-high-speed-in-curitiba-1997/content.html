MARCH 16th, 1997. Time: 20h20. Duration: 30 minutes. In the Xaxim neighborhood in Curitiba, State of Paraná, a family observed two unidentified flying objects in a round shape with a bright white color, and flying at a high speed “above the clouds”. According to the witnesses, the two UFOs were close to each other and were following a circular trajectory. According to the perspective of the observers, they could not estimate the size, but the impression they had looking at the firmament was that the objects “had” approximately “50cm” in size, that is, even when observed from a great distance, the UFOs stood out between the clouds reported at the time of sighting.
