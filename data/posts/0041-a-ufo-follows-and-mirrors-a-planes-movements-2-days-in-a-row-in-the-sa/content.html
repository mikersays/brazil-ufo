JUN 30th, 2011. At 21:45z. For two days in a row, on the same route, a pilot from Total Linhas Aéreas flight TTL 9930 reported sightings of an unidentified flying object in the stretch between Carauari Airport and Porto Urucu District Airport [in Coari], both cities of the state of Amazonas. The pilot reported having identified the UFO two days with the ACAS [Onboard Anticollision System], always on the same route, and always positioning itself about 7 Nautical Miles in front of the pilot’s aircraft, following it, and copying the movements made of the pilot’s aircraft for about 7 minutes.

The report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] brings the following observations according to the pilot’s testemony:

“It was reported by the pilot of the TTL 9930, whose route was from SWCA [Carauari Airport] to SBUY [Porto Urucu Airport, in Coari], that when his cruise level stabilized, a plote appeared on his 7-inch anti-collision radar equipment  miles in front of his aircraft and accompanied him and that any movement made by his ACFT to unknown ACFT also did the same procedures and shortly after 7 min it disappeared. The pilot also reported that this is not the first time this fact has happened, the fact occurred between 21: 30z DEP time and 21:54 ETA ACFT TTL 9930 registration: PR-TTK The same fact occurred the previous day, 06/29/2011, the target appeared again accusing resolution on the TTL 9930 ACAS equipment in the same route.”
