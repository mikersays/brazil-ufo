# Two yellow dot-like UFOs sighted in Porto Alegre [2000]

*2020-06-15T18:43:53-03:00*

JAN 16th, 2000. Time: 12:15 am. Duration: 20 minutes. In Passo da Areia, a district in the city of Porto Alegre, State of Rio Grande do Sul, under good visibility conditions, two people spotted two unidentified flying objects in the form of “yellow dots” at high altitude. According to reports, the UFOs remained stationary during the twenty minutes they were observed, leaving no traces of condensation. There were also no photographs or videos of the sighting made by the witnesses.
