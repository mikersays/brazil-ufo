AUG 11, 1998. Time: 18h00 pm. Duration: 01h30m. In the Serra region, near the Sítio Amarelinho and Sítio João Dias, in the city of Pilõezinhos, State of Paraíba, an unidentified flying object with white color and cylindrical shape, moved at high speed at about 30 meters in altitude. According to the witnesses’ reports of the sighting, in a certain moment the UFO was only 20 meters away from them. There were rumors of abduction attempt in the nearby communities.

Obs .: * in the report of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], due to the wear conditions of the copy made available in the file of this case, some words from the “Observations” section of the report are unreadable, however, in general, it’s possible to have an idea of some details of the phenomenon which, from what can be read, was not an  isolated event:

“[…] the three observers and […] several other members from places close to the sites reported such apparitions and […] even reporting […]actions of […] capture.”
