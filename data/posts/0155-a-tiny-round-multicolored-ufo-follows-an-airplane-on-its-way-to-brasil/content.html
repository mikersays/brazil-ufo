JULY 29th, 1999. Time: 23h40 pm. Duration: 3 minutes. During a flight from Palmas, State of Tocantins, to Brasília, Distrito Federal – just above the region between the cities of Porto Nacional [Tocantins] and Minaçu [Goiás] – a pilot and a co-pilot spotted a small round multicolored UFO [“red, orange and white”], the size of “an orange” moving away and approaching the side of the aircraft and at the same altitude of 18,000ft. Also according to the observations made in the report to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], one of the witnesses stated that:

“The object appeared on the right side of the aircraft, which was flying from Palmas to Brasília, above FL180 and remained in that position for approximately three minutes; always alternating between the colors red, green and white. The phenomenon was also observed by the second pilot.”
