APRIL 26th, 1994. Time: 20:30. Duration: 10 seconds. For about 10 seconds, a commercial pilot saw, in the Plano Piloto region in Brasília, Federal District, “a very large yellow fireball”. The object was round, “very large” and “fire-colored”. The pilot informed that the unknown object had a “very fast, very sudden” speed of about “500km/h” and that “the object had a tail and when it fell it flashed”. Also according to the witness, the object “was descending sharply” at the moment when “the observer was facing the East, in the North position.”
