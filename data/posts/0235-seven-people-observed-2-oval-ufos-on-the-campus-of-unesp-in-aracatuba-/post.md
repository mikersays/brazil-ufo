# Seven people observed 2 oval UFOs on the campus of UNESP in Araçatuba-SP [1996]

*2020-06-27T21:30:03-03:00*

Interrelated with cases 474 and 452

SEPT 2nd, 1996. Time: 19h15. A group of seven people on the Campus of the University of São Paulo – UNESP, in Araçatuba, São Paulo, observed two unidentified flying objects with oval shapes, moving at a “slow, subtle” speed. According to the witnesses, the UFOs followed a “straight path; over the city; one made [a] ninety degree turn to the Northeast”. The UFOs were sighted at an altitude of approximately 500m, and at about 10km [horizontal] distance from the observers. Although distant, the witnesses saw them as “the size of a soccer ball”. The objects were two amber colored spheres, with a sparkling blue halo and a concentrated red center, similar to the ember”.

The report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also stated that: “during the approach and overflight of the city only one object [was seen]; afterwards, in the Northeast sector, two objects were observed, with a distance between them of 4km. The report was provided in a communication made while the sighting was still happening”.

The sighting was recorded by one of the witnesses  with a video camera and film camera. In addition, binoculars and telescopes were used too. The sky was clear, with good visibility.

The witness who reported the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] is a ufologist and, and he will appear in the case file ARX.0.0.474, reporting a series of sightings in the region of Araçatuba and the surrounding area by his ufology group.
