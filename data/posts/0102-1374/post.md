# A red comet-like UFO spotted over Curitiba [2001]

*2020-06-12T20:40:21-03:00*

APRIL 28th, 2001. Time: 6:40 am. Duration: 30 seconds. In the Champagnhagt neighborhood, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, two witnesses saw an unidentified flying object resembling a red comet, going from West to Southeast. According to the witness, the UFO left long reddish trails of fire. The sighting took place under good visibility and with the naked eye. The report from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also informed that: “The observer was unable to specify the distance but declared that the observation was made for 30 seconds.”
