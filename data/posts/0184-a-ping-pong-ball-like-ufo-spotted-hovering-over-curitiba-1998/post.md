# A ping-pong ball-like UFO spotted hovering over Curitiba [1998]

*2020-06-19T21:48:10-03:00*

JUNE 12th, 1998. Time: 22h04 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. In the Água Verde neighborhood, in Curitiba, State of Paraná, under a clear and cloudless sky, with good visibility, two people observed an unidentified flying object with a round shape and silver and red colors, hovering south of their position. The UFO had the appearance of a “ping-pong ball” and remained stationary at an altitude of approximately 10,000m, making circular moves. There were no reports of condensation trails, nor were photographs of the sighting taken.
