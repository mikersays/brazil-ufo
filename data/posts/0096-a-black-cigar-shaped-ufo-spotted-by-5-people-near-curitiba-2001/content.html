JULY 22nd, 2001. Time: 14h20 pm. Duration: 8 minutes. In the city of São José dos Pinhais, metropolitan region of Curitiba, State of Paraná, five (5) witnesses spotted a black cigar-shaped UFO about 8 meters long. The UFO moved slowly at an altitude of 1,000 meters, and moved towards the Northeast. The photograph record was reported “with a common machine” [film camera?] but the photos were not made available in this file. The visibility conditions were good and there was no record of condensation trails. Also according to the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witnesses stated that the UFO was “close to São José dos Pinhais, more or less on the route of planes that come from São Paulo”.
