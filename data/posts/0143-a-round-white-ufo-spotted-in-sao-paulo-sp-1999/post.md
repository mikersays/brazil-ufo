# A round white UFO spotted in Bela Vista-SP [1999]

*2020-06-15T22:21:51-03:00*

DEC 16th, 1999. Time: 03h50 am. Duration: 20 minutes. Two witnesses in the city of Bela Vista, State of São Paulo, spotted a round white UFO with red and green edges. The objetc was “bigger than a big plane”, and also round in shape but with part of its body in a triangular form. The two observers were using binoculars and saw the UFO about 5 km away from them, in favorable weather conditions for sky observation.
