# A round silver UFO reported between Itajubá and São Lourenço-MG [2007]

*2020-06-07T23:36:16-03:00*

APRIL 18th, 2007. Time: 19h52 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. It was reported between the cities of Itajubá and São Lourenço, State of Minas Gerais, the sighting of an unidentified flying object in a circular shape, silver color, with low movement/almost stationary. The UFO was observed for approximately 15 minutes under good visibility conditions, with no reports of trails or any interference in electricity, telephone sets or any noise heard.
