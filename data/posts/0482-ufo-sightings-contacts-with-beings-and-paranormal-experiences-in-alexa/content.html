‘Vale do Rio do Ouro’ Farm, in Alexânia-GO, owned by Mr. Wilson Plácido Gusmão. Occurrences of UFO sightings, contacts with beings and paranormal experiences reported between the years 1967 and 1969.

The report translated below brings some of these occurrences witnessed by a select group of people of good reputation and credibility  in the societies of Brasília and Goiás at the time, such as high-ranking militaries, a lawyer, an employee of the city hall of Brasília-DF, and the owner of the Farm himself, a civil servant from the State of Goiás. The following excerpt shows the interaction between some of these witnesses and their impressions about the observed and reported facts.

“General Uchôa* always said:” Look, people – this guided fog [?] brings a change of the sins.”/ “The case is: a very interesting hat-like fog appeared on the farm. It comes down, surrounds us and holds us. They trap us even inside it. It has done this several times. After “it” left, we went home and left […]. Soon after, another car arrived with Dr. Tuffi, from the Office of the Mayor of Brasília-DF, with four ladies. Soon after I started to mentalize [?], Having “he” returned and disappeared back in the car. From the hill, an immense mist descended as if it were a wave, lodged in the meadow, and from that mist a tail came out, a kind of jet and it is approaching. The women started to get scared, but I calmed them down. When the tail was about 4 meters from us, do you know what happened? (that’s why, Maj Jacob, I say it was the most important fact) Music and perfume. Soft music and perfume invading our environment. In the meantime the light, a small shadow (there is a shadow similar to a bomb that […] has passed around us several times) moved from the hill at high speed and when it was about 10 meters from us, it lit a beacon (like a searchlight) so strong and over that tail. The tail shrank and entered the fog. The shadow came, deflected and left.

– MAJ JACOB: “They” collect that cloud.

– Mr. Wilson: The great fog took off, returned to the hill and from the hill passed over our heads again with a distance of approximately 3km and took off towards the variant.

– MAJ JACOB: This cloud is something extraordinary.

– Mr. Wilson: On the 20th, MAJ JACOB came and it “did” the same thing. It did not send the tail, but covered the ground. We looked through the binoculars and saw the ground below, on the grass.

– CEL BASTOS FILHO: And did the phenomenon of music and perfume happen again?

– Mr. Wilson: No. It was not repeated, but we have already heard a noise similar to that of a helicopter.

– CEL BASTOS FILHO: In this group, besides you, who are assiduous, has it constantly appeared to other people?

– Mr. Wilson: Yes, it appeared to my employees. On the 1st of March, I went to Alexânia and left Dona Geralda with her children at the farm, and told her before leaving: “if some of my friends from […] lia arrive in a silent device, you ask them to go down, serve them a coffee and tell them that I will be back soon. I arrived in Alexânia and had the intuition that “they” were going to appear and had something to talk about. When I returned, Dona Geralda said: “Mr. Wilson – You were up there on the hill all the time and your friends stayed here. It was a large device and cleared everything. “They” (there were three) opened a door and watched. I left with the lamp in my hand but they didn’t want to go down. They stayed a long time, closed the door and left “.

– CEL BASTOS FILHO: Were you in the farm yard?

– Mr. Wilson: Yes. They stayed there waiting a long time. Then the boys arrived. Chico and Ronaldo and they confirmed everything. On the 26th of March, “he” appeared immediately turning on the blue light and I mentally said “Mr. Commander: I am willing to not go to the meeting any more. There is no way I am going any further. It is not fair. You can come in, you can even taste our food, because I am […] already entitled to demand that”. After a while I felt that my head was getting smaller and I said: “Sir, Commander – you know what – it is very cold and I am going to lie down” and I left. Then I looked out the window, “he” took flight and left. “

* General Alfredo Moacyr de Mendonça Uchôa [1906-1996] was a Brazilian Army military officer, civil engineer, professor and one of the pioneers of Ufology in Brazil.

He wrote 6 books on the topics of Ufology and parapsychology:

  • “Além da parapsicologia – 5.ª e 6.ª dimensões da realidade” (Editora Horizonte, 1968);
  • “A parapsicologia e os discos voadores – O caso Alexânia” (Editora Grupo de Expansão Cultural, 1973);
  • “Cristo para a humanidade de hoje” (Editora Horizonte, 1980);
  • “Mergulho no hiperespaço – Dimensões esotéricas na pesquisa dos discos voadores” (Editora Horizonte, 1981);
  • “Muito além do espaço e do tempo” (Editora Thesaurus, 1983);
  • “Uma busca da verdade – Autobiografia” (Edição do autor, 1995).
