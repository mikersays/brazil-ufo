# In Colombo-PR, two people spotted an oval silver UFO [1999]

*2020-06-18T09:45:52-03:00*

MARCH 18th, 1999. Time: 18h30 pm. Duration: 2 minutes. Two people in Colombo, State of Paraná, spotted an oval silver UFO on a clear night in the vicinity of Clube Santa Mônica. The unidentified flying object flew at low altitude, making circular movements. Also according to the witnesses, the UFO moved at a fast speed going from East to West. There were no photographs or video of the sighting, and there was also no record of condensation trails in the trajectory of the UFO.
