MAY 13th, 2000. Time: 12:52 am. Duration: 20 minutes. A witness living in Bairro Alto in Curitiba, State of Paraná, spotted an unidentified flying object in the shape of a plate and circle, “flying saucer”, in white and yellow colors. According to the witness, the UFO was about 15 meters in size, and was at an altitude between 5 km and 8 km. The unknown object was seen moving from West to East without leaving a trail of condensation.
