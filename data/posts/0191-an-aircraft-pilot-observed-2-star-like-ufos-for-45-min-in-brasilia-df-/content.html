APRIL 12th, 1998. Time: 00:20. Duration: 45 minutes. From his residence in Brasília, Federal District, an aircraft pilot spotted with binoculars, for forty-five minutes, two unidentified flying objects. The UFOs had the appearance of stars, but one of the objects was moving at high speed. The two UFOs were multicolored, with predominance of red, green, blue and yellow. One of the objects was seen in the Southwest and the other one in the West. At the end of the sighting, according to the witness, one of them headed Northwest.

The COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report contains the following additional information: “2 (two) objects were spotted, The first, standing at azimuth 240°. A second initially standing at azimuth 270°, then moving on course 300° until it disappeared. The objects [were] sighted for 45 minutes. Several people present. “
