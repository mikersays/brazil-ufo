# TELEX messages informing UFO sightings by two brazilian ships [1986]

*2020-07-11T21:51:02-03:00*

## TELEX communication informing the sightings of the same UFO by two brazilian ships at high seas.

## “On August 12, 1986, the Brazilian ship RENATA / PPWY, informed having had visual contact with an unidentified flying object in the Northwest position of the ship, position of which is LAT. 18.40S / LONG. 38.50W, sighting that was communicated to VITÓRIA RADIO / PPV by the also Brazilian ship PATRÍCIA RAMOS / PPLT shortly after the RENATA / PPWY report, informing the vessels that the “UFO” was of a circular shape wrapped in bright blue cloudiness, having followed the north course with high speed after remaining in visual field for about ten minutes BT 1 DN COMAR3 SUBEMAER4.

COLOR …. 9L … 1GR … RENATA / PPWY COMMUNICATION – 12: 22: 43Z / ASS + / 612528MAER BR.

—

TU WITH VICTORY RADIO / PPV VG AT 21:50Z DAY 12 AUGUST 86 VG 0 BRAZILIAN SHIP RENATA / PPWY VG FROM ROSTOK / GERMANY WITH DESTINATION TO RIO DE JANEIRO / RJ VG REPORTED HAVING VISUAL CONTACT WITH FLYING OBJECT NOT IDENTIFIED IN THE NORTHWEST POSITION OF THE SHIP VG POSITION OF THE SAME BEING FROM LAT. 1840S / L0NG. 50W VG SIGHTING THAT NEXT COMMUNICATION TO VITORIA RADIO / PPV BY ALSO BRAZILIAN SHIP PATRÍCIA RAMOS / PPLT SOON TO THE RENATA / PPWY PT COMMUNICATION INFORM THE VESSELS THAT THE “UFO” HAD A CIRCULAR SHAPE WITH wrapped in bright blue cloudiness, having gone north at high speed after remaining in visual field for about ten minutes and then went with HIGH-SPEED through the NORTH ROUTE AFTER STAYING IN A VISUAL FIELD FOR ABOUT TEN MINUTES BT 1 DN “COMAR3. 27 //// 13: 36Z – ZWT-26”
