# A round and a star-like red UFOs spotted in Curitiba [1997]

*2020-06-24T17:23:17-03:00*

MARCH 22nd, 1997. Time: 20h34. Duration: 5 minutes. In Curitiba, State of Paraná, a person observed two unidentified flying objects, one with a round shape and the other with a star shape, but both of them were bright red. The two UFOs, according to the witness, were far from where he was when he observed them with binoculars, under a clear sky, emphasizing that it was possible to see them with the naked eye as well. Also according to the report, the witness stated that the UFOs were flying in the East/West direction and were “more or less at the speed of aircraft traffic for landing at SBCT “[Curitiba-PR Airport].
