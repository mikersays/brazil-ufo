# In São Paulo-SP, five people spotted a large round UFO [1992]

*2020-07-06T20:22:06-03:00*

MAY 15th, 1992. Time: 21:45. Duration: 5 minutes. Five people in the region of Moema, city of São Paulo, State of São Paulo, observed an unidentified flying object with a round shape and “larger than a Boeing 747”. According to the witnesses, the UFO was blue, light red and yellow, and was flying at a slower than an airplane, in the West-North direction, and at an altitude of 2,000ft. Observers further stated that they saw the UFO at a distance of approximately 1,500 meters, covering a “horizontal” trajectory. The sighting took place in good visibility and was done with the naked eye.
