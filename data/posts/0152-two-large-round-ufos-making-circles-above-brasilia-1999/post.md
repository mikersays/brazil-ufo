# Two large round UFOs moving in circles above Brasília [1999]**

*2020-06-17T15:35:53-03:00*

Interrelated with case ARX.0.0.556

AUG 26th, 1999. Time: 01h00 am. Duration: 30 seconds. In Brasília, Distrito Federal, two witnesses spotted two large round UFOs emitting dull fluorescent lights. The two unidentified flying objects were large and flew at the altitude “of an airplane”, and “making circles in relation to a point above the TV Tower”. Also according to the witnesses, the UFOs were observed alternately moving “from North to South and from South to North”. The event was observed under a cloudy sky in the region where it took place, with no films made or photographs taken. To the report of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], one of the witnesses also made the following statements: “The two lights that move in circles, sometimes move apart, sometimes come closer. The observer does not believe they are UFOs”.
