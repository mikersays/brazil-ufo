# In Brasília, 7 people spotted 4 UFOs zigzagging and leaving light trails [1999]

*2020-06-18T18:04:29-03:00*

FEB 1st, 1999. Time: 08h00 pm. Duration: 7 minutes. In the Samambaia neighborhood, Brasília, Distrito Federal, seven people observed four (4) UFOs zigzagging and leaving trails of light when moving. The four unidentified flying objects had the shape of “points of light”, in red and orange, and were about 30km away from the observation point, as estimated by one of the witnesses. According to the report given to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the sighting took place under a clear sky and it was recorded with a video camera, however the sighting video was not made available with this file. Other observations in the COMDABRA report inform that the: “The objects evolved without a defined distance between them and disappeared. Then they reappeared.”
