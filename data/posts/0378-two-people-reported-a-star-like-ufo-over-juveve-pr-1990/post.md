# Two people reported a star-like UFO over Juvevê-PR [1990]

*2020-07-08T18:21:35-03:00*

APRIL 24th, 1990. Time: 6:30 pm. Duration: 3 minutes. Two people in the region of Juvevê, Curitiba, State of Paraná, spotted an unidentified flying object “passing over the building” [where the two witnesses were] about 10km high. According to the witnesses, the UFO had the “shape of a star”, but “bigger”, and had the “silver color”. The sighting was carried out with the naked eye and with good visibility conditions.
