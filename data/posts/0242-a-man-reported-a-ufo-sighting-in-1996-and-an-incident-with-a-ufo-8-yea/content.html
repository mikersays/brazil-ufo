JUNE 2nd, 1996. Time: 21h07 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. In the Asa Norte region, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, a person “was in the parking lot inside his car, when he saw a strange object. Soon after, he went to his residence and continued to see the object”. According to the witness, the sky was clear and he saw the UFO with his naked eyes moving towards the airport. The object was shaped like a star, with blue and red colors, and moved at an altitude of approximately 1km from the Southwest position in relation to its residence. The report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also states that: “It is not the first time that [the witness] called to talk about UFO. In 1988 [he] had an accident at his home and received an electrical discharge on his forehead” .
