FEB 28th, 1999. Time: 1h50 am. Duration: not determined. In Vila Ipiranga, Porto Alegre, State of Rio Grande do Sul, two people spotted an unidentified flying object, small in size and shaped like a multicolor star. According to the witnesses, the object flew from South to North alternating movements to the sides, ascents and stops. Observations in the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report further state that: “The object was being observed from the apartment’s balcony […] it was stopped at first, then moved to the left and went up. Its colors alternated in green, red and blue. It was not possible to obtain too much data from the observer, because the call was interrupted and he did not try to make a new contact. “
