# In Brasília, three people spotted a large round white UFO going round in circles [1999]**

*2020-06-17T15:53:50-03:00*

Interrelated with case ARX.0.0.557

AUG 26th, 1999. Time: 2:00 am. Duration: 20 seconds. Between SQN 209 and 208 in the city of Brasilia, Distrito Federal, three witnesses spotted a large round white UFO, at approximately the same flight altitude of the plane in that region. The unknown object moved in circles and flew from North to South. The UFO was sighted under a cloudy sky with visibility considered “good” according to the witness. To COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The observer made herself available for further clarification.”
