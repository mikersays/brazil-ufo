MARCH, 6th, 1999. Time: 10h20 pm. Duration: 10 seconds. In Colina Verde, Londrina, State of Paraná, a person spotted an unidentified flying object heading West. According to the witness, the UFO was the size of an airplane and emitted a yellow light, flying at a slow speed. Despite the sighting taking place under clear skies and good visibility conditions, no traces of condensation were reported from the object, nor video records or photographs of the event that lasted ten seconds.
