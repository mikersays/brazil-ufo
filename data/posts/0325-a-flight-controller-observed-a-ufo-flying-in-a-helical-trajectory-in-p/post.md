# A flight controller observed a UFO flying in a helical trajectory in Porto Alegre-RS [1994]

*2020-07-04T13:20:24-03:00*

MARCH 7th, 1994. Time: 21:20. Duration: 40 minutes. “After reported by two aircraft, and an external call”, a Flight Controller at the Control Tower of Porto Alegre Airport observed with the naked eye an unidentified flying object making a “helical trajectory”. According to the military, the UFO was “a moving light” and he had no way of estimating the object’s altitude, size and distance. The Controller himself stated in the report by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “APP-PA [Controle de Aproximação do Aeroporto de Porto Alegre] did not see anything on the radar screen”.
