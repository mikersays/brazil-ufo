JAN 23rd, 2000. Time: 12:14 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. In the town of Rebouças, Curitiba, State of Paraná, two witnesses spotted an unidentified flying object shaped like a “dot” and colored “orange”. The reported UFO had the appearance of a “star”, alternating movements and stops. According to the witnesses, the sky was partially cloudy and the object, at the end of its appearance, went to the West. For the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] the observer of the phenomenon further stated that: “The light disappeared between the clouds while standing still and then reappeared, moving West and disappeared after a few minutes.”
