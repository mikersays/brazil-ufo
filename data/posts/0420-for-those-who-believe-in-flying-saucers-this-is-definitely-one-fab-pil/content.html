On November 30, 1985, at 10 pm, the Brazilian Air Force FAB 1876 plane near Belo Horizonte, State of Minas Gerais, reported seeing a UFO that he tried to photograph. The unidentified flying object that was not detected by the radar, in the pilot’s description, “was on the BHZ [Belo Horizonte-MG] radial 090” and appeared to be composed of “2 blue lights and one more red light” that carried out vertical and lateral displacements “.

Complementary data:

Right after the VOR PMI blockade (Pará de Minas) light was observed in the sky at 11 Hrs. The aircraft was flown on the bow of VOR BHZ (Belo Horizonte) from SBUR (Uberaba) in good weather conditions (VSB over 10 km and clear night). APP-BH questioned whether an object was seen on radial 170 that was being observed by TWR-BH but not detected by radar (ASR).

Answered affirmatively, it was coordinated with the APP-BH flight in the vicinity of the observed object, aiming at a better determination of its nature, including with the use of a camera. The flight was performed up to an approximate distance of 4NM at an altitude of 6800/7000 Ft.

The object that initially appeared to be unique, consisted of two larger foci of light. The largest had two illuminated sectors, one half-structure and the smallest, as if it were the indirect light from the mouth of a large balloon, at the bottom. There was also a smaller light (strong orange point) that moved vertically and horizontally below and just beside the smaller one (attached sketch). Around the object and below was a cloud bank of the SC (Strato-Cumulus) type.
