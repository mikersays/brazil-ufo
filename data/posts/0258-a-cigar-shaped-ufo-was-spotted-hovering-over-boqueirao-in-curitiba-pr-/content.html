MARCH 25th, 1996. Time: 10h15 am. Duration: 1 minute. A military man in São José dos Pinhais, State of Paraná, along with two other people, spotted an unidentified flying object, shaped like a “cigar” and with the size of a “Boeing 707”. According to the witnesses, the UFO was silver in color and initially remained stationary, “stopped, and then started [a] rapid descent until it disappeared”. The UFO was seen with the naked eye and in a clear night, about 1,000ft above the Barracks of Boqueirão.
