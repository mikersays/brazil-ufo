APRIL 12th, 1998. Time: 9:40 pm. Duration: 60 minutes. On a night of good visibility and clear sky, two people in the Asa Norte region, in Brasília, Federal District, spotted an unidentified flying object with a round shape and white color. The UFO remained stationary during the sixty minutes it was observed and, according to witnesses, the object was positioned “close to the Orion’s Belt” and gave the impression of having the size of “a football” compared to the observable aspect of the stars of the firmament, that is, it was positioned close to an altitude of 10,000 feet. Also according to the witnesses, the UFO “changed the tonality” of its color. There were no records of observed condensation trails.
