MAY 6th, 1991. Time: 23:43.

“Transcript of communications on the occurrence of an unidentified flying object in the region of Belém on May 6th, 1991”

LETTER:

To the Hon. Mr. Commander of NUCOMDABRA,

Subject: Occurrence of air traffic
Ref: Specific Guideline 04/89 DEPV
Annex: Transcript of recording of communications n. 05 / APPBE

I – I forward to you the attached document stating that the ACCBL and APPBE do not have video recordings.
II – I inform you that, in the APPBE Record Book, the following report was made, in the 3rd shift of May 6th, 91:

– “VRG888, SOCA destination, observed an unidentified object at 23:43, 10NM BE, in its 12 o’clock position, when it crossed level 080. The object was not being detected on the radar.
– During the reporting of the VRG888 there was no other aircraft under the control of this APP, which was in that sector, besides the VRG888 itself.
– VRG888 took off at 23:38, and, in addition to this aircraft, we had VSP192- SBBE / SBEG, DEP 23:36, having abandoned TMABE at 23:41, and BLC210, SBTU / SBBE, Lulza at 23:36 and Jara at 23:51.
– METAR SEBE 23:00 00000 CAVOK 25/24 10121 ‘
HEAD OF SRPV-BE

Transcript: 

23:39:28Z – VRG888 – Control Belém, very good night, it is the VARIG eight eight eight. Taken at three eight, turning left to intercept at three four seven.

APPBE – Ascension in route, under radar surveillance, eight eight eight 

VRG888 – Eight eight eight.

23:40:50Z – VRG888 – Hey, Belem, VARIG eight eight eight 

APPBE – We”re listening, proceed.

VRG888 – I have traffic in the twelve o’clock position for … from eleven to twelve o’clock, for VARIG eight eight eight?

APPBE – Negative, VARIG eight eight eight

VRG888 – I have eleven o’clock traffic! Okay, is it already stabilized in the three four seven, crossing the seventy to two eight zero, ten miles?

23:41:29Z – APPBE – Hey, VARIG eight eight eight, we are not resonating the return of any object in the sut, position twelve o’clock. Could you confirm the altitude of this traffic?

VRG888 Yeah … it must be ninety, now ninety to one hundred, okapa? In my position, now, eleven o’clock?

APPBE – Affirmative, aware? I inform you that we do not have … uhr … aware of this traffic, eight eight eight.

VRG888 – Aware.

APPBE – I appreciate the information.

23:41:59z – VRG888 – Okay.
