# Two fast silver ball-like UFOs spotted near a plane in Curitiba [1997]

*2020-06-23T00:26:40-03:00*

AUG 29th, 1997. Time: 3h30pm. Duration: 30 minutes. In the Pinheirinho region, Curitiba, State of Paraná, six people observed two small unidentified flying objects, similar to silver “shiny balls”. The sighting report was communicated while the event was taking place. According to the witnesses, the two UFOs were moving at a “speed 3 times the speed of a plane that is nearby”, all towards the South. The witnesses, who were in a car, reported that in a certain moment, “the two UFOs stopped and one of them started following the plane”. The sighting was observed under good visibility conditions and a “clear, cloudless sky”, according to the report.
