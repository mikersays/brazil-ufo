# A man reported UFO sightings two days in a row in Ceilândia-DF [1994]

*2020-07-01T18:39:36-03:00*

OCT 11th, 1994 and OCT 11th, 1994. A man reported two sightings of a static UFO during two days in a row in the Ceilândia region, Distrito Federal. The witness reported that the unidentified flying object had no definite shape, but was “larger than Venus when its brightness is intense”. He also stated that the object had a color that varied between green and red, and that he was unable to estimate the altitude at which the object remained during the two sightings. He and his wife spotted the unknown object in clear skies and with the naked eye.
