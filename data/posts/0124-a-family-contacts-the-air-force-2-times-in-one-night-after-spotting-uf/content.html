* Case related to Case ARX.0.0.586, reported by the same family about 1 hour before this other communication registered in this file ARX.0.0.585 *

NOV 5th, 2000. Time: 01h05 am. For the second time in one night, members of the same family – in Juvevê, a district of Curitiba, State of Paraná – called the Air Force to report the sighting of two (2) unidentified flying objects zigzagging very close to the buildings in front of their apartment. The UFOs, as described in the previous report [ARX.0.0.586] were shaped as luminous dots and yellowish white in color and this time one of them was emmiting sparks. The UFOs flew at low speed and at very low altitude, “below buildings”. The objects performed erratic movements and zigzags.

In the observations of this second report to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the lady “reported almost the same data as in the previous report, except for some details, such as, for example, that one of the objects emitted sparks. Later, Mrs. [. ..] called again saying that Aeronautics were not giving any attention to these cases and that tomorrow it would contact the local newspaper “Gazeta do Povo” to report her history. “
