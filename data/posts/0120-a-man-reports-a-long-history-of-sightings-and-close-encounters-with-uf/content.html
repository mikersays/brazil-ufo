NOV 28th, 2000. Time: 17h40pm. In the city of Igarapava, State of São Paulo, a witness (who asked for discretion) made a brief report to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] about his experience with UFO phenomena during eight (8) years. According to the witness, in this period he not only spotted unidentified flying objects, but also claimed to have had “close encounters” during the phenomena. The features of the UFOs described by this witness in the COMDABRA’s report depicted the UFOs in “various ways”, but specifying some shapes like the “cigar” shape and also the “spoon” shape UFOs. The witness also claimed to have filmed and photographed the UFO phenomena, but these files were not made available with this declassified report.

Additional observations in the COMDABRA’s report on this case: “He reported that there was a long history, approximately eight years, several sightings and forms of contact. He has photos and film. He has already contacted some “researchers” and “organs of the genre” and not found the necessary receptivity or sincerity or answers. Obs .: He made it clear he does not want a fuss or his name involved in public. “
