According to a report given to COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], on August the 17th, 2015 at 11:20pm, three people saw a round flashing white object right above Manaus’ Air Base, moving slowly at approximately 700 miles of altitude. The witnesses, as reported, had meteorological and aviation knowledge. It was a clear sky night and they requested a rescue vehicle from the Air Force Base to check the object, but as soon as the vehicle was ready, the light started to turn off slowly. The object made no noise and left no marks or trails in the sky.
