JUN 28th, 1994. Time: 15:30. Duration: 90 seconds. When he was close to the BR-116 highway, in the Pinheirinho region, Curitiba, State of Paraná, a man spotted two unidentified flying objects in the shape of balls; “two balls, one below, one above”. According to the witness, the objects were “half a meter each”, they were light in color and remained stationary, very close to each other, “glued”. The sighting was carried out with the naked eye and with good visibility conditions.
