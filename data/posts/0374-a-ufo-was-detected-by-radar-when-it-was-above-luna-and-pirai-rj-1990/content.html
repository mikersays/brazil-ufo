JULY 21st, 1990. Time: 01:21. Duration: 5 minutes. A flight controller at Rio de Janeiro-RJ Airport, reported a radar detection of an unidentified flying object “crossing the LS locator between Luna and Piraí”, State of Rio de Janeiro. Three people followed the UFO’s radar detection as it moved between 150kt and 170 kt [277km / h and 314 km / h] and 60 NM [nautical miles] from Rio de Janeiro Galeão Airport. Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The RJ control later informed that several plots have appeared on the radar due to technical problems”.
