- 04. DESCRIPTION: Luminous body

- 05. COLOR: Reddish yellow

- 06. FORM: Undefined

- 07. APPARENT SIZE: 5 cm

- 08. MOVEMENT straight path, Southeast / Northwest

- 09. SPEED: Average (-500km / h)

- 10. ALTITUDE: 1,500 m (estimated)

- 11. DISTANCE: 1,000 m (estimated)

- 12. OBSERVER (S): 1S C AT MT FLÁVIO and Civilians UBIRATAN PINON and Mrs. F.M. FREITAS and SRA. CELIA MONTENEGRO

- 13. NOTE: The UFO started to emit bluish flashes when quite far (8,000m) from the observation point. Continuous accelerated movement.

- 01. REG. n. 083

- 02. PLACE: COLARES-PA

- 03. DATE / TIME: 23 Jan 78 – 23:30 hours

- 04. DESCRIPTION: UFO (landed)

- 05. COLOR: Dark reddish on the top and blue-green reflections on the bottom

- 06. SHAPE: Rounded (circular), “short-brimmed hat”, with 2 feet.

- 07. APPARENT SIZE: Estimated, depending on the distance, between 2 m and 3 m

- 08. MOVEMENT: Stationary. He moved, gaining height at great speed.

- 09. SPEED: Supersonic (when moving)

- 10. ALTITUDE: On the ground

- 11. DISTANCE: 400m (estimated)

- 12. OBSERVER (S): Mr MANOEL JOÃO DE OLIVEIRA FILHO

- 13. NOTE: The rapporteur observed the UFO landed, for a period of 10 (ten) minutes, He tried to call his companions, when returning the CL [luminous body] was already moving at a high speed upwards, in the Northwest direction.
