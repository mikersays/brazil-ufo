# A round UFO hovered and then went off at high speed in Curitiba-PR [1996]

*2020-06-28T22:09:51-03:00*

INTERRELATED with cases 430 and 432

MARCH 26th, 1996. Time: 21h00. Duration: 30 minutes. Eight people in the city of Curitiba, State of Paraná, spotted an unidentified flying object, round in shape, with green and red colors. According to the witnesses, the UFO remained stationary for a while and then moved with “fast speed” in a “straight line”. The observer was unable to estimate the altitude of the unknown object, but stated that it was “very high” and “far”. All the sighting was performed with the naked eye and good weather.
