APRIL 24th, 1991. Time: 20:00. Duration: 50 minutes. In São Roque region, in São Paulo-SP, a military in duty at the DTCEA-SRO, São Roque Airspace Control Department, reported an unrecognized “target” on the radar, an unidentified flying object. According to the report, the UFO was “7NM from the position of the Zone Axis of the G-6 78”, and it was moving at low speed, “almost stopped”. The detection was also witnessed by 6 more people at the Airspace Control in São Roque. According to the witness, there was “recording video coverage [at] São Roque”. Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “[The] plot was spotted on synthetic video radar, speed Mach 1 to Mach 3, radial 193 / 47NM from the São Roque antenna [with] intermittent appearance. It was asked the RG169B aircraft if any traffic was seen, the  aircraft did not report visual with any traffic. “
