# A multicolor UFO was spotted zigzagging between Luziânia and Ipameri-GO [1999]

*2020-06-18T18:18:06-03:00*

JAN 23rd, 1999. Time: 10h40 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. Between the cities of Luziânia and Ipameri, State of Goiás, an unidentified flying object with the appearance of bright red, blue and green lights, and the size of an airplane, was spotted by two people. According to the witnesses, the UFO was observed zigzagging in the Southwest direction at an altitude of approximately 10km and flying at 500km / h.
