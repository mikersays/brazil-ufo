FEB 3rd, 2003. Time: 22h00pm. Duration: 25 minutes. Three people of the same family in the city of São José dos Pinhais, near Curitiba, State of Paraná, spotted 6 (six) unidentified flying objects with a circular shape and white color. The UFOs, according to the witnesses, were “5 meters in diameter”, were going “very fast” at about 2,000 meters high. Also according to the witnesses, the UFOs maintained a certain distance between themselves and they were approximately 12 km from the observers, in the direction of Serra do Mar, moving in a South / North direction. The visibility conditions were good.
