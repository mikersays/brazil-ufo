# An oval white UFO spotted flying at 200m in Curitiba-PR [1992]

*2020-07-06T17:43:53-03:00*

AUG 4th, 1992. Time: 18:15. Duration: 2 hours. A person in Curitiba, State of Paraná, reported the sighting of two UFOs making circular movements, approaching and moving away from each other. According to the witness, the unknown objects were oval, “sparkling white” and “extremely fast”. The observer reported that they positioned themselves approximately 200 meters high, at “Alto da XV, over Rádio Atalaia”, being observed horizontally by him from about 1 km. The witness stated that he made the sighting with the naked eye and good visibility conditions.
