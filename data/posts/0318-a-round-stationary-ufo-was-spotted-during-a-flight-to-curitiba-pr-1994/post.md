# A round stationary UFO was spotted during a flight to Curitiba-PR [1994]

*2020-07-03T20:53:46-03:00*

MAY 2nd, 1994. Time: 13:26. Duration: 7 minutes. A pilot and a co-pilot reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object during a “night flight to Curitiba [SBBI – Bacacheri Airport] at 68NM bow 250 FL240 [24,000 feet]”. According to the witnesses, the UFO remained stationary for all of the time. It was round, but “varied to the shape of a star”. The object was also white, blue and red. The co-pilot stated that the UFO positioned itself “northwest of the aircraft”, and that he was not able to estimate the distance between the object and the plane. The sighting of the pilots was done with the naked eye and with good visibility conditions.
