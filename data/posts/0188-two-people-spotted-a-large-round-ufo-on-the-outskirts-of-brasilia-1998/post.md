# Two people spotted a large round UFO on the outskirts of Brasilia [1998]

*2020-06-20T20:25:39-03:00*

MAY 4th, 1998. Time: 22h40. Duration: 15 minutes. In a ranch 4 km from Brasilia, Distrito Federal, two people observed an unidentified flying object, “the size of a Boeing 737”, with a round shape and blue color. The large UFO was flying slow and, according to the witnesses, at an altitude of about 1,000 meters. The unknown object was 5km away from them and they noticed that the object, when moving, left clear trails of light, similar to a “tail”. The sighting took place in good conditions of visibility and under a clear sky, according to the observers’ report. No photographs of the event were taken.
