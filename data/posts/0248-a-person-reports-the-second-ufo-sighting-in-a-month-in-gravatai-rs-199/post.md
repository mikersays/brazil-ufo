# A person reports the second UFO sighting in a month in Gravataí-RS [1996]

*2020-06-28T13:19:21-03:00*

APRIL 30th, 1996. Time: 23h05. Duration: 3 to 5 minutes. A person in the region of Gravataí, State of Rio Grande do Sul, reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object with the appearance “bigger than a star” in the sky and blue in color. According to the witness, “the object remained in the sky” for 3 to 5  minutes. The witness stated that he observed the same UFO a month earlier as well. The sighting took place in clear skies and with the naked eye.
