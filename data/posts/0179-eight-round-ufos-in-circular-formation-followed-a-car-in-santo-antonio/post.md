# Eight round UFOs followed a car  in Santo Antonio de Lisboa-SC [1998]

*2020-06-19T18:11:38-03:00*

SEPT 5th, 1998. Time: 9:50 pm. Duration: 1 hour. In Santo Antônio de Lisboa, a city in the state of Santa Catarina, four people reported a close encounter with eight (8) white spheres that followed their car flying in “circular formation”. The 8 UFOs had three feet in diameter each, and were only 50 meters away from the four witnesses. They managed to take photographs but the photos were not made available with this file.
