FEB 20th, 2007. Time: 00h13am. Duration: approximately 2 minutes. A witness was in the Copacabana neighborhood, in the south zone of the city of Rio de Janeiro, RJ, on a clear night, when he spotted two unidentified flying objects moving near the sea, towards the neighborhoods of Botafogo and Flamengo. The UFOs were two big flying saucers, and one of them was slightly larger than the other. They emitted colors that varied between red, green and white, and were making oblique trajectories and rising slowly to an altitude that the witness was unable to inform.
