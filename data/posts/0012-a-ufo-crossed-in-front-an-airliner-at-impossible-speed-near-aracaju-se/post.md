# A UFO crossed in front of an airliner at impossible speed near Aracaju-SE [2015]

*2020-05-30T20:40:24-03:00*

FEB 2nd, 2015. From 04h57am, for about 10 minutes, the pilot of an aircraft in a flight 55 nautical miles distant from Aracaju, State of Sergipe, reported seeing an unidentified object, flying very fast, and crossing ahead of his aircraft from right to left at about 2,000 feet of altitude. Regarding the form and nature of what may have been the object sighted, the witness’ testimony to COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] also informed: “Bright lights, speed incompatible with known aircraft. I couldn’t identify whether it would be a star or a satellite. It was sighted for 10 minutes “.
