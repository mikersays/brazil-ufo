DEC 16th, 2002. Time: 1:35 am. Duration: 25 minutes. A Brazilian Army Reserve Officer was in the neighborhood of Navegantes, city of Porto Alegre, State of Rio Grande do Sul, when he sighted 7 (seven) unidentified flying objects with the shape of small white “dots”. The 7 UFOs were just 600 meters from the witness and they were traveling at about 100km/h and at an altitude of approximately 300m. The objects flew in the Southwest/Northeast direction. The sighting was carried out in good visibility conditions and, according to the witness, there was no record of trails or electrical/electronic anomalies during the event.
