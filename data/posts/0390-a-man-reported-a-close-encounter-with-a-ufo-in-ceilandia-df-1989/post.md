# A man reported a close encounter with a UFO in Ceilândia-DF [1989]

*2020-07-10T12:03:54-03:00*

Ceilândia, Distrito Federal. Date: March 27th, 1989. Time: 10:30 pm. Duration: 5 minutes. A Security Agent of the Brasilia Airport spotted an unidentified flying object in the shape of a “full moon, without emitting strong lights”. According to the witness, the UFO was small, orange in color and remained stationary between 30 and 50 meters above the observer. Also according to the witness’s report, “the object made a 90° turn, however without moving much horizontally”. The sighting was carried out in a clear sky.
