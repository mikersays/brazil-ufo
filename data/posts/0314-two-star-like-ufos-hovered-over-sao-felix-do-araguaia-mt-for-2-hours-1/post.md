# Two star-like UFOs hovered over São Félix do Araguaia-MT for 2 hours [1994]

*2020-07-03T17:21:59-03:00*

JUNE 18th, 1994. Time: 18:00. Duration: 2 hours. In the São Félix do Araguaia region, State of Mato Grosso, two people observed two unidentified flying objects remain stationary for two hours. According to the witnesses, the UFOs were star-shaped, blue in color and were flying close to each other. The witnesses couldn’t estimate the altitude of the objects. The sighting was carried out with the naked eye and with a clear sky. There were no reports of photographs or filming of the event.
