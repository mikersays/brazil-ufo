# Five round blinking red UFOs spotted moving in circles in Londrina [2000]

*2020-06-14T18:03:08-03:00*

OCT 22nd, 2000. Time: 16h15pm. Duration: 3 seconds. Five (5) unidentified flying objects were spotted moving in circles at a very high altitude downtown Londrina, State of Paraná. The five round blinking red UFOs had the aspect of a star and were very close to each other in their trajectory, going from East to West at varying speed. Three witnesses who observed the sighting live in the city center and saw the unknown objects with the naked eye and under good visibility conditions.
