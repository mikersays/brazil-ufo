# Several people saw a UFO moving like a windshield wiper in Senador Firmino [2003]

*2020-06-11T12:00:26-03:00*

MAY 23rd, 2003. Time: 8:00 pm. Duration: 15 minutes. In the city of Senador Firmino, State of Minas Gerais, several people witnessed the sighting of an unidentified flying object “in the sky, making movement like a windshield wiper”, according to the words of the witness who provided information to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command ]. The UFO was round in shape and emitted a strong glow. Still according to the report, the visibility conditions at the time were “optimal”, “clear skies”.
