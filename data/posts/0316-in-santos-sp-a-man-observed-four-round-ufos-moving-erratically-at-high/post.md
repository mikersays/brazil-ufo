# In Santos-SP, a man observed four round UFOs moving erratically at high speed [1994]

*2020-07-03T17:52:18-03:00*

MAY 16, 1994. Time: 01:10 am. Duration: 30 minutes. In the city of Santos, State of São Paulo, a man observed, for thirty minutes, four unidentified flying objects “crossing each other’s path at high speed”. According to the witness, the UFOs were above the city center at an altitude that he could not estimate. The UFOs were spherical, yellow, and had a “medium size”, flying at a “very fast” speed. The sighting was performed with the naked eye and under good visibility conditions.
