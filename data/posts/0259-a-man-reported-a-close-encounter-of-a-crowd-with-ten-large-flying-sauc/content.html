MARCH 23rd, 1996. Time: 00h50 am. Duration: not determined. In São Leopoldo, State of Rio Grande do Sul, several people saw 10 unidentified flying objects “in the form of disks”, large in size and white. According to the witnesses, the UFOs moved close to each other and were only 10 meters high from the witnesses, emitting “a noise”. The sighting occurred with the naked eye and in good weather. There was information about photographs taken of the event, however, the witness said he did not know who took them.
