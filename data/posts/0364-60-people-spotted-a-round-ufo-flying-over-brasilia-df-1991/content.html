APRIL 13th, 1991. Time: 22:40. Duration: 30 seconds. A military officer on duty, along with 60 people, reported a UFO sighting near the Papuda Penitentiary Complex in Brasilia, Distrito Federal. The witnesses spotted an unidentified round flying object with one meter in diameter, white and yellow colors, and flying in a straight path at a height of 300 meters. According to the observers, the UFO was traveling on an East-West trajectory about 2km away from them [horizontally].

Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “The object appeared and disappeared quickly. It was seen in the direction Unaí – Brasília near Papuda where the observer was on duty.”
