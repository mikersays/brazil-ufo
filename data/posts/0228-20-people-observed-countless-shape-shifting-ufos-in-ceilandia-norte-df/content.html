Interrrelated with Case ARX.0.0.470

DEC 7th, 1996. Time: 8:20 pm. Duration: 20 minutes. In Ceilândia Norte, Distrito Federal, about 20 people observed “lights changing from diamond to circle” in the skies of their neighborhood. The sighting of the several UFOs was done with the naked eye and a sky with several clouds. The shape-shifting UFOs, according to the witnesses, were approximately 5 meters long. They were white and flew at a speed of approximately 180km/h. The witnesses further claimed that the unknown objects were at an altitude of approximately 10,000 meters and were in a northern direction in relation to the observers of the event.
