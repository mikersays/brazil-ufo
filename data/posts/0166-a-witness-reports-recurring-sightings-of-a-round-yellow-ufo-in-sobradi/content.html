MARCH 10th, 1999. Time: 19h00. Duration: 1 hour. Three people observed a round yellow UFO flying slowly in Sobradinho, Distrito Federal. It was a clear sky night when the three witnesses spotted the UFO at great altitude moving towards Radial RDL 040 BRS [Brasília] in slow motion, almost stopping. The witness who reported the sighting to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], also stated that she has “observed this object four times in the past 30 days.”
