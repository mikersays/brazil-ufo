APRIL 1st, 2004. Time: 6:50 am. Duration: 01 hour.

For an hour, two witnesses on duty at the Control Tower at Goiânia Airport, State of Goiás, spotted two bright and spherical unidentified flying objects, with the size of four Boeing 747 planes. The two UFOs were positioned one in front of the other, following a linear trajectory, about 28NM [nautical miles] away from the witnesses. Following is the description of the COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] representative’s remarks, with text partially unreadable due to the wear of the document provided:

“Flight TAM3591 (A320) from SBGO [Santa Genoveva Airport, in Goiânia, Goiás] was requested to SBBP [Bragança Paulista Airport, São Paulo] that […] at 10.03UTC to inform any observation […] in RDL 185 the aircraft was unable to see anything, when the 145NM in Goiânia […] returned to the control frequency and reported that the object was in the form of […] very large floats […] above the FL350 [35.000ft]. At […] h35UTC I received […] informing that I was seeing the TA (UFO) in the Vertical of Caldas Novas [Goiás]. “

Cf. ARQUIVO NACIONAL (Brasil). Sistema de Informações do Arquivo Nacional (SIAN). Fundo ‘Objeto Voador Não Identificado (OVNI)’.
