JULY 19th, 2009. Time: 18h50. Duration: 90 minutes. In Lagoa de Carcará, City of Nísia Floresta, State of Rio Grande do Norte, a witness observed for 90 minutes two unidentified flying objects with blue and red color, and star-like appearance. The two objects were sighted East of Lagoa do Carcará and heading South. The UFOs moved at variable speeds in a zigzag motion and at high altitude. A power failure was reported by the witness as a parallel phenomenon caused by the UFOs during the sighting. The additional observations in the COMDABRA’s report [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] further state that UFOs “were far apart, [and they] could be mistaken for stars”.
