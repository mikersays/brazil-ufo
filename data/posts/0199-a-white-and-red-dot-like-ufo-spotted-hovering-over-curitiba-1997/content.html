Interrelated case with ARX.0.0.505

DEC 26th, 1997. Time: 05h30 pm. Duration: 03m40s. In Bairro Batel in Curitiba, Paraná State, a person with binoculars observed an unidentified flying object, shaped like a white and red dot, for more than three minutes. According to the witness, the sky was clear and the visibility was good when he noticed that a small and clear UFO was stationary above the Northwest region of Curitiba for as long as it was observed.
