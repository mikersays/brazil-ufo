NOV 26th, 1994. Time: 00:40 am. Duration: less than one minute. Six people observed an unidentified flying object near the landing strip at Belém Airport, in the State of Pará. According to witnesses, the UFO was “delta” shaped, small and its colors “varied between green and orange”. Still according to the report, the speed of the unknown object was “superior to the speed of a Boeing 737” and the UFO was seen flying from East to West. The sighting was done with the naked eye and with good visibility, not being filmed or photographed.
