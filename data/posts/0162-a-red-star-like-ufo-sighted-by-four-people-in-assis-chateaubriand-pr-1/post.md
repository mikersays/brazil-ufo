# A red star like UFO sighted by four people in Assis Chateaubriand-PR [1999]

*2020-06-17T23:32:01-03:00*

APRIL 21st, 1999. Time: 20h00 pm. Duration: 01h30m. In the city of Assis Chateaubriand, State of Paraná, on a night of clear sky and good visibility, four people spotted an unidentified flying object moving from the South to the North of the region. The UFO had a star shape and emitted an intermittent red light. Despite the good visibility condition for the sighting, the witnesses did not report seeing any condensation trail left by the observed object.
