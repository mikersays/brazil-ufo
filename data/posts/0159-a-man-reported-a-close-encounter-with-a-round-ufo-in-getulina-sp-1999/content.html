JUNE 10th, 1999. Time: 08h00 pm. Duration: 30 minutes. In the city of Getulina, State of São Paulo, a man observed an unidentified flying object hovering just 10 meters away from him. The UFO was round, white and had 3 meters of diameter. According to the witness, the UFO was stationary, and it was just three feet from the ground.

“He reported that the object was three feet high and that it had three arms that seemed to want to take something. The next day, he returned to the site and found that the vegetation was burnt.”
