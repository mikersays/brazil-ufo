August 3, 1986. Time: 6:30 pm. Duration: 40 minutes. A group of eight people “when in a farm in the city of Vinhedo [State of São Paulo] observing the sky, noticed the presence of strange objects that moved with an amazing speed” towards Campinas-SP. According to the witness, there were 6 UFOs, with one of the objects “having the shape of a large luminous star, which moved in the aforementioned direction, employing a high speed, varying from white to orange; and sometimes moving alone, sometimes moving grouped with two or more objects”. The witness further stated that when he called CINDACTA, he was still seeing the objects “.
