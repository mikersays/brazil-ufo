JULY 29th, 2015 – 14h25. While flying over the southern region of the State of Minas Gerais, about to enter the airspace of the State of São Paulo, a fighter jet pilot from the Brazilian Air Force spotted an unidentified flying object for about a minute. The occurrence was reported to COMDABRA’s representative [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] in the following terms: “the presence of a long tubular black object at approximately 33,000 feet of altitude, positioned to the left and close to the path of our descent (.. .) “. The report also informs that the object left no marks or trails in the sky, and does not inform the presumable distance between the military aircraft and the probable object.
