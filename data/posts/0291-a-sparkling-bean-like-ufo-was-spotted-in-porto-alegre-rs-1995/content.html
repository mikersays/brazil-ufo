MARCH 20th, 1995. Time: 18h15. Duration: 30 seconds. When walking on a cottage in the region of Porto Alegre, State of Rio Grande do Sul, three people spotted “a sparkling, half-rounded, bean-like object” of white color flying at high speed. According to witnesses, the UFO was flying on a straight trajectory towards the south at high altitude, and was observed with the naked eye in a clear sky of a sunny afternoon.
