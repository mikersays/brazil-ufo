JAN 7th, 2001. Time: 6:30 pm. Duration: 5 to 10 minutes. Under good visibility conditions, an unidentified flying object was sighted over the city of Iguarapava, State of São Paulo, by two witnesses. They reported a round orange UFO that was “bigger than an airplane” and it was approximately 20km from the observation point, going from North to Southwest.
