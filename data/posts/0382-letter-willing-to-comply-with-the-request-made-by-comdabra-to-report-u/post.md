# Letter willing to comply with the request, made by Comdabra, to report UFOs in the brazilian airspace [1990]

*2020-07-08T19:29:26-03:00*

FEB 14th, 1990. 

From: VARIG – Rio de Janeiro, February 14, 1990.

To: Brigadier […]

Commander of NUCOMDABRA

Ministry of Aeronautics

Brasilia DF

Dear sir:

We acknowledge receipt of your Official Letter 097 / CMD0 / C-201, dated December 26, 1989, which we are grateful for your attention.

We inform that we will attend to your request, when there is any report on Unidentified Flying Objects (UFOs), which may be observed in our airspace, by company airmen.

No more for the moment.

Kind regards,

[…] Director of Operations
