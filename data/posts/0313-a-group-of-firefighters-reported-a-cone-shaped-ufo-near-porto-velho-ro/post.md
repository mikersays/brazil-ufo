# A group of firefighters reported a cone shaped UFO near Porto Velho-RO [1994]

*2020-07-03T16:30:45-03:00*

JUN 19th, 1994. Time: 23:55. Duration: not determined. A group of firefighters in the region of Porto Velho, State of Rondônia, observed several appearances of an unidentified flying object performing a “curvilinear, anti-clockwise” trajectory, and flying slowly towards the RD076 highway, near Porto Velho. According to the firefighters who reported the sighting, the UFO “looked like a cone (oval)”, it was big and red. The object was between FL090 [9.000ft] and FL150 [15.000ft], and between RDL 010 and RDL 085. The witnesses stated that “each apparition lasted an average of 5 minutes”. Firstly they observed the UFO with the naked eye, and then with binoculars. The report collected by COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], also states that: “The moment he reported the occurrence, the object reappeared, remaining that way during the report of that military man”.
