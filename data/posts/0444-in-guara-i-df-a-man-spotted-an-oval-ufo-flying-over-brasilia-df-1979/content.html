Brasília, November 21st, 1979. Time: 5:58 am. Duration: 15 seconds. A Post Office worker, living in Guará I, Distrito Federal, reported “the passage of an unidentified object” of oval shape and “silver color, as if it were made of stainless steel”. According to the witness, the UFO “did not emit light because its color was quite defined by the blue contrast of the sky”. Also according to the description, the UFO made no noise and was seen on a trajectory from Guará I until the Brasília Airport.
