MARCH 1st, 1998. Time: 7h30 am. Duration: 1 hour. Seven people in the Tarumã region, Curitiba, State of Paraná, observed for one hour an unidentified flying object with a “round shape and two lateral lumps”. According to one of the witnesses who reported to COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the UFO was zigzagging and it was about the size of a Boeing 737, with blue, green and yellow colors. The same person who contacted the Air Force also reported that the: “Object stopped for about an hour; at one point two lights were shaped like a triangle with a great flash. There was alternation of colors; and its movements were fast in zigzag. “
