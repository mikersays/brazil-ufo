# In Brasília-DF, a woman reported continuous UFO sightings in 3 months [1991]

*2020-07-08T10:23:40-03:00*

APRIL 19th, 1991. Time: 01:35. Duration: not determined. A woman in the Asa Sul region, in Brasília, Distrito Federal, reported continuous sightings of UFOs that occurred for about 3 months. According to the witness, the last sighting, reported in this file, took place “approximately 16km from the end of Asa Sul”, in the “West position”. The three UFOs were large, flashing red and blue lights alternately, and remained stationary most of the time observed. Complementary data from COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command]: “02:30P: a new R/173 call was made and the same [reporting witness] informed that the object of the center suddenly disappeared”. The witness stated that he was with 2 more people in this last sighting, made with the naked eye and under good visibility conditions.
