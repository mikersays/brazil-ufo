SEPT 1st, 2002. Time: 6:30 pm. Duration: 45 minutes. A witness residing in Curitiba, State of Paraná, spotted two large, unidentified flying objects in the form of silver and white globes, that most of the time remained static at an altitude of about 2,000ft. The two UFOs had a “bigger than star” observation aspect and when they moved in the west direction, they did it slowly in circular movements. Also according to the witness, the UFOs had a clear halo when they were in motion.

In the report of COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command], the witness made more observations regarding the sighting:

“Approximately 5 or 6 days ago, the same object was observed at approximately the same location, with behavior similar to the observation reported today, but heading south after some time parked, disappearing between clouds. The object disappeared during the communication of this report.”
