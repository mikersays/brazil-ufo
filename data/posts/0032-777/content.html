JUL 15th, 2012. Time: 19h40m. Clear sky. Near the Municipal Airport of Santa Maria, Rio Grande do Sul. Duration: three minutes. A pilot of TAM airline company reported the sighting of an unidentified flying object, the size of a jet, 5NM in front of his aircraft. The UFO was moving in the opposite direction, with flashing red lights, at an altitude of approximately 20,000ft and heading Southwest. The COMDABRA [Brazilian Aerospace Defense Command] report contains additional observations from the pilot, according to which, “the sighting appeared to be of an experimental aircraft, for military use”, also informing that the pilot “does not believe in UFO studies”.
